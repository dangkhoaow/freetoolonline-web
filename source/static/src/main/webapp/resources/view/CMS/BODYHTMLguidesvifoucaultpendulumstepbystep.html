Open the explorer and watch the swing plane turn as the Earth rotates.

  1. Open /space-3d/foucault-pendulum.html and wait for the swinging pendulum.
  2. Watch the orange floor track slowly sweep around as the plane turns.
  3. Slide the latitude toward 90 to make it turn fast, like at a pole.
  4. Slide it to 0 (the equator) and see the turn stop completely.
  5. Read the facts panel for the full-turn time at your chosen latitude.
