How the Acrocanthosaurus size-vs-human toggle stays honest

What a person would see beside a Acrocanthosaurus

What a person would see is a large theropod whose panel length is 11.5 m beside a 1.8 m person with a low dorsal ridge visible along the back. Turn on Size vs human and orbit.


What the Acrocanthosaurus size numbers are not

What these size numbers are not is a tall Spinosaurus-style sail. Soft-tissue color is still an artistic reconstruction. This model does not claim coexisted with Spinosaurus or Giganotosaurus.
