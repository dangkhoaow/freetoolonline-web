Comet Orbit 3D Explorer Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Comet Orbit 3D Explorer khoi dong ngay khi trang tai xong: keo de quay quanh Mat Troi va quy dao, cuon de phong to, va nhan Play orbit. Moi thu duoc dung tren chinh thiet bi cua ban - khong cai dat, khong tai khoan.


Buoc 1 - tim hinh elip

Tim quy dao lech tam voi Mat Troi gan mot tieu diem. Bang thong tin liet ke P khoang 76 yr, q 0.586 AU, Q 35.1 AU va e 0.967.


Buoc 2 - keo diem can nhat

Keo Orbit progress ve diem can nhat de cac duoi dai ra; ca hai hinh non deu huong nguoc Mat Troi.


Buoc 3 - bat tat duoi

Nhan Ion tail va Dust tail de an hoac hien moi luong, sau do bat man hinh toan phan de giu cac dieu khien hien thi khi ban quay quanh khung nhin.
