Frequently Asked Questions

What is Connect Four AI?

A free browser Connect Four game. Drop discs on a 7x6 board and try to connect four in a row against AI or a local human.

How do I play?

Press Play, choose offline AI, local human, or AI vs AI, then click a column to drop your disc.

Is online multiplayer available?

No. This page ships offline modes only. There is no remote matchmaking.

How big is the download?

About 40 KB of HTML, JS, and CSS after you press Play. Later visits use the browser cache.

Does it save progress?

No persistent save is required. Each match starts from the settings dialog.

Is this open source?
