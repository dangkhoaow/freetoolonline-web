Online Stopwatch

Last reviewed: 2026-07-02
freetoolonline.com Editorial Team

This stopwatch runs entirely in your browser and times an activity to the hundredth of a second, with a lap list for comparing splits. There is nothing to download, install, or sign up for - click Start and the display begins counting up immediately.

Click Start to begin timing. Lap records the current elapsed time into the list below without stopping the clock, so you can capture as many splits as you need while the stopwatch keeps running. Click Stop to pause the count, then Start again to resume from where you left off. Reset clears the display and the lap list back to 00:00.00 for a fresh run.

The display updates continuously in MM:SS.hh format while the stopwatch is running, driven by your browser's own clock - not a server - so there is no upload and no account. Nothing you time or record here is sent anywhere or stored once you leave the page.

Use it for things like timing a workout set, a cooking or study session, a presentation rehearsal, or comparing lap splits during a run or a race - anywhere you need a simple, no-fuss stopwatch without installing an app. The tab has to stay open while timing: closing it or navigating away stops the count, since there is no background process keeping it running.

Need to count down instead of up? The companion countdown timer lets you set a duration and counts down to zero. Just want to check the current time? The digital clock and analog clock both show a live display, one as a large numeric readout and the other as a classic sweeping clock face.
