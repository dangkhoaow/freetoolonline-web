Khi nao choi Layer Flip Platformer

Layer Flip Platformer hop khi ban muon van ngan tren ban phim: lat lop bang Space, qua man lam tay, roi thoat khong can luu. Tai khoang ~44 KB sau Play, khong can tai khoan.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Khi nao hop

Chon khi co vai phut, co ban phim hoac gamepad, va thich timing puzzle-platform hon chase diem endless. Co che dac trung la lat lop dang bat khi dang nhay. Tien do chi trong phien - dong tab se reset, tot tren may dung chung.

Khi nao khong phai lua chon dung

Bo qua neu chi can touch (ban nay khong co), neu muon runner endless nhu Pixel Spike Run, hoac dash mot cham nhu One Tap Platformer. Cung khong luu high-score cloud - khong co localStorage.

Ky vong gi

Hai muoi lam man, dem chet va sao, Web Audio procedural va menu controls. Thich nghi tu ONOFF (js13kGames 2018, MIT). Mien phi, khong ads trong iframe, khong cai.
