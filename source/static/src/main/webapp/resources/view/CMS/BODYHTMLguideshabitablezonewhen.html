When to use Habitable Zone 3D Explorer

Use the Habitable Zone 3D Explorer when you need a quick browser view of how the liquid-water HZ shrinks for dimmer stars - and skip it when you need a full atmospheric or climate integration.

  • Classroom demos of r_HZ proportional to sqrt(L/Lsun) from 0.95-1.37 AU at L=1
  • Comparing Sun vs M dwarf vs TRAPPIST-1-class HZ distances in AU
  • One-screen refresher before reading exoplanet-transit or star-lifecycle siblings

This page does not replace Kopparapu per-star climate integrations, does not model greenhouse atmospheres, and compresses orbital spacing for teaching.
