Cach choi Eight Ball Pool - tung buoc

The Eight Ball Pool page loads a ~13 MB classic eight-ball billiards game in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, pick 1P or 2P, aim with the mouse, set power with W/S, and click to shoot.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~13 MB). The canvas #screen boots inside #gameArea.

Step 2 - pick a mode

Choose 1 player (then a difficulty) or 2 players from the main menu.

Step 3 - aim and set power

Move the mouse to aim the cue. Press W to raise power and S to lower it.

Step 4 - shoot

Left-click to strike. Esc returns to the main menu. Mute is available on the menu.
