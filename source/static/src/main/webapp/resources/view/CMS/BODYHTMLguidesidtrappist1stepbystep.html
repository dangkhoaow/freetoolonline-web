Cara menjelajah TRAPPIST-1 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch all 7 real planets orbit together.

  1. Open /space-3d/trappist-1.html and wait for the star and 7 planets to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch the planets orbit at their real relative speeds.
  3. Notice how the inner planets lap the outer ones repeatedly - that's the resonance chain in motion.
  4. Toggle the habitable-zone band to see which planets it covers.
  5. Check the per-planet table in the facts panel for exact distances and periods.
