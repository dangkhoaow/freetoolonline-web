How to explore N-Body Sandbox 3D Explorer

Open the N-Body Sandbox 3D Explorer, click the ground to add bodies, then watch real gravity produce orbits, scatters, and captures.

  1. Open /space-3d/n-body-sandbox.html and wait for the starter four-body system.
  2. Read the facts panel: the real G value, bound count, and escaping count.
  3. Choose Light or Heavy for the next body, then click the ground to add it.
  4. Watch fading trails as bodies orbit, scatter, or get captured.
  5. Compare with Three-Body Problem 3D for a fixed choreography, or Gravity Well 3D for static potential wells.
