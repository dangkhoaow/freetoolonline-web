Parallax Distance 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Parallax Distance 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and try the parallax presets. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - Earth baseline

Press Earth baseline to see the opposite-orbit ends that form the 2 AU teaching base of the triangle.


Step 2 - Proxima preset

Press Proxima 0.7687" and read the panel distance about 1.30 pc from d = 1/p.


Step 3 - definition presets

Press 1 arcsec = 1 pc and 0.5 arcsec, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while the star depth follows 1/p.
