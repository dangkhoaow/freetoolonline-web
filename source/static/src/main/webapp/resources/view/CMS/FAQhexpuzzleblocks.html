Frequently Asked Questions

What does Hex Puzzle Blocks do?

It is a hexagon color-matching puzzle that runs entirely in the browser tab: rotate the center hexagon with the arrow keys, A / D, or clicks/taps so 3 or more same-colored blocks touch and clear before a stack reaches the middle on any side. It runs on the open-source Hextris engine.

Does it work on a phone or tablet?

Yes. Tapping the left or right half of the screen rotates the hexagon, so it plays on touchscreens as well as desktop browsers with a keyboard or mouse.

Is my progress or high score saved?

Yes, but only in this browser: the current run and your top 3 scores are kept in local storage on this device, so returning to the page can resume where you left off. Nothing is uploaded, and there is no cross-device sync, leaderboard, or account.

Is there multiplayer or an online leaderboard?

No. Hex Puzzle Blocks is single-player only, with no online leaderboard, matchmaking, or account system.
