When to use Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer

Use the Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer when you need giant-planet magnetosphere scale - and skip it when you only need Earth's ~6-10 Re case.

  • Classroom demos of ~20,000x volume vs Earth
  • Magnetotail past Saturn (~9.5 AU) literacy
  • Contrasting Rj standoff with Earth Magnetosphere 3D

This page does not solve MHD, does not stream live solar wind, and does not model the Io torus.
