Tip Calculator Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Tip Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Tip Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it to settle a group bill quickly without doing the math by hand.


Step 3 - get the result

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.

Change any field at any time and every total recalculates immediately.
