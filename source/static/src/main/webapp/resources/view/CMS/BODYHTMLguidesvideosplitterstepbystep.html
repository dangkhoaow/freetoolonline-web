Video Splitter Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Video Splitter runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Video Splitter. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Upload your video file, then type the split point into the "Split at" field - plain seconds ("90") or mm:ss ("1:30") both work. That one point is where the cut happens.


Step 3 - get the result

The tool produces two separate clips: everything before the split point, and everything after. Each one appears as its own download link - there is no zip or archive step, and the original file is not retained once processing finishes.
