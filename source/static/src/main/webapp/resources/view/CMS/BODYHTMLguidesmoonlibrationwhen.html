When to use Moon Libration 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Use this explorer when you want to understand why a tidally locked Moon still shows 59% of its surface - and skip it when you want the lunar phases or the mechanism of tidal locking itself.

  • Classroom demos of libration in longitude, latitude, and the 59% result
  • Separating the elliptical-orbit wobble from the axial-tilt nod
  • Explaining how the near side and far side relate to what we can see

This is an educational visualization with a time-compressed, optionally exaggerated wobble and illustrative surface markers.
