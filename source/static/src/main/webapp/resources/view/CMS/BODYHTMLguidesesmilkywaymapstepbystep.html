Milky Way Map 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Milky Way Map 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit the disk, scroll to zoom, and press Play spin. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - find the Sun

Click Sun so the yellow fleck and ~26,000 ly figure appear in the panel against the ~100,000 ly disk.


Step 2 - named arms

Jump Sagittarius, Orion, and Perseus to read each teaching label while the disk slowly turns.


Step 3 - galactic center

Press Center, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while you orbit the warm core.
