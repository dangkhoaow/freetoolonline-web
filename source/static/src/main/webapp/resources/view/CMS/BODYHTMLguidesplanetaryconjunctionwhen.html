When to use Planetary Conjunction 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want the rhythm of planets lining up, and skip it when you want a planet apparent backward loop or the shape of a single orbit.

  • Explaining the great conjunction and its 19.86-year rhythm
  • Showing the synodic-period rule with a real planet pair
  • Seeing why the conjunction triangle drifts across centuries

This is an educational visualization with circular, not-to-scale orbits and Sun-centered timing.
