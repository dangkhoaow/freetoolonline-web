How to Play Gravity Orbit Golf

Gravity Orbit Golf loads about ~41 KB in an iframe after Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Press Play, pick Daily/Random/Seed, then drag to aim each shot.

Launch

Click Play on this page, then choose a course mode inside the iframe.

Aim

Drag from the comet to preview trajectory; release to fire.

Controls

M mutes audio; R bails a doomed flight; Esc returns to the title menu.
