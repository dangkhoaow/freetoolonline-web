Choose a photo and click Restore & upscale 4x; the long edge is resized to 512px before processing so the AI model finishes in a reasonable time even on a large photo.

Restoring a photo takes three steps:

  1. Choose a photo - the original previews immediately, before any processing starts.
  2. Click Restore & upscale 4x - the first run downloads the restoration model once (cached offline after that), then sharpens, denoises, and upscales the photo 4x.
  3. Download the result - the photo is processed entirely in this browser tab and is never uploaded to a server.
