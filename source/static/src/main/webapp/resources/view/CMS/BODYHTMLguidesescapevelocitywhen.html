When to use Escape Velocity 3D Explorer

Use the Escape Velocity 3D Explorer when you need a quick browser view of sub-orbital vs circular vs escape vs hyperbolic paths - and skip it when you need a full n-body propagator or rocket staging model.

  • Classroom demos of why circular orbit speed is v_esc / sqrt(2)
  • Comparing Moon 2.38 km/s vs Earth 11.2 km/s vs Jupiter 59.5 km/s escape thresholds
  • One-screen refresher before reading gravity-well or kepler-orbits siblings

This page does not replace STK or GMAT ephemerides, does not model atmospheric drag, and does not solve n-body gravity.
