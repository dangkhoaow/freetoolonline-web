Welcome to Desk Cat Coder - a free browser stealth desk platformer you can play without an account or install. Press Play to load the ~72 KB canvas engine in a same-origin iframe, click Start Game on the title screen, then use arrow keys to climb shelves to the computer. Spam letter keys to add lines while the owner is away. When they return, drink, snack, or push desk items to buy more typing time. Seven work days fill a git-style grid; ranks update from total lines shipped. Privacy note: nothing leaves this device except the initial page and game files from this site.
