Khi nao Satellite Orbit Classes thang the

Satellite Orbit Classes thang the khi cau hoi la "vi sao cac ve tinh khac nhau song o do cao khac nhau" - ca nam dai cung chuyen dong voi toc do tuong doi thuc te, nen MEO cua mot ve tinh GPS va GEO cua mot ve tinh thoi tiet co the so sanh truc tiep trong mot goc nhin.


Khi nao ISS Orbit Tracker thang the

ISS Orbit Tracker thang the khi cau hoi la ve mot tau vu tru thuc chi tiet - no ve duong bay mau vang tren mat dat duoi duong di cua tram va cho ban keo pha quy dao, dieu mot canh so sanh nam dai khong lam.
