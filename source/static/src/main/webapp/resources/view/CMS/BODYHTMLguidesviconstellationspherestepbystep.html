Cach dung Constellation Sphere 3D Explorer theo tung buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Bat dau voi Orion, chuyen giua cac hinh sao Ursa Major, Crux hoac Summer Triangle, chinh gioi han do sang (Mag limit) roi bam vao mot ngoi sao de doc RA theo gio, Dec theo do va do sang V tren mat cau minh hoa.

  1. Mo Constellation Sphere 3D Explorer va cho mat cau ve xong.
  2. Keo de xoay, cuon hoac chum hai ngon tay de thu phong, giu bang thong tin hien thi tren man hinh rong.
  3. Cham vao Orion, Ursa Major, Crux hoac Summer Triangle de lam noi bat cac hinh sao.
  4. Dung Mag limit va Hide grid de dieu chinh do day sao va luoi giang day theo xich dao.
  5. Bam vao mot ngoi sao co ten de RA, Dec va do sang V la thong tin dang tin cay.
