How to use Type Ia Supernova Standard Candle 3D Explorer

  1. Open the Type Ia Supernova Standard Candle 3D Explorer and wait for the canvas to paint.
  2. Drag to orbit the view and scroll to zoom in on the white dwarf and its red-giant donor.
  3. Scrub the sequence slider from 0 to 70 to grow the white dwarf's mass toward the Chandrasekhar limit.
  4. Keep scrubbing past 70 to watch the explosion and the fading light-curve readout.
  5. Move the distance slider to see the apparent magnitude the facts panel computes at that distance.
