Cara Main Claudicus Quest - Langkah demi Langkah

Halaman Claudicus Quest memuat a ~132 KB turn-based medieval RPG in an iframe. You explore Brannford, Thornwood, and Greymoor Crypt, take quests, shop for gear, and save to ftol:claudicusquest:save. Tekan Play untuk mulai.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

WASD or arrows move; Space/Enter interact; combat 1 Attack, 2 Defend, 3 Potion, 4 Flee, 5 special; I inventory; Q quest log; M mute; Esc pause/save. Saves to ftol:claudicusquest:save.

Langkah 1 - tekan Play and start or continue

Tekan Play di halaman game to inject the iframe (~132 KB). On the title screen pick New Game for character select, or Continue when a namespaced save exists. The engine is Claudicus by Brian Benzinger (MIT); LICENSE and CREDITS ship beside the game files.

Langkah 2 - bergerak and interact on tile maps

Walk with WASD or arrow keys on Brannford (village hub), Thornwood (forest), and Greymoor Crypt (dungeon). Press Space or Enter to talk to NPCs, open chests, use shop counters, and step through map transitions.

Langkah 3 - bertarung with the combat menu

When a fight starts, press 1 Attack, 2 Defend, 3 Potion (shows your remaining count), 4 Flee, and 5 when a special is listed (Shield Bash, Ambush, Intimidate, or weapon skills such as Backstab at level 3+). Combat is turn-based with a scrolling log at the bottom.

Langkah 4 - inventori, quests, and class at level 3

Press I for the inventory screen (weapons, armor, potions, earned titles). Press Q for the quest log. When you reach level 3, the game opens class select: WARRIOR (+2 DEF), SCOUT (+2 AGI), or BRIGAND (+2 STR) - the screen states the choice is permanent.

Langkah 5 - jeda, save, and maps

Press Esc for pause (Resume, Save Game, Quit to Title). Auto-save also runs after key events. Defeat respawns you in Brannford with a gold penalty. Press M anytime to mute procedural music and SFX.
