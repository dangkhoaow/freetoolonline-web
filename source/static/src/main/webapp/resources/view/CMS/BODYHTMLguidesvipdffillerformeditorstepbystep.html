PDF "co the dien" nghia la gi

Mot file PDF co the mang mot tu dien AcroForm: mot tap hop cac truong co ten, nam tren lop noi dung tinh cua trang. Moi truong co mot loai (o van ban, o chon, danh sach xo xuong, nhom tuy chon, hoac danh sach chon nhieu muc) va mot gia tri hien tai. Nguoi tao PDF chu dinh them cac truong nay - bieu mau thue, bieu mau dang ky, don xin viec va nhieu mau hop dong deu co san dang nay. Mot PDF khong co tu dien AcroForm la "phang" - thu ban thay la toan bo file, khong co lop truong rieng de dien. Quet mot bieu mau giay thanh PDF cung tao ra file phang: cac o tren trang chi la muc in, khong phai truong ma chuong trinh co the doc duoc.

PDF Form Filler hoat dong bang cach doc tu dien AcroForm cua file ban chon voi thu vien pdf-lib, hoan toan trong tab trinh duyet. No khong bao gio gui file len may chu. Neu tu dien rong, cong cu se noi ro dieu do thay vi gia vo da dien duoc gi - no dien cac truong da ton tai san va khong them truong moi vao mot trang phang hoac ban quet.

Khi mot truong khong duoc nhan dien

PDF Form Filler ho tro nam loai truong trong bang tren, chiem phan lon cac mau bieu mau dua tren AcroForm. Mot loai truong ngoai danh sach nay van hien trong danh sach, o dang o nhap bi khoa kem chu thich rang truong nay khong ho tro va duoc giu nguyen - vi vay mot bieu mau co mot truong khac thuong se khong lam ket lai viec dien cac truong con lai, va khong co gi bi bo qua hay hong am tham.

Cong cu nay khong lam nhung gi

Ba viec de bi nham voi dien bieu mau; moi viec la mot cong viec khac nhau:

  • Them truong vao PDF phang hoac ban quet. PDF Form Filler dien cac truong da ton tai san. No khong chay OCR de tim o trong tren trang quet va khong bien mot PDF phang thanh PDF co the dien. Neu ban can tao mot mau bieu mau moi co the dien, buoc do dien ra trong phan mem soan PDF truoc khi file den voi cong cu nay.
  • Khoa vinh vien cac gia tri da dien. Dien mot truong o day khong lam phang bieu mau - file PDF tai xuong van con truong dang hoat dong va co the sua, nen bat ky ai mo file sau do (ke ca ban) cung co the doi lai gia tri trong phan mem PDF khac. Neu quy trinh can gia tri da dien duoc coc dinh thanh noi dung tinh cua trang, hay dua ket qua qua Flatten PDF sau khi dien.
  • Sap xep lai file. Tach mot PDF, ghep nhieu PDF, chuyen trang thanh anh, hoac go mat khau la nhung viec rieng, do cac cong cu khac tren trang PDF tools dam nhan - PDF Form Filler chi doc va ghi gia tri cua truong bieu mau.

Vi sao khong tai len lai quan trong

Mot bieu mau da dien thuong mang ten, dia chi, so tai khoan hoac cac thong tin ca nhan khac - dung loai noi dung ban khong muon gui den mot may chu ban khong kiem soat. Vi PDF Form Filler chay cac buoc doc, dien va luu bang pdf-lib ngay trong tab trinh duyet cua ban, file va cac gia tri ban nhap luon nam tren may cua ban tu dau den cuoi; khong co yeu cau tai len de kiem tra va khong can tao tai khoan.

PDF Form Filler co tai file cua toi len may chu khong?

Khong. File duoc doc trong trinh duyet cua ban bang pdf-lib va khong bao gio roi khoi tab; PDF da dien cung duoc tao va tai xuong ngay tren may.

Toi co the tiep tuc sua bieu mau sau khi tai PDF da dien xuong khong?

Co. Dien mot truong khong lam phang truong do - file PDF tai xuong van giu cac truong co the sua trong phan mem PDF khac. Dung Flatten PDF sau do neu ban can khoa vinh vien cac gia tri.

File PDF cua toi khong co truong nao de dien - phai lam sao?

PDF Form Filler khong the them truong vao mot trang phang hoac ban quet. Ban se can mot phien ban file da co tu dien AcroForm, hoac mot quy trinh khac de them truong truoc khi dua file vao cong cu nay.

Neu mot truong bi sai hoac khong dien duoc thi sao?

Mot loai truong khong duoc nhan dien se hien o dang bi khoa kem chu thich, thay vi dien sai. Neu mot truong trong rong du PDF hien gia tri, hay tai lai file va doi chieu ten truong voi bieu mau in - cong cu giu ten goc cua moi truong lam nhan cho dieu khien.
