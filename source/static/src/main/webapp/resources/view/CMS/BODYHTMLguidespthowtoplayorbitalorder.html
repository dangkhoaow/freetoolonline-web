Como jogar Orbital Order - passo a passo

The Orbital Order page loads a ~32 KB Aufbau electron puzzle in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, move the cursor to guide electrons, and fill orbitals from hydrogen to oxygen.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~32 KB). Canvas #gameCanvas boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - move the cursor

Blue s-electrons follow the cursor. Orange p-electrons flee. Use that attract/repel pair to steer.

Step 3 - seat electrons

Guide each electron into an empty orbital. Avoid double-hitting filled shells or the electron ejects.

Step 4 - climb elements

Clear six element levels. Optional keys: M mute, I help, L log, Esc close. Best time stores under ftol:orbitalorder:fastestTime.
