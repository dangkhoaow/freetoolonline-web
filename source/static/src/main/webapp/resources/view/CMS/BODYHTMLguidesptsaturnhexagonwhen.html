Quando usar Saturn Hexagon 3D Explorer

Use the Saturn Hexagon 3D Explorer when you need the polar hexagon jet - and skip it when you need ring-band radii or belt banding alone.

  • Classroom demos of Voyager/Cassini polar imagery
  • Linking ~30,000 km width and ~120 m/s jet before atmosphere units
  • One-screen refresher on the six-sided polar outline

This page does not solve fluid dynamics, does not map Cassini camera frames, and exaggerates hexagon scale for teaching.
