Mo trang Gryponyx va dieu khien camera

Mo Gryponyx 3D Viewer va cho bang thong tin day du. Khi dong trang thai cho phep keo, quay quanh Gryponyx bang con tro va zoom bang ban cuon hoac cham hai ngon. Dung bon dieu khien Gryponyx duoi canvas

Duoi canvas Gryponyx, bon dieu khien xu ly goc nhin va ty le:

  • Fullscreen - phong man hinh giu cac nut hien
  • Stop rotation / Auto-rotate - dung hoac tiep tuc tu quay
  • Idle motion - them dao nhe; cham lai cho Stand still
  • Size vs human - hien nguoi 1.8 m o ty le chieu dai trung thuc cua panel (khoang 5 m)

Doc bang fact Gryponyx va bam vung co the

Bang liet ke chieu dai khoang 5 m (panel 5 m), cao khoang 1.6-2.0 m o hong (panel 1.8 m), khoi luong bi rang buoc kem voi uoc tinh phu khoang 485 kg, Ky Jurass som khoang 201-190 trieu nam o Tang Upper Elliot Nam Phi, an co (sauropodomorph), dat ten Broom 1911 voi lich su lien quan va dong nghia Massospondylus - Vasconcelos va Yates cho Gryponyx la rieng. Bam ngan - khong keo - tren vung hien fact ngan. Mau mo mem la tai tao nghe thuat. Khi glTF CC-BY-SA 4.0 cua seth the yutyrannus qua Printables tai, dong trang thai ghi model that voi credit do - tai tao co phong cach, khong phai laser scan. Model nay khong phai bo xuong trung thuc voi hoa thach.
