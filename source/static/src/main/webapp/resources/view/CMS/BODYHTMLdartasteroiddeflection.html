Before impact - Dimorphos orbit: 11h 55m

Published figures (NASA/JHU Applied Physics Laboratory DART mission; peer-reviewed orbit result in Nature, 2023): DART hit Dimorphos on 26 September 2022 at 23:14 UTC at about 6.14 km/s (about 22,530 km/h), with a spacecraft mass at impact of about 579 kg. Dimorphos's orbital period around its larger companion Didymos (diameter about 780 m; Dimorphos about 160 m) shortened from 11 hours 55 minutes to about 11 hours 22 minutes - a change of 33 ± 1 minutes, more than 25 times NASA's minimum-success threshold of 73 seconds.
