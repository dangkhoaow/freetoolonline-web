Khi Nao Dung Comet Orbit 3D Explorer - Buoi Tot Nhat va Gioi Han

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Mo Comet Orbit 3D Explorer khi ban can elip lech tam kieu Halley voi duoi ion va bui huong nguoc Mat Troi - keo perihelion den aphelion, bat tung duoi rieng, va doc P khoang 76 yr, q 0.586 AU, Q 35.1 AU, va e 0.967 tren bang.


Demo lop nam phut

Demo lop nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Comet Orbit 3D Explorer, keo gan perihelion, va tam dung de hoc sinh thay duoi ion (xanh) va bui (vang) keo dai ra khoi Mat Troi - nhanh hon mot elip sach phang don le.


So Halley trong mot bang

So Halley trong mot bang van hien khi ban doc chu ky khoang 76 yr, perihelion 0.586 AU, aphelion 35.1 AU, va do lech tam 0.967, kem doc khoang cach quanh Mat Troi xap xi tren quy dao da nen. Khoang cach quy dao bi rut de vua mot man hinh; bang giu cac so da cong bo.


Buoi ma Comet Orbit 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Buoi ma Comet Orbit 3D Explorer khong phu hop la tour chi cac hanh tinh, khoang Kirkwood vanh dai chinh, hoac khoa thuy trieu Mat Trang. Hay dung Solar System 3D Explorer, Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer, hoac Tidal Locking 3D Explorer thay the. Trang sao choi can WebGL, khong phai mo hinh N vat the hay hoa hoc outgassing, va khong cho ephemeris truc tiep cho lan Halley tro lai tiep theo.
