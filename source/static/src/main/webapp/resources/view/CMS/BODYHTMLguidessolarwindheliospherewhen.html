When to use Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer

Use the Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer when you need a quick browser view of the termination shock and heliopause bubble - and skip it when you need a full MHD heliosphere run.

  • Classroom demos of termination shock ~94 AU vs heliopause ~121 AU
  • Pinning Voyager 1 (2012) on the outer boundary next to ~400 km/s wind context
  • One-screen refresher before reading solar-system or aurora siblings

This page does not solve MHD equations, does not forecast heliosheath structure, and compresses radial AU spacing for teaching.
