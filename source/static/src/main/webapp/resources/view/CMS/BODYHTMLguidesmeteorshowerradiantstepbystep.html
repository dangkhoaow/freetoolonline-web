How to explore Meteor Shower Radiant 3D Explorer

Open the Meteor Shower Radiant 3D Explorer, switch between the Perseids and Leonids, and watch parallel streaks keep one apparent radiant on the sky.

  1. Open /space-3d/meteor-shower-radiant.html and wait for the local sky dome and radiant ring.
  2. Read the facts panel: Perseids peak Aug 12-13, ZHR ~100, parent 109P/Swift-Tuttle.
  3. Switch from Perseids to Leonids to compare parent streams.
  4. Scrub Speed and Trail, or press Pause/Play streaks.
  5. Orbit the camera to confirm the radiant is a perspective direction, not a physical origin point.
