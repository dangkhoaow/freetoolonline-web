Cong cu tao ZIP truc tuyen thuc su lam gi

Cong cu xu ly ba viec tren mot trang. Truoc tien, no gom: cac lua chon nhieu tep va toan bo thu muc (cac thu muc con duoc giu nguyen) tro thanh mot .zip duy nhat ma nguoi nhan tai xuong bang mot cu nhap. Thu hai, no co the khoa bang mat khau: mat khau tuy chon va mot phuong phap ma hoa (Standard, AES-128 hoac AES-256) tao mot kho luu tru duoc bao ve khi mat khau se duoc chia se qua mot kenh rieng. Thu ba, no dat ten cho dau ra: mot cua so Cai dat cho phep dat ten kho luu tru truoc khi tai xuong de nguoi nhan thay du an hoac chu de trong ten thay vi mot dau thoi gian chung chung.

Khi nao tao ZIP that su tiet kiem khong gian

ZIP chi thu nho dau vao khi dau vao do co the nen duoc. Van ban thuan, log, CSV, JSON, ma nguon va bitmap chua nen thuong giam con 30-60% kich thuoc goc khi gop voi nhau vi cac byte lap lai va thuat toan ZIP loai bo phan lap. Anh JPG, video MP4, am thanh MP3 va da so PDF da duoc nen boi codec rieng cua chung, nen ZIP boc chung lai voi muc thay doi khoang 0-5% va co the tang nhe vi tieu de dong goi. Meo de gui kho luu tru nho hon la zip cac tep co the nen lai voi nhau de duoc loi that, va xu ly phuong tien rieng (thay doi kich thuoc hoac ma hoa lai), chu khong phai zip moi thu va hy vong.

Cach tao mot tep ZIP trong trinh duyet

  1. Mo /zip-tools/zip-file.html.
  2. Tha hoac chon cac tep (mot thu muc cung duoc; cac thu muc con duoc giu nguyen).
  3. Tuy chon: mo Cai dat, dat ten tep va them mat khau cung phuong phap ma hoa neu kho luu tru can di qua kenh ma mat khau duoc chia se rieng.
  4. Nhan Zip. Trang tai cac dau vao len qua HTTPS, dung kho luu tru tren may chu, va hien thi mot lien ket tai xuong.
  5. Luu lai tep .zip. Neu ban dat mat khau, gui no qua mot kenh khac voi tep.

Bai doc lien quan

  • Khi nao nen ZIP that su tiet kiem khong gian - boi canh ve quan he kich thuoc va nen ma huong dan nay tom luoc.
  • Trinh nen Zip - cung mot tac vu voi cach dien dat thay the ma doc gia cung go vao tim kiem.
  • Cach nen mot thu muc - huong dan o che do thu muc tiep tuc tu noi huong dan nay dung lai.
  • Trung tam cong cu ZIP - cac cong cu chi em (giai nen, them/xoa mat khau, tep co mat khau) cho phan con lai cua quy trinh ZIP.
