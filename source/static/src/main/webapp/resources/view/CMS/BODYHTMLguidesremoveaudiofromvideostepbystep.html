Remove Audio From Video Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Remove Audio from Video runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Remove Audio from Video. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

The output keeps the original video quality, resolution, and container format; only the audio track is dropped.


Step 3 - get the result

The file is processed in this browser tab and is never uploaded to a server.

To trim or cut the video instead of removing its audio, see the related-tools section.
