Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

This page renders a Gryponyx as a 3D model you can spin in the browser - drag to rotate, scroll or pinch to zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person beside it for scale, and click a body region to read a fact.

Gryponyx was an Early Jurassic sauropodomorph from South Africa, known from a nearly complete postcranial skeleton (SAM 3357-59) in the Upper Elliot Formation. Broom named G. africanus in 1911. Length is about 5 m. Mass is poorly constrained; one secondary estimate is about 485 kg. Some older work synonymized it with Massospondylus; later analyses argued it is distinctive.
