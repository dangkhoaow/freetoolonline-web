Cara menjelajah Equation of Time 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the Sun trace the analemma figure-8 over a year.

  1. Open /space-3d/equation-of-time.html and wait for the figure-8.
  2. Watch the Sun move along the analemma, day by day.
  3. Note the sideways swing (the equation of time) and the up-down reach (declination).
  4. Toggle the blue month markers to place yourself in the year.
  5. Read the facts panel: the two causes and the +16 / -14 minute extremes.
