This explorer shows tidal heating: what the Io:Europa:Ganymede 1:2:4 resonance does to two moons. Io flexes with ~400 active volcanoes (NASA Juno); Europa keeps a subsurface ocean under thin ice.

Orbital Resonance 3D owns the 1:2:4 mechanics; Galilean Moons 3D owns the moon overview; this page owns the consequence.

  • Io flexing with volcanic hotspots
  • Europa ocean-cutaway toggle, plus Ganymede completing the 1:2:4 chain
  • Facts panel lists volcano count, surface flex, heat flow, and the Juno finding
  • Runs in-browser with three.js - no account, no upload

Io is the most volcanic body known; Europa's ocean is a leading habitability target.
