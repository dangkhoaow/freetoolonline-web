Frequently Asked Questions

What does Garden Defense do?

Pick a defender card - Shooter (100 energy), Battery (50, makes more energy), or Wall (50, soaks damage) - then click or tap a cell to plant it; the first placement starts wave 1 of 5.

How does the energy economy work?

Energy ticks in on its own every few seconds and every Battery adds more, so the core decision is spending on offense now versus economy for later waves.

How do I win or lose?

Lose only if an invader crosses the left edge; clear all 5 waves and the garden is saved. Your best wave is remembered in this browser.
