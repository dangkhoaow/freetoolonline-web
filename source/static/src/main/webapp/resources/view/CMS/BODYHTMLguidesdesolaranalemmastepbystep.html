So nutzen Sie Solar-Analemma 3D Explorer

Open the Solar-Analemma 3D Explorer, read the 23.44 deg / +/-16 min figures, and scrub day of year while the Sun marker rides the figure-8.

  1. Open /space-3d/solar-analemma.html and wait for the gold analemma curve and Sun marker.
  2. Read the facts panel: obliquity 23.44 deg, equation of time about +/-16 min.
  3. Scrub day of year from ~80 (spring) toward ~172 (June) to climb the northern loop.
  4. Press Play year, then Pause year, to auto-advance along the curve.
  5. Toggle Hide/Show EoT bar to see the east-west equation-of-time offset.
