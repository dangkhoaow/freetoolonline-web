Find And Replace Text Khi Nao Dung

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Find and Replace Text dang de mo khi ban can chay nhanh mot lan ma khong can cai dat gi. Trang nay noi khi nao phu hop va khi nao khong.


Khi nao phu hop

Dung de doi mot ten trong tai lieu da dan, sua mot loi go lap lai, hoac cap nhat mot gia tri placeholder trong khoi van ban.


Can gi khi dung

Tim kiem la khop van ban theo dung nghia (khong phai bieu thuc chinh quy); o "Match case" dieu khien viec tim co phan biet hoa thuong hay khong.

Thay the chay cuc bo ngay trong tab trinh duyet nay - khong tai len va khong can tai khoan; khong luu lai bat cu thu gi ban go giua cac lan truy cap.


Khi nao khong phu hop

Find and Replace Text khong giup duoc neu ban can khop theo mau - mot ky tu dac biet hoac lop ky tu trong cum tu tim kiem duoc so khop nhu van ban thuong, khong phai bieu thuc chinh quy, nen se khong bat duoc cac bien the nhu regex.
