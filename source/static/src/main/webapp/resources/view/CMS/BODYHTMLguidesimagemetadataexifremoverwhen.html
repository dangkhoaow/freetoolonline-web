When to strip EXIF before you share a photo

Use this remover when a JPEG or HEIC still carries camera model, shoot time, or GPS and you are about to post, email, or upload it somewhere public.

Choose the file, optionally click Read Metadata to confirm the tags, then click Download Without Metadata. The page re-encodes a JPEG in the browser so those tags are dropped - the original never leaves your device.

If you only need to inspect tags and keep the original file bytes, use the sibling Image EXIF Metadata Viewer instead. Prefer this remover when the downloadable clean JPEG is the goal.
