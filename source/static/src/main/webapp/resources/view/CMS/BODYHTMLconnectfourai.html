Connect Four AI is a free browser Connect Four board where you drop discs to make four in a row. Play offline against the built-in AI, sit across from another person on the same device, or watch two AI players. About 40 KB after Play. No account. Online multiplayer is not offered on this page.

Want a deeper turn-based board game? Try Mor Chess 2. Prefer a short AI duel with a different mechanic? Try Rock Paper Neural.
