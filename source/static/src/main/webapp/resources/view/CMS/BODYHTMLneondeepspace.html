Neon Deep Space is a free browser geometry-war shooter. Move with WASD, aim and shoot with the mouse, dash with Shift, drop a bomb with Space, and switch weapons with F. Earn energy, open the shop between runs, and keep upgrades on-device. About ~152 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a different star shooter loop? Try Andromeda Star Shooter. Prefer a fuel-combat arena? Try Star Fuel Battle.

NEON DEEP SPACE

Geometry-war roguelite: WASD move, mouse aim / click fire, Shift dash, Space bomb, F weapon switch, Esc pause. Energy, upgrades, and high score use ftol:neondeepspace:* keys. Adapted from ou178/neon-deep-space (MIT). About ~152 KB.

Best with keyboard + mouse. Touch can aim, but WASD and Shift are desktop-first.

Press Play to load the game.
Saves stay on this device only.
