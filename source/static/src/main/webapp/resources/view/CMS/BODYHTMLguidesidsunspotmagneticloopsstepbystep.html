Cara menjelajah Sunspot Magnetic Loops 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch coronal loops arc between a sunspot pair, tracing the Sun magnetic field.

  1. Open /space-3d/sunspot-magnetic-loops.html and wait for the active region.
  2. Read the facts panel: the umbral field (~0.3 T), umbra temperature, and 1-3 million K loops.
  3. Watch plasma packets flow along the arcade of loops.
  4. Toggle the polarity markers to see the two magnetic footpoints, or hide the loops.
  5. Compare with the 3D Sunspot Cycle explorer for the 11-year cycle, or Sun Structure 3D for the interior layers.
