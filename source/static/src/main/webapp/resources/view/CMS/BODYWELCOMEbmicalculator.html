BMI Calculator

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Calculate body mass index (BMI) from height and weight and see the standard weight category.

Use it as a quick, general reference point for weight-for-height.

Pick metric (kg/cm) or imperial (lb/in) from the unit dropdown - the weight and height labels switch to match, so there is no unit conversion to do by hand.

BMI sorts into four standard bands: Underweight (below 18.5), Normal weight (18.5 to 25), Overweight (25 to 30), and Obesity (30 and above). The category updates alongside the BMI value as you type.

BMI does not account for muscle mass, age, or body composition - it is a general screening estimate only, not medical advice.

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.
