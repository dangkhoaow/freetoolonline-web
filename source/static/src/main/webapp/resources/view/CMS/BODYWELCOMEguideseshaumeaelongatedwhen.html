When to use Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer

Companion guide for the Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer.

Reach for this guide when you are planning a short astronomy segment and need to decide where Haumea fits: introduce it right after Pluto and the Kuiper Belt, or save it for a lesson on fast rotation and triaxial shapes. The talking points below travel well even if you have never read the original occultation paper.
