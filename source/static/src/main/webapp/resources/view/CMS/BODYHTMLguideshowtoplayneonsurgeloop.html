How to Play Neon Surge Loop - Step by Step

The Neon Surge Loop page loads a ~40 KB canvas roguelite shooter in an iframe. Mouse-move steering and auto-fire - no install required.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play on the page, click START GAME inside the iframe, move the mouse to steer, survive waves, collect XP gems, and pick upgrade cards on level-up. Press Space for Nova only after you unlock that upgrade.

Step 1 - launch

Click Play on the game page to load the iframe. Click START GAME on the neon title screen to begin the run.

Step 2 - steer and shoot

Move the mouse anywhere on the canvas; your ship follows the cursor. Projectiles auto-fire on a cooldown without clicking.

Step 3 - level up

Touch lime or gold XP gems to fill the bar. When you level, three upgrade cards appear - click one to apply fire-rate, damage, multi-shot, pierce, or Nova unlocks.
