Pdf Compressor Online Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Pdf Compressor Online runs in three steps: open the tool, choose your PDF, then download the smaller file. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Pdf Compressor Online. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - choose your PDF

Pick a PDF file and click Compress PDF; the file is re-saved with a more efficient internal structure entirely in the browser - it is never uploaded.


Step 3 - download the result

The status line shows the before and after size and the percent saved, or "already optimal" when there is no meaningful change. Click Download PDF to save the result; original input is not retained beyond the page session.
