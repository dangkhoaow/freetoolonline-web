Companion guide for the JWST L2 Halo Orbit 3D Explorer. This page sits alongside the when-to-use guide and the comparison against alternatives, so you can move between control instructions, timing guidance, and the numbers table without leaving the guide set.

The explorer itself runs entirely client-side in the browser with the vendored three.js engine - no account, no install, and no scene data leaves your device. For the wider picture, the Space 3D collection also has a Lagrange Points explorer covering the general five-point geometry and an N-Body Sandbox for free-form gravity play.
