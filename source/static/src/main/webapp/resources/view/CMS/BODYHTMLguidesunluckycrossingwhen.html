When to Play Unlucky Crossing

Unlucky Crossing fits short breaks where you want a score-chase arcade loop with simple row-by-row movement - about ~72 KB after Play, no install.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

One-more-row score runs

One-more-row score runs fit Unlucky Crossing: each forward step is a quick risk decision - dodge humans, avoid obstacles, and line up close crosses for bigger point splashes.

When you like infinite levels

When you like infinite levels, Unlucky Crossing fits: new rows generate as you climb. There is no fixed campaign end - only your high score and nine happiness lives.

When to pick another game

When to pick another game instead of Unlucky Crossing: want keyboard typing instead of crossing? Use Cat Typing Race. Want a glow chase? Use Glow Firefly Cat.
