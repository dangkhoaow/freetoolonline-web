When the Amargasaurus browser viewer fits

Pick the browser viewer when you want drag-orbit with Size vs human at the honest 10 m panel length ratio and click facts on the neck spines - without installing anything.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for real mounts.
