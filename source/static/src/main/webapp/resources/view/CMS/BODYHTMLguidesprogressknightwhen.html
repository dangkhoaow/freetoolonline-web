When to Play Progress Knight

Progress Knight fits a long idle life-sim session - about ~0.12 MB after Play, no install.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

When you want medieval careers and prestige

Jobs, skills, shop, aging, and amulet rebirth reward planning across many lives.

When you want on-device saves

Progress stores in ftol:progressknight:gameDataSave. Nothing leaves the browser.

When to pick another game

Want a capitalist factory idle instead of medieval life-sim? Use Idle Capitalist Loop. Want a short timing arcade? Use Swing Block Tower.
