When the Brachylophosaurus browser viewer fits

Pick the Brachylophosaurus browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a Campanian hadrosaurid with Size vs human at the honest 9 m panel length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything. Nothing about the visit is sent to a server.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your living-room floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for walking beside real hadrosaur mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame with no interactivity.
