Frequently Asked Questions

What does Voxel World Builder do?

It is a first-person voxel sandbox that runs entirely in the browser tab: infinite procedurally generated terrain with six biomes, caves, and ore veins, 35 block types on a creative picker, flying, and a 20-minute day-night cycle. Hold left click to break blocks, right click to place them.

How is my world saved?

The world autosaves to this browser every few seconds while you play and again when you quit to the title screen - closing the tab does not lose the build. The save lives in this browser's storage only, so clearing site data deletes it and it does not follow you to another device. The seed on the title screen rebuilds the same terrain anywhere, but not the blocks you placed.

How big can I build?

The terrain is infinite - new chunks generate as you walk - with a world height of 128 blocks. You choose how much renders at once with the render distance option (4 to 12 chunks) in the pause menu.

Does it work on a phone?

The page and the world render on a phone or tablet, but the game has no touch controls - moving, looking, and building need a keyboard and mouse, so a desktop or laptop is the way to actually play.
