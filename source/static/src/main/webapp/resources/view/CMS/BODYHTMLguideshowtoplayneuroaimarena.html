How to Play Neuro Aim Arena - Step by Step

The Neuro Aim Arena page loads a ~160 KB neuroevolution aim duel in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Press Play, click the canvas to start, then WASD move and mouse aim/shoot against evolving neural bots.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~160 KB). Artwork loads, then the start screen appears.

Step 2 - click to start

Click the canvas once to leave the start screen and begin the first generation.

Step 3 - move, aim, shoot

WASD or arrows move. Mouse aims. Hold mouse button to fire. Avoid screen borders - they damage you. Push bots into borders when you can.

Step 4 - survive generations

When all bots die, genetics breed the next generation from fitness scores. Die and click to restart from generation 1.
