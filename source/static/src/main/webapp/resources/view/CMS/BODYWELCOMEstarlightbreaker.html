Starlight Breaker

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play and a full brick-breaker session loads in the tab. Move the deer paddle with your mouse or finger, keep the star ball in play, break every brick, and bank high scores locally. Three lives per run; clear the board to win.

Controls at a glance:

ActionInput
Move paddleMouse move or touch drag
Start / menuOn-screen buttons inside the game
Pause / resumeEsc or Pause button
High scoresHigh Scores menu (saved locally)

High scores and touch-guide preference save locally under ftol:starlightbreaker:* keys in your browser only. The game is adapted from Chan Meng's open-source html-brick-game project (MIT license); credits and license text ship next to the game files.
