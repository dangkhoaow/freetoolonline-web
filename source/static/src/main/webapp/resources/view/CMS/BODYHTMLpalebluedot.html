On 14 February 1990, the Voyager 1 spacecraft - already well past Neptune and speeding away from the Sun forever - turned its cameras back toward home one final time. From about 40.5 AU away, Earth showed up as less than a single pixel, an accident of light barely visible inside a scattered ray of sunlight.
