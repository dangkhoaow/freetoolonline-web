Garden Defense so voi Medieval Wall Defense

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Trang nay co hai game thuoc the loai phong thu, va chung duoc xay dung cho nhung phien choi khac nhau. Garden Defense la mot game phong thu theo lan, nhe, mot luot choi duy nhat; Medieval Wall Defense la mot game phong thu tuong nang hon voi tien trinh danh vong. Duoi day la mot cai nhin trung thuc ve nhung gi moi game mang lai, kem so lieu cu the.

Garden Defense tai ngay lap tuc vi toan bo game duoc ve boi chinh JavaScript cua trang, khong co gi them de tai. Medieval Wall Defense tai xuong khoang 631 KB o lan dau (sau do duoc cache) cho mot iframe doc lap voi engine canvas rieng va hieu ung am thanh Web Audio.


Garden Defense thang o diem nao

Mot luot choi Garden Defense ket thuc trong mot phien - ba loai vat can, nam lan song, mot ket qua thang hoac thua ro rang, va lan song tot nhat cua ban duoc luu de lan thu tiep theo co muc tieu. Khong co gi phai hoc ngoai viec can doi giua nang luong va tan cong, dieu nay giup no la lua chon nhanh hon khi ban co nam phut va muon mot game hoan chinh, khong phai mot game mo.


Medieval Wall Defense thang o diem nao

Medieval Wall Defense duoc xay dung de choi qua nhieu luot: vang kiem duoc trong mot luot mua anh hung va ky nang, Diem Nghien Cuu va Vuong Mien tich luy giua cac luot cho phan thuong danh vong, va bon ky nang danh so cong voi mot bang nghien cuu mang lai nhieu do sau hon moi phien so voi ba the cua Garden Defense. No cung co hieu ung am thanh theo thu tuc va dieu khien cham than thien voi dien thoai, trong khi Garden Defense khong co am thanh nao ca.


Quy tac thuc te

Chon Garden Defense khi ban muon mot luot choi hoan chinh nam lan song duy nhat ma khong can hoc gi truoc. Chon Medieval Wall Defense khi ban muon mot phien dai hon voi tien trinh duoc mang sang luot choi tiep theo.

De xem gan hon khi nao Garden Defense phu hop nhat, xem khi nao Garden Defense phu hop.
