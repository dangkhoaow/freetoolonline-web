This page renders a Doliosauriscus as a 3D model you can spin in the browser - drag to rotate, scroll or pinch to zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person beside it for scale, and click the head, a limb, or the tail to read a fact about that part.

Doliosauriscus was a Middle Permian anteosaurid dinocephalian from Russia - a synapsid on the mammal line, not a dinosaur. It lived about 268-260 million years ago, tens of millions of years before the first dinosaurs. Orlov named it Doliosaurus in 1958; Kuhn renamed it Doliosauriscus in 1961 (type species D. yanshinovi). Modern reviews often place the same material in Titanophoneus; this page keeps the familiar Doliosauriscus name and states the synonymy plainly.
