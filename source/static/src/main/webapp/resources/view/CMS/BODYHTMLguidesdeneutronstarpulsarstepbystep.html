Neutron Star Pulsar 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Neutron Star Pulsar 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and press Play spin. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - spin the lighthouse

Click Play spin so the tilted beams sweep; use Slow or Fast to change the readable demo rate.


Step 2 - star facts

Press Star so the panel shows radius about 12 km and mass about 1.4 solar masses.


Step 3 - beams

Press Beam, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while you orbit the dual cones.
