Khi nao viewer Giganotosaurus tren trinh duyet phu hop

Chon viewer Giganotosaurus tren trinh duyet khi ban muon xoay mot trong nhung loai khung long an thit lon nhat da biet, Size vs human o ty le trung thuc khoang 12.5 m trong khoang 12-13 m (toi khoang 13.2 m) da cong bo, va bam su that tren dau, canh tay, duoi hoac chan - khong cai dat gi. Khong gi ve chuyen tham duoc gui len may chu - tot cho laptop lop hoc hoac kiem tra nhanh tren desktop so lieu Formacion Candeleros (99.6-95 Mya; Ruben Dario Carolini gan Villa El Chocon nam 1993; Rodolfo Coria va Leonardo Salgado 1995; holotype MUCPv-Ch1 tai Ernesto Bachmann Municipal Paleontological Museum).


Khi nao ung dung AR dien thoai hoac bao tang phu hop hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi ban can silhouet tren san phong khach; viewer nay khong bao gio khang dinh dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di ben canh xuong Giganotosaurus duoc dung va cam nhan ty le hall ma man hinh khong theo kip - gom Ernesto Bachmann Municipal Paleontological Museum o Villa El Chocon cho ngu canh holotype. Anh tinh chi thang khi ban can mot khung chia se don, khong tuong tac.


So sanh Giganotosaurus nay khong phai la gi

So sanh Giganotosaurus nay la bang trade-off cho viewer da ship - khong phai bang xep hang thuong hieu AR, khong biet tinh nang AR ma trang khong co, khong biet mot can nang chinh xac duy nhat (khoang 4.2-13.8 tan la co chu dich), khong khang dinh Giganotosaurus chac chan lon hon hay nho hon Tyrannosaurus rex, khong biet danh sach moi chac chan, khong biet credit glTF, va khong biet diem so hay trang thai thang. Xem huong dan dieu khien tai cach xem Giganotosaurus trong 3D. Xem chieu dai va khoi luong ben canh nguoi 1.8 m tai so sanh kich thuoc Giganotosaurus.
