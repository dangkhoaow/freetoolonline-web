When to Use Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Open the Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer when you need a hyperbolic flyby assist picture with the ~+10 km/s Voyager / Jupiter literacy figure - and skip it when you only need Hohmann burns or local escape speed.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer, scrub periapsis closer, play the craft, and pause so students see the hyperbola bend while the facts panel keeps ~+10 km/s in view.


Assist literacy in Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer

Assist literacy in Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer stays readable when you keep the prograde velocity arrow and periapsis scrubber paired - the path is a flyby hyperbola, not a closed transfer ellipse.


Sessions Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer does not fit are n-body ephemerides, Voyager SPICE playback, or mission optimizers. Use Hohmann Transfer 3D for two-burn ellipses or Escape Velocity 3D for local launch speeds. Walk the controls in the Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer step-by-step guide.
