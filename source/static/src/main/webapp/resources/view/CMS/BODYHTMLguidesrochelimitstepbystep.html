How to explore Roche Limit 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Roche Limit 3D Explorer, read the ~2.44 R / ~1.26 R figures, and scrub distance until the moon becomes shards inside the active limit.

  1. Open /space-3d/roche-limit.html and wait for the primary, orange fluid ring, and green rigid ring.
  2. Read the facts panel: fluid ~2.44 R, rigid ~1.26 R, distance in primary radii.
  3. Scrub distance from ~3.6 R down toward 2.0 R in fluid mode to cross the orange ring.
  4. Toggle Mode to rigid 1.26 R and compare where shards appear.
  5. Press Play approach to watch an inbound path cross the limit automatically.
