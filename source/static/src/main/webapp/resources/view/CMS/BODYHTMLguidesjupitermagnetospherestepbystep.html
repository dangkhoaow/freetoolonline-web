How to explore Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer

Open the Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer, read the ~420 uT / ~20,000x / past-Saturn figures, and scrub the magnetopause standoff.

  1. Open /space-3d/jupiter-magnetosphere.html and wait for Jupiter, dipole lines, and the orange magnetopause shell.
  2. Read the facts panel: ~420 uT, ~20,000x Earth, magnetotail past Saturn (~9.5 AU), ~45-100 Rj.
  3. Try Quiet ~90 Rj and Compressed ~50 Rj, or scrub Magnetopause Rj.
  4. Toggle magnetotail, Saturn marker, and solar wind.
  5. Compare with Earth Magnetosphere 3D when you need the ~6-10 Re Earth case.
