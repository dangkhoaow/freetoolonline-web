Character Counter Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Character Counter runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Character Counter. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it to check a post against a word limit, estimate reading time for an article, or get a quick character count for a bio field.


Step 3 - get the result

Counting runs locally in this browser tab - no upload and no account.

Nothing you type is saved; refreshing the page clears the box.
