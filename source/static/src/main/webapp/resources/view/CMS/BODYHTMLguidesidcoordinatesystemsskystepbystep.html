Cara menjelajah Celestial Coordinate Systems 3D Explorer

Open the explorer, toggle each grid, and watch how the star-fixed equatorial grid tilts against your observer-fixed horizon.

  1. Open /space-3d/coordinate-systems-sky.html and wait for the celestial sphere.
  2. Read the facts panel: RA/Dec ranges, Alt/Az ranges, and the 23.44 degree obliquity.
  3. Toggle the equatorial (blue) and horizontal (amber) grids to compare them.
  4. Move the latitude slider and watch the celestial pole rise to match your latitude.
  5. Compare with Constellation Sphere 3D for star patterns, or Ecliptic and Zodiac 3D for the Sun path.
