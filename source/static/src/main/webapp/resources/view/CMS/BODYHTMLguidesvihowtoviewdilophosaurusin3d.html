Doc panel facts Dilophosaurus va bam bo phan co the

Panel facts Dilophosaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 6.03 m cua holotype (lon nhat gan khoang 7 m; Size vs human dung khoang 6.03 m), khoi luong khoang 283 kg cua holotype (lon nhat toi khoang 400 kg), cua so 195-184 Mya Ky Jura Som (thuong trich quanh 193 Mya) o Formacion Kayenta, bac Arizona, va kham pha - Samuel Welles mo ta hoa thach nam 1954 nhu loai Megalosaurus, roi tao chi Dilophosaurus ("than lan hai mo") nam 1970. Mot cap mo mong cong chay doc dinh so (xuong nasal va lacrimal), render o day thanh hai vay lech qua rewrite cuc bo feats.crest. Jurassic Park (1993) them beo co bung va phun doc de gay drama - khong cai nao co bang chung hoa thach. Bam ngan - khong keo - vao dau (gom vung mo), canh tay, duoi, hoac chan se hien fact hoa thach ngan; bam cho trong de tro lai to loai. Mau da va lop phu mo chinh xac la phuc dung nghe thuat; mo hinh la theropod procedural chua re-host glTF Dilophosaurus mien phi.
