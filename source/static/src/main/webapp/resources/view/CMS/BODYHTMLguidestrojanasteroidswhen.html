When to use Jupiter Trojan Asteroids 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want to see how asteroids get trapped at Lagrange points, and skip it when you want the main asteroid belt or a moon gravity boundary.

  • Classroom demos of Lagrange points and Trojan swarms
  • Showing why the swarms stay 60 degrees from Jupiter
  • Introducing tadpole libration and the Lucy mission targets

This is an educational visualization with illustrative counts and libration, not a full N-body integration.
