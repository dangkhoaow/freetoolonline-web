When to Use Gravity Well 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Open Gravity Well 3D Explorer when you need to compare Moon / Earth / Jupiter / Sun escape speeds (2.38 / 11.2 / 59.5 / 617.5 km/s) as wells on one sheet - or to read r_s = 2GM/c^2 literacy for the Sun without a textbook plot.

Use it in a classroom warm-up, a homework preview, or a quick check that escape velocity grows sharply from Moon to Sun. Skip it if you need a numeric GR geodesic solver or an accretion-disk movie - that is Black Hole 3D Visualizer territory.

Everything stays on your device: no upload, no account, and the facts panel cites the same published escape figures shown in the scene.
