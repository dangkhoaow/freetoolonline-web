Everyday utilities in your browser

This hub collects small everyday tools that run in your browser: compress a file or folder with the free File Compressor, build a QR code, convert units and epoch timestamps, keep a to-do list, spin a name wheel, flip a coin, or time a countdown and stopwatch. Companion ZIP workflows with password options live on /zip-tools/zip-file.html when you need format choices beyond a quick compress.

Most of these pages keep your inputs on the device - no account and no upload for the common converters, clocks, lists, and pickers - so you can use them for a one-off job and leave. Pick the tool that matches the job below; each page states what it does and what it does not claim.
