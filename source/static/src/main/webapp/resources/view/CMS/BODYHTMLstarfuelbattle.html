Star Fuel Battle is a free browser spaceship dogfight. Fly with WASD, fire with Space, and keep your fuel above zero while you score hits on enemies and planets. About ~0.67 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a different star shooter loop? Try Andromeda Star Shooter. Prefer chili-themed arcade fire? Try Chili Blast Shooter.

STAR FUEL BATTLE

WASD move, Space fire. Fuel starts at 15 and drops each second - grab bottles (max 30). Hit enemies and planets; avoid friends. P pause, M mute. Local ranking uses ftol:starfuelbattle:gameData. Adapted from gd4Ark/star-battle (MIT, 4Ark). About ~0.67 MB.

Keyboard required for flight. Works best on desktop; phone needs a hardware keyboard.

Press Play to load the game.
Ranking scores stay on-device (ftol:starfuelbattle:gameData).
