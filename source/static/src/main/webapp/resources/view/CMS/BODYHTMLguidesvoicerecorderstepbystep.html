Online Voice Recorder Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

Online Voice Recorder runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Online Voice Recorder. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it for a quick screen capture, webcam clip, or voice note without installing an app.


Step 3 - get the result

Recording stays on your device and is never uploaded; the clip downloads straight to you.

The clip is saved as WebM; to re-encode it to MP4, see the related video tools.
