Como explorar Synodic Lunar Month 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch one lunar orbit fall short of one lunar month.

  1. Open /space-3d/synodic-lunar-month.html and wait for the Sun, Earth and Moon.
  2. Note the start: new moon, with the yellow Sun line and blue star line together.
  3. Watch the Moon finish a star-orbit at day 27.3 - the sidereal month.
  4. Keep watching to day 29.5, when the Moon reaches new moon again - the synodic month.
  5. Use the speed slider and reset button; read the facts panel for the 2.2-day gap.
