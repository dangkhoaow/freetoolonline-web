Welcome to Rock Paper Neural - a free browser rock-paper-scissors duel against a learning neural net, with no account or install. After a few rounds the model tries to predict your next pick from your recent history, so repeating the same pattern gets you countered. About ~20 KB after Play, session-only with no localStorage, adapted from Victor Ribeiro's MIT jokenpo. Use cases: a quiet break, noticing your own tells, or comparing tiny client-side AI toys. Privacy note: scores and training stay in this tab only until you refresh. For more free browser games, open the games hub. Related neural-net toys on this site include Bangbang Artillery and the decision puzzle Googol Stopping Game.
