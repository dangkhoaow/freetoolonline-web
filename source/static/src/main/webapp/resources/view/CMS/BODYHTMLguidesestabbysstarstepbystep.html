Como explorar Tabby's Star 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and see the real evidence for yourself.

  1. Open /space-3d/tabbys-star.html and wait for the star to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch the dust cloud sweep across the star, dimming it.
  3. Watch the live blue and red brightness readout in the facts panel as it happens.
  4. Toggle Megastructure on and compare: now both colors dim by the same amount.
  5. Compare the two modes to see which one matches the real evidence.
