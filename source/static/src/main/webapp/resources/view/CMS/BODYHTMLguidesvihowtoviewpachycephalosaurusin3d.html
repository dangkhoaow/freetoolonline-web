Doc bang su that Pachycephalosaurus va bam vom so

Bang su that Pachycephalosaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 4.5 m (Britannica trich den khoang 5 m; Size vs human dung 4.5 m), khoi luong khoang 370-450 kg, vom so day den khoang 22 cm (gap khoang 20 lan tran so phan lon khung long khac), cua so 70-66 Mya Creta muon (Maastrichtian) o vung nay la Montana, South Dakota, Wyoming va Alberta, va lich su dat ten - Charles W. Gilmore dat ten loai kieu P. wyomingensis nam 1931 truoc la loai Troodon; Barnum Brown va Erich Maren Schlaikjer lap chi Pachycephalosaurus nam 1943. Chuc nang vom van tranh luan (hung dau, dung suon, hoac trung bay), va che do an khong chac (chu yeu thuc vat tu rang gai, omnivory han che van tranh luan). Mot cum bam ngan - khong keo - tren vom so, dau, tay, duoi, hoac chan se hien su that hoa thach ngan; bam cho trong de quay lai to loai.
