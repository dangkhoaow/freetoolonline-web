Doc bang su that Plesiosaurus va nhap bo phan co the

Bang liet ke chieu dai khoang 3.5 m theo Natural History Museum (Britannica khoang 4.5 m; EBSCO khoang 3-5 m), khoi luong khoang 90 kg theo so EBSCO bao thu (mot so nguon pho bien den khoang 450 kg), cua so Ky Jura som khoang 199 den 175 trieu nam (Hettangian-Toarcian), rang hinh non khong rang cua phu hop bat ca va moi bien nho, va lich su dat ten - William Conybeare va Henry De la Beche dat ten nam 1821 tu manh vo o Lyme Regis, Dorset, Anh; Mary Anning tim khung gan hoan chinh nam 1823 cung vach da, lan dau lo ke hoach than co dai; mau chinh o Natural History Museum, London (NHMUK PV R.1330). Day la bo sat bien Sauropterygia - khong phai khung long, va khac nhom Mosasaurus da ship (Mosasauria).
