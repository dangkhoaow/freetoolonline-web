About this explorer:

This is an educational visualization, not a physics simulation. Io's volcanoes are plotted at real NASA Juno-era coordinates with actual eruption temperatures (700K to 1700K) and heights from peer-reviewed sources. Sizes and distances are scaled for clarity so you can see multiple volcanoes at once. Click any red hotspot to read each volcano's name, coordinates, discovery source, and mission data. Drag to rotate the view; scroll to zoom in and out.
