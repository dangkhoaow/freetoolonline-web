Cach choi Layer Flip Platformer - Tung buoc

Trang Layer Flip Platformer tai platformer SVG ~44 KB trong iframe. Ban chay, nhay, bam Space de lat lop sang/toi de nen va gai doi cho tren khong. Qua 25 man. Bam Play de bat dau.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

A/D hoac Trai/Phai di chuyen, W hoac Up nhay, Space lat lop dang bat (nut gamepad 1 cung lat). Khong luu gi - dong tab se reset.

Buoc 1 - bam Play va mo menu

Tren trang game, bam Play de chen iframe (~44 KB). Trong khung, menu tieu de co play, controls va editor. Chon play de vao man 1. Engine thich nghi tu ONOFF cua Daniel Marino va Brad Dunbar (js13kGames 2018, MIT); LICENSE va CREDITS di kem.

Buoc 2 - di chuyen va nhay

Di chuyen bang A/D hoac Trai/Phai. Nhay bang W hoac Up (nut gamepad 0 hoac 12 cung nhay). Khong co dieu khien touch trong ban nay - can ban phim hoac gamepad. Space khong nhay; Space lat lop.

Buoc 3 - lat lop tren khong

Bam Space (hoac nut gamepad 1) de doi lop dang bat. Nen va gai cua lop kia xuat hien hoac bien mat de mo duong hoac ne gai khi dang nhay.

Buoc 4 - dem so va man choi

Dem chet tang khi cham gai hoac roi. Dem sao/man theo doi 25 man lam tay. Sau khi chet ban hoi sinh o dau man. Khong co continue localStorage.

Buoc 5 - ket thuc hoac mo controls

Qua het 25 man de ket thuc. Mo man hinh controls tu menu neu can goi y phim. Choi client-side, khong cai, khong tai khoan.
