When to use Roche Lobe Binary 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want the geometry of binary mass transfer, and skip it when you want two stars simply orbiting or the Lagrange points of a planet-moon pair.

  • Introducing novae, cataclysmic variables, and the Algol paradox
