Hash Generator (SHA-256/512) chay qua ba buoc: mo cong cu, go hoac dan van ban, roi chon thuat toan va copy digest. Trang nay di qua tung buoc can gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Vao Hash Generator (SHA-256/512). Khong can cai gi hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - go hoac dan van ban

Go hoac dan van ban ban muon bam hash vao o; roi chon SHA-1, SHA-256, SHA-384, hoac SHA-512 trong menu xo xuong. Digest cap nhat truc tiep ben duoi khi ban go.


Buoc 3 - copy digest

Bam Copy de lay chuoi digest hex ket qua. Viec bam hash chay hoan toan trong trinh duyet qua WebCrypto API - khong co gi duoc gui len server.

MD5 khong duoc cung cap o day vi cac trinh duyet hien dai bo qua no do khong an toan; hay dung cong cu md5-converter lien quan cho thuat toan cu the do.
