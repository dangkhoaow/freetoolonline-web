Frequently Asked Questions

What is Star Fuel Battle?

A free browser spaceship dogfight. Fly, shoot, manage fuel, and chase a local ranking score.

How do I play?

Press Play, click Start game inside the iframe, then use WASD to move and Space to fire.

How does fuel work?

Fuel starts at 15 and drops 1 point per second. Touch falling fuel bottles for +15 (cap 30). At 0 fuel the run ends. Hitting an enemy ship costs 15 fuel on collision.

What about friends and planets?

Hitting a friend ship deducts 10 score (bullets or collision). Planets need two hits and award 10 score. Enemy ships award 5 score per hit. Negative scores are allowed.

Can I pause or mute?

Press P to pause or resume. Press M to mute or unmute. On-screen pause and mute buttons work too.

Does it save scores?

Yes. Local ranking uses the namespaced key ftol:starfuelbattle:gameData in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

How big is the download?

About ~0.67 MB of canvas logic, PNG art, and audio after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from gd4Ark/star-battle (MIT, Copyright (c) 2019 4Ark). This build adds robots noindex and namespaces localStorage.
