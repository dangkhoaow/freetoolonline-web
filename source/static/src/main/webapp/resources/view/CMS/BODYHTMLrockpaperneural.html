Rock Paper Neural is a free browser rock-paper-scissors duel against a small multilayer perceptron that trains on your move history. After a few rounds it tries to predict your next pick. About ~20 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a cannon duel vs a neural net instead? Try Bangbang Artillery. Want an optimal-stopping puzzle? Try Googol Stopping Game.

ROCK PAPER NEURAL

Pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors. A tiny neural net watches your sequence and tries to beat your next move. Keyboard: J / K / L. Adapted from Victor Ribeiro's jokenpo (MIT). About ~20 KB.

Works with click/tap or keyboard. Session-only - no save data.
