When the Deinocheirus browser viewer fits

Pick the browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a giant Mongolian ornithomimosaur with Size vs human at the honest 11 m panel length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for real mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame.


What this Deinocheirus comparison is not

This comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - not an AR brand ranking, no invented AR features, no single mass outside published ranges (panel 6.4 t disclosed), it does not conflate Deinocheirus with smaller ornithomimids such as Gallimimus, and no game score.
