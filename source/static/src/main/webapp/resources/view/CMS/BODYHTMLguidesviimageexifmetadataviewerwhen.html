Image EXIF Metadata Viewer Khi Nao Dung

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Mo Image EXIF Metadata Viewer khi ban can xem the camera, ngay gio va GPS tren anh truoc khi chia se - khong upload file va khong cai ExifTool.


Khi nao hop

Kiem tra anh dien thoai truoc khi dang cong khai. Xac nhan JPEG con giu ngay chup. Phat hien GPS tren anh du lich.Uu tien parse tren may khi file phai o lai thiet bi nay.


Nhung gi se thay

Chon anh, bam Read Metadata, xem Camera, Date / Time, GPS va Other. Neu co GPS, canh bao rieng tu va link OpenStreetMap xuat hien; co the tai JPEG da go metadata. Tat ca trong trinh duyet - khong upload, khong tai khoan.
