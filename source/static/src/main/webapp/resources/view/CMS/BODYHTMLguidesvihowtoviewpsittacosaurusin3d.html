Use the four Psittacosaurus controls under the canvas

Under the Psittacosaurus canvas, four controls handle view and scale:

  • Fullscreen - fills the display while keeping the buttons visible
  • Stop rotation / Auto-rotate - freezes or resumes auto-spin
  • Idle motion - adds subtle body sway; tap again for Stand still
  • Size vs human - shows a 1.8 m person at the honest panel length ratio (2 m)

Read the Psittacosaurus facts panel and click a body part

The Psittacosaurus facts panel lists length about 2 m, height about 0.9 m, weight about 20 kg, era 126-101 Mya Early Cretaceous Asia, herbivore diet, named by Henry Fairfield Osborn in 1923. Early ceratopsian with a beak but no frill or horns in the shipped model features. Distinct from horned ceratopsians like Triceratops or Chasmosaurus on this site. Soft-tissue color is an artistic reconstruction. Optional CC0/PD glTF (Model Magic via Printables 1753562) may swap in. This model is not a fossil-accurate skeleton.
