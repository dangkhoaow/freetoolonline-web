How to explore Kilonova 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the real GW170817 merger and its aftermath.

  1. Open /space-3d/kilonova.html and wait for the two neutron stars to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch them spiral together into a bright merger flash.
  3. Watch the brief gamma-ray jet flash right at the merger moment.
  4. Scrub the Day slider slowly from 0 to 14 and watch the ejecta cloud expand and turn from blue to red.
  5. Toggle the GRB jet off and on, or drag to orbit the scene.
