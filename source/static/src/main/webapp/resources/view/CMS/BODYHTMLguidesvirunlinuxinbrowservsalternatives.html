Cach lua chon

Chon may trinh duyet cho cac cong viec nhanh, an toan, khong can cai dat: luyen tap lenh, thu mot script co rui ro, day hoc hoac bat ky may tinh bi khoa nao ma viec cai dat phan mem khong phai la lua chon - va de di chuyen cong viec giua cac may, vi mot phien duoc xuat thanh mot file duy nhat ma ban co the nhap vao noi khac de tiep tuc chinh xac. Chon may ao tren may tinh de ban khi can toc do, phan mem 64-bit hoac mang; chon USB khoi dong khi muon kiem tra Linux tren phan cung thuc. Them ve muc dich su dung tai khi nao nen chay Linux tren trinh duyet.
