The Mochi and the Midnight Escape page loads a ~52 KB pixel platformer in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, use WASD or arrows to move and jump, collect stars, and reach the exit flag on each of nine levels.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~52 KB). The 320x180 canvas scales to fit the stage.

Step 2 - confirm the title

Press Enter, Space, or a gamepad button on the title and level intro screens.

Step 3 - move and jump

WASD, ZQSD, or arrow keys move Mochi. Up/W jumps. Watch for springs, ziplines, levers, keys, and drones.

Step 4 - collect and exit

Grab stars for score. Touch the finish tile to advance. Progress saves under ftol:mochimidnightescape.
