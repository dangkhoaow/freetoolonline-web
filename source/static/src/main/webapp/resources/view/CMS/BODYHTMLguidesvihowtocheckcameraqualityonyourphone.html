Cach Kiem Tra Chat Luong Camera Dien Thoai Cua Ban

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-04-27. Mo bai kiem tra camera de xac minh camera dien thoai cua ban trong trinh duyet - khong can cai app.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Nam bai kiem tra bat hau het cac van de camera: xac nhan xem truoc truc tiep mo (camera co the chon va trinh duyet co quyen), kiem tra do phan giai so voi thong so ky thuat, lay net van ban o khoang cach canh tay va kiem tra do net, chup mot anh trong anh sang sang va toi, va kiem tra cac dom co the nhin thay tren ong kinh.

Test 1 - camera mo duoc

Che do hong dau tien la "camera khong xuat hien trong trinh duyet". Hai nguyen nhan pho bien: trinh duyet khong co quyen camera, hoac ung dung khac dang giu camera.

Test 2 - do phan giai khop voi thong so

Thong so camera dien thoai duoc quang cao bang megapixel. Mot cam bien 12 MP tao ra anh 4032x3024. Trang test cho thay do phan giai thuc te ma trinh duyet nhan duoc.

Test 3 - lay net van ban

Giu van ban in o khoang cach canh tay. Cham de lay net. Van ban phai sac net trong 0.5 giay. Lay net cham hoac preview mem cho biet autofocus loi hoac ong kinh ban.

De co quy trinh tien-cuoc-goi hoan chinh, xem checklist kiem tra thiet bi cho lam viec tu xa.

Loi phan cung vs. loi phan mem - cach nhan biet

Phan biet loi phan cung va loi phan mem la cau hoi quan trong nhat sau ca nam bai kiem tra. Mot dom co dinh o cung mot goc trong moi buc anh, du ban cam dien thoai the nao, nam trong module camera - day la truong hop bao hanh, khong phai can lau chui. Autofocus that bai tren moi bo mat o moi dieu kien anh sang cho thay dong co bi hong hoac ong kinh bi hu. Hat sau khi cap nhat he dieu hanh, hoac loi focus tu-su-phuc-hoi khi khoi dong lai app camera, cho thay van de xu ly ma nha san xuat co the sua bang phan mem. Pixel-binning la nguyen nhan nham lan pho bien: dien thoai 48 MP thuong chi cap 12 MP (4000x3000) qua API camera cua trinh duyet vi bon pixel vat ly hop thanh mot de tang do nhanh cam sang.
