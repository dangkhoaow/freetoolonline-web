How to explore Tidal Heating 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Tidal Heating 3D Explorer, watch Io flexing hotspots flicker, then toggle the Europa cutaway to reveal its subsurface ocean.

  1. Open /space-3d/tidal-heating.html and wait for Io, Europa, and Ganymede to appear.
  2. Read the facts panel: Io volcano count, surface flex, heat flow, and the interior finding.
  3. Watch the orange hotspots flicker on Io as it flexes.
  4. Press the cutaway button to reveal the blue Europa ocean under its icy shell.
  5. Compare with Orbital Resonance 3D for the period-ratio mechanics, or Galilean Moons 3D for the four-moon overview.
