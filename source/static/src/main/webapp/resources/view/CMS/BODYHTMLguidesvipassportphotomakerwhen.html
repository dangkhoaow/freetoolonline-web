Passport Photo Maker Khi Nao Dung

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Passport Photo Maker dang mo khi ban can dung nhanh mot lan ma khong can cai dat gi. Trang nay noi ro khi nao phu hop va khi nao khong.


Khi nao phu hop

Ket qua duoc ghep tren nen trang tinh, dung kich thuoc pixel cua preset, tinh theo 300 DPI de in; chon "Also generate a 4x6in print sheet" de tai them mot trang in gom nhieu ban, du bao nhieu ban vua voi mot trang.


Nen mong doi gi

Moi thu chay cuc bo qua Canvas API - anh khong bao gio duoc gui len server.

Cong cu nay chi cat va chinh kich thuoc cho dung, tren nen trang - khong xoa hay thay nen co san, khong tu nhan dien hay can giua khuon mat, va khong kiem tra viec dap ung day du yeu cau cua mot co quan cap passport cu the (anh sang, bieu cam, khan/mu tren dau); hay kiem tra quy dinh chinh thuc cua noi ban nop truoc khi gui.
