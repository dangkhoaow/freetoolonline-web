The Quantum Optics Puzzle page loads a ~1.2 MB photon optics puzzle in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, pick a level, place optics tiles, and run photons into the detectors.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~1.2 MB). The #game board boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - pick a level

Open a level from the level list. Progress remembers the current level under ftol:quantumoptics:CurrentLevelId.

Step 3 - place optics

Drag mirrors, polarizers, splitters, glass, and detectors from the stock onto the board.

Step 4 - run and win

Run the simulation. When detectors fire correctly, the level marks won under ftol:quantumoptics:LevelIsWon keys.
