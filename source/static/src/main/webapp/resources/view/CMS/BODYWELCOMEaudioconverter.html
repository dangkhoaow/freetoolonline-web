Audio Converter

Last reviewed: 2026-07-02

Turn an audio file, or the soundtrack of a video, into an MP3 right in the browser. Choose an MP3 bitrate, drop or select your file, and save the result - conversion runs in the tab using FFmpeg.wasm and nothing reaches a server. The working copy is released when you navigate away.

You can start from MP3, WAV, AAC, OGG, FLAC, or M4A audio, or a video whose sound you want to keep; the output is always an MP3. The bitrate dropdown lets you choose 128, 192, 256, or 320 kbps. Your input file is processed locally and discarded when the page closes - nothing is stored or uploaded at any step.
