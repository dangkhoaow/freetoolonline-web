Cong Cu Chuyen JPG Sang WebP Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-06

Cong Cu Chuyen JPG Sang WebP chay theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, cung cap dau vao cua ban, sau do tai xuong ket qua. Trang nay giai thich moi buoc mong doi dieu gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Mo cong cu Cong Cu Chuyen JPG Sang WebP - khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - cung cap dau vao cua ban

Cach dung pho bien: khi nen tang dich cua ban chap nhan WEBP nhung khong chap nhan JPG.


Buoc 3 - nhan ket qua

Ket qua la mot tep WebP co kich thuoc va chat luong duoc dieu khien boi cac tuy chon phia tren o chon tep; gia tri mac dinh phu hop voi cach dung pho bien nhat.

De biet cac dinh dang anh khac, xem trung tam cong cu chuyen doi anh.
