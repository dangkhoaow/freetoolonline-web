Planetary Rings Comparison 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

This explorer lines up all four planetary ring systems: Saturn ~7 major rings of water ice (1655, Huygens); Uranus ~13 dark, carbon-rich rings (1977, occultation); Neptune ~5 rings with partial arcs (1989, Voyager 2); Jupiter a faint dusty ring (1979, Voyager 1).

Saturn Rings 3D is a single-planet deep dive; this page owns the cross-planet comparison.

  • All four ring systems rendered at a compressed scale
  • A pulsing highlight ring marks the selected planet
  • Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune selector buttons
  • Facts panel lists ring count, composition, and discovery year
  • Runs in-browser with three.js - no account, no upload

Saturn's rings are far brighter than the rest; Neptune's are partial arcs, not full rings.
