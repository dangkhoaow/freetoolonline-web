Como jogar Pixel Necromancer

Pixel Necromancer loads about ~27 KB in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Click Play, then click inside the frame to start. Move the pointer to aim and click to cast; press Space to resurrect.

Launch

Click Play on this page, then click inside the iframe when prompted.

Combat

Move the pointer to aim. Click to cast a spell while you have charges.

Resurrect

When the resurrect bar fills, press Space to raise corpses as allies.

Rituals

Between levels, use arrow keys to highlight a ritual and Enter to buy it with souls.
