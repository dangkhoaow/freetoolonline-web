Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer khoi dong ngay khi trang tai xong: keo de quay quanh Mat Troi va vanh dai, cuon de phong to, va nhan Play spin. Moi thu duoc dung tren chinh thiet bi cua ban - khong cai dat, khong tai khoan.


Buoc 1 - tim vanh dai

Tim vong hat tu khoang 2.2 den 3.2 AU. Bang thong tin liet ke pham vi va khoi luong vanh dai gan 3% cua Mat Trang.


Buoc 2 - khe Kirkwood

Nhan Show Kirkwood gaps de cac vong dan huong mau cam danh dau cong huong 3:1, 5:2 va 7:3 gan 2.50, 2.82 va 2.95 AU.


Buoc 3 - Ceres

Giu Highlight Ceres bat de nhan ra vat the xam lon hon va doc ve duong kinh khoang 940 km trong khi che do toan man hinh giu cac dieu khien hien thi.
