So nutzen Sie Solar Granulation 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the Sun surface boil with rising and sinking plasma.

  1. Open /space-3d/solar-granulation.html and wait for the granule field.
  2. Watch bright granules (rising hot plasma) brighten and fade over the dark sinking lanes.
  3. Read the facts panel: granule size, lifetime, flow speed, and supergranule scale.
  4. Toggle the supergranule overlay to see the larger convection pattern.
  5. Pause the boiling at any time.
