Chuyen mot tep tin don le thanh tep .zip chi la mot buoc ngan tai cong cu tao zip-file: tha tep vao bang tai len, bam Zip, va cong cu tra ve mot tep .zip ban co the gui email, dinh kem hoac tai len. Hieu qua nen phu thuoc vao noi dung ben trong tep. Dau vao van ban, ma nguon, JSON va CSV thuong giam 60-80%; tai lieu Office 10-25%; PDF 5-15%; anh da nen san (JPG, PNG, HEIC) chi 0-5% - voi nhung tep do, hay nen bang cong cu anh truoc roi moi zip. Bang "Reduce ZIP file size - what to expect" tren trang cong cu cho thay cach tinh de dat cac muc tieu pho bien nhu gioi han 25 MB cua tep dinh kem email. Voi noi dung nhay cam, mo bang Settings tai cong cu tao va them mat khau truoc khi zip - co ba muc ma hoa (Standard, AES-128, AES-256). Gui mat khau qua mot kenh khac voi tep nen.
