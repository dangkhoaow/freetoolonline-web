Cong Cu Chuyen PNG Sang WebP Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-06

Cong Cu Chuyen PNG Sang WebP chay theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, cung cap dau vao cua ban, sau do tai xuong ket qua. Trang nay giai thich moi buoc mong doi dieu gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Truy cap Cong Cu Chuyen PNG Sang WebP. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - cung cap tep PNG cua ban

Chon mot tep PNG bang bo chon tep tren trang. Cong Cu Chuyen PNG Sang WebP tai hinh anh vao ban xem truoc tren cung trang - khong co gi duoc gui den may chu. Neu trinh duyet cua ban ho tro, mot thanh truot chat luong se xuat hien de ban can bang kich thuoc tep va do trung thuc hinh anh truoc khi chuyen doi.

Cach dung pho bien: khi nen tang dich cua ban chap nhan WEBP nhung khong chap nhan PNG.


Buoc 3 - tai xuong ket qua

Kich thuoc va chat luong dau ra duoc dieu khien boi cac tuy chon phia tren o chon tep; gia tri mac dinh phu hop voi cach dung pho bien nhat. Nhan Chuyen doi, sau do dung lien ket tai xuong de luu tep WebP vao thiet bi cua ban.

De huong dan khi nao nen chuyen PNG sang WebP, xem Cong Cu Chuyen PNG Sang WebP - khi nao nen dung.

De biet cac dinh dang anh khac, xem trung tam cong cu chuyen doi anh.
