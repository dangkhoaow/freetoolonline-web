When to Use Globular Star Swarm 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Open the Globular Star Swarm 3D Explorer when you need a dense ancient star-swarm lesson with published census and size figures - without an N-body stellar dynamics lab.


A star swarm scale lesson

A scale lesson starts when you scrub from about 10,000 stars toward ~10 million and from about 50 ly toward 450 ly while the facts panel keeps the published ranges.


Sessions this explorer does not fit

Sessions this page does not fit are galaxy-wide structure or stick-figure constellation lore. Use the Milky Way Map 3D Explorer or the Constellation Sphere 3D Explorer. Walk the controls in the step-by-step guide.
