Jwt Decoder Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

JWT Decoder hoat dong theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, dan token, roi doc ket qua da giai ma. Trang nay giai thich tung buoc can gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Truy cap JWT Decoder. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - dan token

Dan toan bo token - ba phan cach nhau boi dau cham (header, payload, signature). Mot token thieu mot phan, hoac co mot phan khong phai JSON base64url hop le, se bi tu choi voi thong bao ro rang thay vi bi loi.


Buoc 3 - lay ket qua

Ket qua hien thi header va payload duoi dang JSON da dinh dang. Neu payload co exp hoac iat, chung se hien thi duoi dang timestamp de doc, va mot exp da qua han se duoc danh dau EXPIRED.

Viec giai ma chay cuc bo ngay trong tab trinh duyet nay - khong can tai len va khong can tai khoan.
