Swing Block Tower spielen - Schritt fuer Schritt

The Swing Block Tower page loads a ~2.0 MB canvas stacking game in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, tap Start in the game, then tap or click to drop each swinging block onto the tower.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~2.0 MB). The canvas scales to your screen.

Step 2 - tap Start

Inside the iframe, tap the Start button on the landing screen to begin the run.

Step 3 - time each drop

Watch the block swing on the rope. Tap, click, or touch to release when it lines up with the stack below.

Step 4 - chase score

Perfect landings add score. Three missed drops (empty hearts) trigger game over with your final tally.
