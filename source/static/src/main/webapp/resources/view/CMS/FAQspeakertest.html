Does this use my microphone?

No. The tone is generated with the Web Audio API. Nothing is recorded and no microphone permission is requested.

Why do I need to click Play?

Browsers block autoplay of audio until you interact with the page. A click on Play Left, Play Right, or Play Both starts the AudioContext.

How do I tell left from right?

Use headphones. Play Left should be louder in the left ear; Play Right in the right ear. Play Both sends the same tone to both channels.
