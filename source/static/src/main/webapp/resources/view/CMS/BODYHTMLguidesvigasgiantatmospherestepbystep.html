Gas Giant Atmosphere 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Gas Giant Atmosphere 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and press Play spin. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - spin the bands

Click Play spin so belts and zones turn on the educational beat near Jupiter's about 9h55m rotation.


Step 2 - atmosphere facts

Press Bands so the panel shows wind order 100-150 m/s against the cloud decks.


Step 3 - Great Red Spot

Press Great Red Spot, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while you orbit the ~16,000 km oval.
