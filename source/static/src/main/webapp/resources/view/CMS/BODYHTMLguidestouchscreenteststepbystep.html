Touchscreen Test Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Touchscreen Test takes three short steps: open the page, enable listening, then touch the pad.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Touchscreen Test. No install and no login.


Step 2 - enable listening

Click Enable touch listening (or touch/click the pad once). The pad border turns active and the status line shows Listening.


Step 3 - touch and move

Touch with one or more fingers - dots mark contacts. Move to bump the move counter. Use Reset counters to start over. Nothing left the tab.
