Frequently Asked Questions

How big was Gryponyx?

About 5 m long. Mass is poorly constrained; one secondary estimate is about 485 kg. Turn on the human figure for scale.

Is Gryponyx the same as Massospondylus?

Older papers synonymized them. Later work (Vasconcelos and Yates) argued Gryponyx is distinctive enough to keep as its own genus. This page treats it as Gryponyx and notes the history.

When and where did it live?

Early Jurassic South Africa, about 201-190 million years ago (Upper Elliot Formation). Named by Broom in 1911.

What does the name mean?

Gryponyx means hooked claw. The species name africanus refers to Africa, where the holotype was found.

Is the model scientifically accurate?

Length follows published estimates, but skin color and soft tissue are artistic. This is not a fossil-accurate skeleton.

Do I need to install anything to view it?

No. The model renders in your browser with WebGL - no app, no account, and nothing about your visit is sent to a server. When available, a free-licensed glTF model may swap in after first paint.
