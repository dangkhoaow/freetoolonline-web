How to Use Aurora 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-15

Raise teaching Kp, watch the oval expand equatorward, and read green 557.7 nm / red 630.0 nm altitudes in the facts panel.

  1. Open Aurora 3D Explorer and wait for Earth plus the glowing oval to paint.
  2. Drag to orbit the night side; keep the facts panel visible.
  3. Press Kp + / Kp - and watch the oval move toward lower teaching latitudes.
  4. Pause spin to compare green (~90-150 km), red (~200-300 km), and purple lower-fringe bands.
  5. Reset to Kp 3 and repeat so the emission altitudes stay the takeaway.
