Cach Choi Violence Town - Tung Buoc

Trang Violence Town tai RPG 2D theo luot ~1.1 MB trong iframe. Ban giao burger cho Borgir, kham pha tam vung overworld kem noi that, danh nhau bang banh xe hanh dong radial, va autosave cuc bo. Nhan Play de bat dau.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

WASD hoac mui ten di chuyen (giu de tiep tuc); cham/click dat de di duong; Space mo banh xe hanh dong; 1-9 tui do; T cho; Esc huy; ? tro giup; L log; E kiem tra; Tab Remoticon. Autosave vao ftol:violencetown:save; cai dat dung ftol:violencetown:settings.

Buoc 1 - nhan Play va bat dau hoac tiep tuc

Nhan Play tren trang game de nap iframe (~1.1 MB). Tren splash, nhan GAME START hoac Space cho van choi moi, hoac CONTINUE khi da co save theo namespace. Engine la Violencetown cua Caelan Gander (MIT); LICENSE va CREDITS nam canh file game.

Buoc 2 - di chuyen va tuong tac

Di mot o bang WASD hoac mui ten (giu de tiep) hoac cham/click dat de di quanh nha. Cham/click muc tieu de di den va lam viec ro rang (Take / Talk / Attack). Long-press, click phai, hoac F mo danh sach dong tu day du cho doi tuong truoc mat.

Buoc 3 - danh bang banh xe hanh dong

Nhan Space o bat ky dau de mo banh xe radial da nham san ke thu gan nhat. Nhan doi Space lap lai hanh dong cuoi. Tren banh xe, Len/Xuong chon vong (hanh dong / item / huong), Trai/Phai xoay, Space ban, Esc quay lai. Tren cam ung, cham ✦ de mo banh xe.

Buoc 4 - tui do va Remoticon

Nhan 1 den 9 de chon o hotbar; Space mo Use / Smash / Give (nem nam tren banh xe). Tab mo Remoticon (ITEMS / GEAR / QUESTS / MAP); [ ] doi tab; C / J / M nhay toi GEAR / QUESTS / MAP. Tren cam ung, cham ▤ cho Remoticon.

Buoc 5 - luu, tuy chon va vung

Autosave ghi vao ftol:violencetown:save voi khoa backup va staging. Tuy chon (nhac, SFX, tat tieng, reduce motion, toan man hinh, che do mo banh xe) luu duoi ftol:violencetown:settings. Tam vung overworld (Town, Downtown, Factory, Sewer, Carnival, Graveyard, Wilderness, Canyon) noi tren ban do the gioi.
