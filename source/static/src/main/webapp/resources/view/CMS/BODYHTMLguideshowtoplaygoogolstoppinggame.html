How to Play Googol Stopping Game - Step by Step

The Googol Stopping Game page loads an ~8 KB optimal-stopping puzzle in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Press Play, click numbered tiles to reveal huge values, then press I'm done when you think you hold the biggest.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~8 KB). The board boots with fifty numbered tiles, a Plays counter, a history log, Computer, and I'm done.

Step 2 - reveal tiles

Click or tap a number button. The hidden value appears in the history log (current pick highlighted). Each tile can be used once.

Step 3 - decide to stop

Your last revealed value is your choice. Press I'm done when you believe it is the biggest of all fifty. You cannot return to an earlier pick.

Step 4 - optional Computer demo

Press Computer to watch a reset round that samples about n/e tiles, then keeps picking until it beats that sample threshold.
