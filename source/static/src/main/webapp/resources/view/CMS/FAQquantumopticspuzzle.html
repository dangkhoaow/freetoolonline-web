Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quantum Optics Puzzle?

A free browser photon puzzle. Guide photons with mirrors, polarizers, splitters, and detectors through optics levels.

How do I play?

Press Play, choose a level, drag optics tiles onto the board, then run the simulation so photons reach the detectors.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Level progress, win flags, and the current level id use ftol:quantumoptics:* keys in this browser.

How big is the download?

About ~1.2 MB of HTML, CSS, JS, sounds, and level data after you press Play.

Is this the same as Quantum Shift?

No. Quantum Optics Puzzle is a photon optics board with polarizers and detectors. Quantum Shift is a different quantum-flavored puzzle on this site.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from stared/quantum-game (MIT). Analytics and Google Fonts were removed for a same-origin build.
