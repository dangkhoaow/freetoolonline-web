Fast Radio Bursts 3D Explorer vs alternatives

Fast Radio Bursts 3D Explorer animates the millisecond pulse and its frequency-vs-time dispersion sweep for three published sources side by side in the browser - a public FRB catalogue wins for browsing hundreds of additional cataloged bursts; a textbook diagram wins for a fixed printed reference.
