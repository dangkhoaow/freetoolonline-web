Opens with the wheel pre-set to Yes and No; type your own list, one entry per line, then click Spin.

How it works:

  1. The wheel loads already split into two slices, Yes and No, ready to spin.
  2. Replace the two entries with your own list if you want more than a yes/no result - one entry per line.
  3. Click Spin; the wheel splits into equal slices, one per entry, and spins to a stop.
  4. The entry under the pointer when the wheel stops is the winner - edit the list and spin again for another round.
