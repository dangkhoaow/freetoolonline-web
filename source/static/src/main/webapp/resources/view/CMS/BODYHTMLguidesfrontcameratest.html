Front Camera Test

Want to confirm your selfie camera works before a call or photo? Open the camera test and click Start Camera to grant browser permission; a live preview appears with the resolution your device reports. If it opens on the back lens, press Switch to select the front camera and watch yourself appear - your front camera passing the test.

Implementing tool/camera-test.html

This is a permission, preview, and capture check, not a video recorder. After Start Camera grants access, the live stream runs inside the browser tab and the resolution readout names what the operating system reports for the lens. Use Switch to move between the front and back cameras so you can verify the selfie lens specifically. Flip mirrors the preview, and Filters change only the live view. When the front camera looks right, press Capture to save a single JPEG you can Edit or Download. No frames leave your device.
