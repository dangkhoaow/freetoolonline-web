Loan Calculator Step By Step

Loan Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, enter your numbers, then read the result. This page walks through what each step expects.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

freetoolonline.com Editorial Team

Quick answer. Open Loan Calculator, enter the loan amount, interest rate, and term, and read the monthly payment - it updates as you type.

Step 1 - open the tool

Open Loan Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.

Step 2 - enter your numbers

Enter the loan amount, the annual interest rate as a percentage, and the term in years.

Step 3 - get the result

The monthly payment, total paid, and total interest update as you type - there is no button to click for the main result. Select "Show first 12 months" to see how the first year of payments splits between principal and interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to use it?

No. The tool runs directly in the browser, with no install or login.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. The numbers you enter stay on your device and are never sent to a server or an analytics service.

Does the result include taxes or fees?

No. The result is illustrative math only - it does not include taxes, insurance, or lender fees specific to your loan.
