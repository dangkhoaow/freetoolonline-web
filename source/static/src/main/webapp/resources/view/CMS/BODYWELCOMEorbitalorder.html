Welcome to Orbital Order - a free browser Aufbau electron puzzle with no account or install. Move the cursor to attract blue s-electrons and repel orange p-electrons, then guide each particle into an empty orbital without double-hitting a filled shell. Climb six elements from hydrogen through oxygen while procedural audio plays after the first click. About ~32 KB loads after you press Play. Best time and tutorial progress stay on-device under ftol:orbitalorder keys. Nothing is uploaded. For more free browser games, open the games hub.
