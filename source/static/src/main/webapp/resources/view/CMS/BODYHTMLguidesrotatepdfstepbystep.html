Rotate Pdf Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Rotate PDF runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Rotate PDF. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

The angle applies uniformly to the whole document; it does not support rotating individual pages to different angles.


Step 3 - get the result

The file is processed in this browser tab and is never uploaded to a server.

To split, merge, or compress the PDF instead, see the related-tools section.
