When to Play Head Soccer Arena

Head Soccer Arena fits short physics duels where you want keyboard head-soccer, 1P or hotseat 2P, power-ups, and local bests - about ~88 KB after Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Quick pitch breaks

One match to five goals or a 90-second timer is a focused duel. Restart with R and chase a cleaner win without leaving the tab.

Keyboard practice

Players need A/D/W/S/Q and arrow keys. Use a desktop or laptop keyboard; phones load but lack on-screen controls.

When to pick another game

Want paddle rallies? Use Mono Paddle Duel. Want a minefield puzzle? Use Mono Minefield Grid.
