Doc bang facts Allosaurus va bam cac phan

Bang facts Allosaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 8.5 m trung binh (lon nhat xac nhan gan 9.7 m), chieu cao hong khoang 3 m (khoang cong bo khoang 2.9-4.9 m), trong luong khoang 1.5-2.7 tan, cua so 155-143 Mya Jura muon (Oxfordian-Tithonian) chu yeu o Formacion Morrison mien tay Hoa Ky, va discovery - Othniel Charles Marsh dat ten chi nam 1877 o Garden Park, Colorado. Nam 2023 ICZN chi dinh mau Smithsonian USNM 4734 la neotype cua loai. Gai xuong nho (sung le) nam tren moi mat, va moi ban tay co ba ngon vuot - doi lap voi canh tay hai ngon rut gon cua Tyrannosaurus rex.
