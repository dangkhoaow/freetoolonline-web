Text Case Converter Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16
freetoolonline.com Editorial Team

Text Case Converter runs in three steps: open the tool, paste your text and pick a case, then copy the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Text Case Converter. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - paste your text and pick a case

Paste or type your text into the input box, then click one of the seven case buttons: UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, snake_case, or kebab-case.


Step 3 - copy the result

The converted text appears immediately in the read-only box below the buttons. Select it and copy it - nothing is uploaded, and no file is created, so this box is the only place the result exists.

Want a different case afterward? Click another case button on the same input and the output updates instantly - no need to re-paste your text.
