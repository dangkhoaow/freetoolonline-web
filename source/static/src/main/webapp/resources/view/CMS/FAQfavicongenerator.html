Frequently Asked Questions

Is my image uploaded?

No. Resizing runs in this tab with canvas. Nothing is sent to a server.

What sizes are created?

PNG previews at 16, 32, 48, 180, 192, and 512, plus a favicon.ico packing 16, 32, and 48 as PNG-in-ICO.

How is the square crop chosen?

Each size uses a cover crop centered on the image so the icon fills the frame.

Can it remove the background?

No. Background removal and text-to-icon generation are not offered.
