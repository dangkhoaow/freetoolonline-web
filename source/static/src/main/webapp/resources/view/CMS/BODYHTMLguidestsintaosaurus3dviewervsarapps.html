When the Tsintaosaurus browser viewer fits

Pick the Tsintaosaurus browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a Shandong Late Cretaceous upright-crest lambeosaurine with Size vs human at the honest 10 m length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything. Nothing about the visit is sent to a server.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your living-room floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for walking beside Late Cretaceous hadrosaur mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame with no interactivity.


What this Tsintaosaurus comparison is not

This Tsintaosaurus comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - it is not a ranking of commercial AR brands, it does not invent AR features the page does not have, it does not invent one precise length or weight beyond the disclosed about 8.3-12 m / 2.5-3 t ranges, it does not claim the optional glTF is a scientifically exact reconstruction, and it does not invent game score or win states.
