Wow! Signal 3D Explorer vs alternatives

This explorer covers a single 1977 radio burst recorded by a fixed-beam telescope. Tabby's Star 3D and 'Oumuamua 3D cover other real, still-unexplained observations, but from optical astronomy rather than radio/SETI.

OptionStrengthAccessTrade-off
Wow! Signal 3D (this page)The real 1977 recorded intensity codeFree, in browserSimplified dish and beam geometry, not a telescope simulator
Tabby's Star 3D siblingUnexplained dimming of a real starFree webOptical photometry, not a radio signal
