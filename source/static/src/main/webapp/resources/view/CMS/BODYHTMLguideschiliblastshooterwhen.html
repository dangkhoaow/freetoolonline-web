When to Play Chili Blast Shooter in the Browser

Open Chili Blast Shooter when you want a vertical arcade round without installing anything - the whole game is about 26 KB and starts after Play plus Enter.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-09

30-second answer. Use this page for a quick keyboard shooter break on a school or office machine where installs are blocked, or when you only need one short wave session with a local high score.

It fits a desktop or laptop with a keyboard. Phones can load the iframe but cannot move because there are no touch movement controls.

Skip it when you need touch controls, cloud saves, or a full commercial vertical STG with hours of stages and meta-progression - this is a lightweight wave loop with only a localStorage high score.
