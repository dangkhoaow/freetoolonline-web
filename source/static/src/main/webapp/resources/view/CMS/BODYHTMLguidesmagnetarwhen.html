When to Use Magnetar 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Open the Magnetar 3D Explorer when you need an extreme magnetic-field strength lesson with published NASA Chandra / Swift figures - without a magnetohydrodynamics lab.


A field-strength comparison lesson

A field-strength lesson starts when you scrub from Earth (~1 G) and fridge (~100 G) up through ordinary pulsars to magnetar (~10^14-10^15 G) while the facts panel keeps the published ranges.


Sessions this explorer does not fit

Sessions this page does not fit are lighthouse-beam rotation mechanics alone. Use the Neutron Star Pulsar 3D Explorer for that. Walk the controls in the step-by-step guide.
