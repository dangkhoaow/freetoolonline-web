Mo Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer khi ban can buoi lop hoac khoang to mo ve vanh dai chinh giua Sao Hoa va Sao Moc - vong khoang Kirkwood, diem nhan Ceres, va khoi luong vanh dai gan 3% Mat Trang - ma khong gia nhu day la mo hinh N vat the.


Demo lop nam phut

Demo lop nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer, bam Show Kirkwood gaps, va tam dung quay de hoc sinh thay cho trong gan 2.50, 2.82 va 2.95 AU - nhanh hon mot hinh sach phang don le.


Ngu canh Ceres va khoi luong

Ngu canh Ceres va khoi luong de doc khi Highlight Ceres van bat trong khi bang liet ke duong kinh khoang 940 km va khoi luong vanh dai khoang 3% Mat Trang. So luong va kich thuoc hat duoc nen cho trinh duyet, nen dam hat la phac thao cau truc, khong phai danh sach moi tieu hanh tinh co ten hay NEO.


Buoi ma Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Buoi ma Asteroid Belt 3D Explorer khong phu hop la tour chi cac hanh tinh, duoi sao choi tren elip lech tam, hoac khoa spin-quy dao Mat Trang. Hay dung Solar System 3D Explorer, Comet Orbit 3D Explorer, hoac Tidal Locking 3D Explorer thay the. Trang vanh dai cung can WebGL trong trinh duyet va khong dat ephemeris truc tiep cho dem nay.
