The House Point and Click is a free browser point-and-click house adventure. Click hotspots, collect inventory items, talk through dialogue, and move through linked rooms until you find a way out. Progress saves under ftol:thehousepointclick keys. About ~18 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a physics idle loop instead? Try Particle Clicker. Prefer a shorter escape room? Try Mochi Midnight Escape.

THE HOUSE POINT AND CLICK

Click rooms and objects, keep useful items in your inventory, and follow the story through linked scenes. Adapted from arturkot/the-house-game (MIT). Progress uses ftol:thehousepointclick:* keys. About ~18 MB.

Mouse works best. Touch can tap hotspots. First load is larger because rooms, images, and sound ship together.

Press Play to load the game.
Inventory and scene progress stay on this device under ftol:thehousepointclick keys.
