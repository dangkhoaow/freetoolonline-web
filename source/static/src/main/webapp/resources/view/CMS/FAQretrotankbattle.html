Frequently Asked Questions

What does Retro Tank Battle do?

Drive your tank with the arrows or WASD, fire with Space, and clear the wave of 6 enemy tanks before they destroy your base or your 3 lives run out.

What is the difference between brick and steel walls?

The arena is a 13x13 tile map with brick walls your shells break through and steel blocks that stop everything - carve your own firing lanes.

Where is my best score saved?

Your best destroyed-tanks count is saved in this browser and shown in the score line - no account, no leaderboard.
