Cong Cu Chuyen WebP Sang JPG Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-09

Cong Cu Chuyen WebP Sang JPG chay theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, cung cap dau vao cua ban, sau do tai xuong ket qua. Trang nay giai thich moi buoc mong doi dieu gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Truy cap Cong Cu Chuyen WebP Sang JPG. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - cung cap dau vao cua ban

Cach dung pho bien: khi nen tang dich cua ban chap nhan JPG nhung khong chap nhan WEBP.


Buoc 3 - nhan ket qua

Kich thuoc va chat luong dau ra duoc dieu khien boi cac tuy chon phia tren o chon tep; gia tri mac dinh phu hop voi cach dung pho bien nhat.

Neu ban can chieu nguoc lai, hay xem phan cong cu lien quan cho cong cu chuyen JPG sang WebP.
