Como explorar Coriolis Effect 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch a parcel curve as the planet spins beneath it.

  1. Open /space-3d/coriolis-effect.html and wait for the spinning ground.
  2. Watch the orange parcel launch and bend away from the grey straight line.
  3. See it curve to the right - that is the Northern Hemisphere.
  4. Toggle to Southern and watch the bend flip to the left.
  5. Replay the launch and read the facts panel for f = 2 Omega sin(latitude).
