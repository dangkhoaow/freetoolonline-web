Cat Typing Race vs alternatif

Cat Typing Race loads a ~28 KB typing duel in an iframe. Press keyboard A-Z to match letters and race the AI cat. Ten levels with three rounds each; bomb, rocket, and snowflake perks.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Click the canvas after Play so keys register. Press J to continue dialogs. Engine MIT (cemalgnlts/js13k-typing-test, JS13K 2025).
