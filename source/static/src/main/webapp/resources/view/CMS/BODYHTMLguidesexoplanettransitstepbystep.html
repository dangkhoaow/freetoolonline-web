Exoplanet Transit 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

The Exoplanet Transit 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit the star and planet, scroll to zoom, and press Play orbit. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - pick a depth preset

Click Jupiter depth for about 1% dip or Earth depth for about 0.0084%. The panel table updates with the published values.


Step 2 - scrub mid-transit

Drag the orbit phase slider until the planet crosses the star face and the star yellow dims. Read Planet in transit in the panel heading.


Step 3 - TRAPPIST-1 note

Press Show TRAPPIST-1 note to add seven-planet catalog periods from 1.5 to 19 days while fullscreen keeps controls visible.
