Seasons Earth 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Seasons Earth 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit the Sun and Earth, scroll to zoom, and press Play year. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - find the tilt

Look for the orange axis and 23.44 deg obliquity in the panel. Tropic guides mark Cancer and Capricorn.


Step 2 - jump solstices

Click Jun solstice then Dec solstice so the yellow subsolar marker reaches about +/-23.44 deg.


Step 3 - scrub the year

Drag Day of year through equinoxes near 0 deg declination, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while you orbit the view.
