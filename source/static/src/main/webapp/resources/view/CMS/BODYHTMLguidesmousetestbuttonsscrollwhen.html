Mouse Test (Buttons & Scroll) When To Use

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Open Mouse Test (Buttons & Scroll) when you need a quick check that left, middle, and right clicks register, or that the scroll wheel still reports movement - without installing a desktop utility.


When it fits

A new mouse feels wrong on one button. A laptop trackpad click is flaky. You want to confirm wheel direction before a long editing session. You prefer an in-tab check so no driver pack or account is required.


What to expect

Enable listening, move and click on the pad, and read coordinates plus button and wheel counters. Reset clears the session. Nothing is uploaded.
