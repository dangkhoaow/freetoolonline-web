How to Play Head Soccer Arena - Step by Step

The Head Soccer Arena page loads a ~88 KB head-soccer duel in an iframe. Jump, kick, score goals, and win by goals or time. Press Play to start.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

P1: A/D move, W jump, S kick, Q power shot; P2: arrows + /; Space pause; R restart; Esc menu. Scores: ftol:headsoccerarena:storage.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~88 KB). The engine auto-opens head soccer. Upstream is the Cursor Arcade soccer module by flancast90 (MIT).

Step 2 - move and kick

In 1P, A/D and W/S/Q control your head while the CPU plays the other side. Kick the ball into the opponent goal. The crossbar bounces shots. Fill the power bar for a rocket shot with Q or /.

Step 3 - modes and match rules

Open Settings: 1P or 2P, match length (60/90/120/180 s), and goals to win (3/5/7/10, default 5). Changing mode restarts the match.

Step 4 - power-ups

Pick up dropped F (fire kick), I (freeze opponent), G (giant head), or M (multi-ball). Each buffs your next kick or yourself.

Step 5 - pause and scores

Space pauses. R restarts. Esc returns to the one-game menu. Wins update high scores under ftol:headsoccerarena:storage.
