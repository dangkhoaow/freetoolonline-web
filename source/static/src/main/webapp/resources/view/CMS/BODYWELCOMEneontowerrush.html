Neon Tower Rush

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play and a full tower-defense session loads in the tab. Draft towers before each map, place them on grass tiles, spend gold from kills on upgrades, and survive 30 waves including summoners and a final boss.

Flow at a glance:

StepAction
DraftPick 1-7 towers from the pre-match pool
MapChoose one of six paths with different modifiers
BuildClick or tap grass to place towers; open tower panel to upgrade
WavesPress Start Next Wave; early starts add speed score
WinDefeat the wave-30 boss before base health hits zero

Audio volume and game preferences save locally under ftol:neontowerrush:* keys only. Adapted from Ooxygen7's open-source TowerRush (MIT); online leaderboard was removed for static hosting. Credits and license ship next to the game files.
