Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Mo Online Voice Recorder. Khong can cai dat hoac dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.

Buoc 2 - cung cap dau vao

Chon nguon ghi: man hinh, camera hoac micro. Dung de chup man hinh nhanh, ghi clip webcam hoac ghi chu giong noi ma khong can cai dat ung dung.

Buoc 3 - lay ket qua

Nhan Stop de hoan thanh. Ban ghi luon tren thiet bi cua ban va khong bao gio duoc tai len; file tai xuong truc tiep cho ban.

File duoc luu o dinh dang WebM; de chuyen sang MP4, xem cac cong cu video lien quan.
