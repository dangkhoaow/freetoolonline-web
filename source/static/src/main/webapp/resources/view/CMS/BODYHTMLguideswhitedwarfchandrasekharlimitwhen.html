When to Use White Dwarf Chandrasekhar Limit 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Open the White Dwarf Chandrasekhar Limit 3D Explorer when you need a white-dwarf mass-ceiling lesson with published Chandrasekhar / NASA Chandra figures - without a stellar-structure lab.


A mass-radius ceiling lesson

A ceiling lesson starts when you scrub mass toward ~1.44 Msun, watch radius shrink toward Earth-sized, and keep the density order (~1 tonne/cm^3) in the facts panel.


Sessions this explorer does not fit

Sessions this page does not fit are broad stellar evolution tours alone. Use the Star Lifecycle 3D Explorer for that. Walk the controls in the step-by-step guide.
