Miami Mice is a free browser beat-em-up with bone-animated feline fighters. Move with WASD, punch with H, kick with J, and block with B. Clear waves in Story mode or survive Horde mode. About ~0.11 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a pixel platformer instead? Try Mochi Midnight Escape. Prefer a short reflex arcade? Try Bounce Back.

MIAMI MICE

Procedural canvas beat-em-up: WASD move, H punch, J kick, B block. Story and Horde modes. Gamepad supported. Adapted from DerBenniBanni/js13k2025_miami_mice (MIT). About ~0.11 MB.

Works on phone and desktop. After load, press START or HORDE MODE on the title screen.

Press Play to load the game.
No cloud saves - session play only.
