How to Explore Globular Star Swarm 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Globular Star Swarm 3D Explorer, scrub star count and diameter, and read the census, age, and size figures.

  1. Open /space-3d/globular-cluster.html and wait for the star swarm to paint.
  2. Drag Star count toward ~10 million and note the denser teaching core.
  3. Drag Diameter toward 450 ly and watch the halo ring expand.
  4. Reset, then try ~10,000 stars and ~50 ly for a compact teaching case.
  5. Read the facts panel for ~150 known star swarms, 11-13 Gyr ages, 50-450 ly, and 10,000 to ~10 million stars.
