How to Play Egg Time Rewind - Step by Step

The Egg Time Rewind page loads a ~60 KB Nokia-style shooter in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Press Play, move with WASD, shoot with Space, and let parallel lives help after you die.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~60 KB). An 84x48 pixel canvas boots in Nokia green.

Step 2 - move and shoot

WASD / arrows / mouse / gamepad to move. Space, Shift, click, or gamepad button to shoot.

Step 3 - break eggs and die smart

Kills are 1 point. The space egg is 50. When you die, time rewinds and prior lives stay as helpers.

Step 4 - reset or chase high score

Press R to reset. High score saves under ftol:eggtimerewind:highScore in this browser only.
