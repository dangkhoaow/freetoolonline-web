How to Use Sidereal vs Solar Day 3D Explorer - Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-15

Play one solar day, watch the orange observer against the blue star line and gold Sun line, then read ~23h 56m 04s vs 24h (~3m 56s) in the facts panel.

  1. Open Sidereal vs Solar Day 3D Explorer and wait for Earth, Sun, and the distant star to paint.
  2. Drag to orbit, keep the facts panel visible, and note mean sidereal vs solar day lengths.
  3. Use Play / Pause and +5% day to scrub through one solar day of teaching time.
  4. Toggle Star only / Sun only / Both lines to isolate each definition.
  5. Reset and repeat so the ~3m 56s gap stays the reliable takeaway.
