Layer Flip Platformer is a dimension-toggle platformer: move with A/D or Left/Right, jump with W or Up, and press Space (or gamepad button) to flip the active layer so platforms and spikes swap in or out. Clear 25 hand-crafted stages. About ~44 KB. Keyboard preferred; gamepad supported. No touch controls.
