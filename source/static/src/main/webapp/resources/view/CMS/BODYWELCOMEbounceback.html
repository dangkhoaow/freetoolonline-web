Welcome to Bounce Back - a free browser boomerang roguelite you can play without an account or install. Press Play to load the ~120 KB game in a same-origin iframe. Move with WASD, aim and throw with the mouse, dash with Space, and keep coins after death for shop upgrades. Privacy note: save keys stay on this device under ftol:bounceback:*; nothing else leaves this device except the initial page and game files from this site.
