A fast-paced canvas tower-defense game in the browser tab: draft up to seven towers, pick one of six maps, survive 30 procedural waves, and upgrade towers to EX forms with signature skills. No install, no account, audio and preferences save on your device.

NEON TOWER RUSH

Draft towers, defend the path, and beat the wave-30 boss. Mouse or touch to build and upgrade; landscape works best on phones. About ~1.2 MB loads when you press Play. In-game UI is Chinese; controls are icon-driven.

Press Play to start the game.
Landscape recommended on phones; pause and settings are inside the game HUD.
