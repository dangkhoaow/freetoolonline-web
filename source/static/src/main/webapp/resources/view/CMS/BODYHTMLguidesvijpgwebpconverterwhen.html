Cong Cu Chuyen JPG Sang WebP Khi Nao Nen Dung

Last reviewed: 2026-07-06

Cong Cu Chuyen JPG Sang WebP dang de mo khi ban can mot lan chay nhanh, don le ma khong can cai dat gi. Trang nay noi ve khi nao phu hop va khi nao khong.


Khi nao phu hop

Cach dung pho bien: khi nen tang dich cua ban chap nhan WEBP nhung khong chap nhan JPG.


Nen mong doi dieu gi

Mong doi mot tep WebP co kich thuoc va chat luong dau ra duoc dieu khien boi cac tuy chon phia tren o chon tep; gia tri mac dinh phu hop voi cach dung pho bien nhat.

De biet cac dinh dang anh khac, xem trung tam cong cu chuyen doi anh.
