Lagrange Points 3D Explorer Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Lagrange Points 3D Explorer khoi dong ngay khi trang tai xong: keo de quay quanh, cuon de phong to, va nhan Play orbit. Moi thu duoc dung tren chinh thiet bi cua ban - khong cai dat, khong tai khoan.


Buoc 1 - cac diem thang hang

Nhan L1 va L2 de bang thong tin hien khoang 1.5 trieu km tu Trai Dat tren duong Mat Troi-Trai Dat.


Buoc 2 - cac diem tam giac

Nhay den L4 va L5 de doc hinh tam giac deu 60 do phia truoc va phia sau.


Buoc 3 - moc tau vu tru

Nhan SOHO va JWST, roi bat man hinh toan phan de giu cac dieu khien hien thi khi khung dong quay xoay.
