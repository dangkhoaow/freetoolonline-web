How to Explore Stellar Main-Sequence Lifetime Calculator

Last reviewed: 2026-07-22

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Stellar Main-Sequence Lifetime Calculator, drag the mass slider, and watch the probe star trace the mass-lifetime relation across five real stellar types.

  1. Open /space-3d/main-sequence-lifetime.html and wait for the starfield and main-sequence curve to render.
  2. Drag the mass slider to its lowest setting (0.1 solar masses) and watch the probe star settle near the faint, cool end of the curve.
  3. Drag the slider to the middle and note when the probe lines up with the reference sphere at 1.0 solar mass, the Sun's position.
  4. Drag the slider to its highest setting (20 solar masses) and watch the probe jump to the hot, blue-white end of the curve.
  5. Press Reset to the Sun to snap the probe back to 1.0 solar mass at any point.
  6. Read the facts panel for the probe's live surface temperature, luminosity, and main-sequence lifetime.
