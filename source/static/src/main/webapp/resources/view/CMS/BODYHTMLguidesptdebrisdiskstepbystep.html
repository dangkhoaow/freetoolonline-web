Como explorar Debris Disk 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the real Beta Pictoris disk turn.

  1. Open /space-3d/debris-disk.html and wait for the star and the disk to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch the disk slowly turn, shown nearly edge-on.
  3. Notice the disk fading outward, following the real reported brightness falloff.
  4. Press the Warp button to see the inner disk tilt and the hidden planet marker appear.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow down or speed up the turning.
