Text to Speech

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

Type or paste your text, choose a voice, and press Speak to hear it read aloud right in your browser.

Typical use: hearing a message, an article, or a draft read out loud when you would rather listen than read.

Pick from the voices your browser and operating system provide, then use the Rate slider (0.5 to 2) and Pitch slider (0 to 2) to make the voice faster, slower, higher, or lower. There is no file picker and nothing to download - the words are spoken by your browser and never leave your device, and you can press Stop any time to cancel playback.
