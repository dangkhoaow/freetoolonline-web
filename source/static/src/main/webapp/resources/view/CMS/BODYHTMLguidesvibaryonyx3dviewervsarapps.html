Khi nao viewer trinh duyet Baryonyx phu hop

Chon viewer trinh duyet khi muon xoay spinosaurid khong buom lung, Size vs human theo ty le 7.5 m trung thuc, va nhap su that tren dau/than/co/duoi/chan/canh tay - khong cai gi. Khong gui du lieu tham den may chu - tot cho laptop lop hoc hoac kiem tra nhanh so 2010, mong ~31 cm, va bang chung che do an trong da day.


Khi nao ung dung AR hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR thang khi can siluet tren san phong khach; viewer nay khong bao gio tuyen bo dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di canh holotype tai Natural History Museum, London. Anh tinh thang chi khi can mot khung chia se khong tuong tac.


So sanh Baryonyx nay khong phai la gi

Day la bang trade-off cua viewer da ship - khong xep hang thuong hieu AR, khong biet tinh nang AR trang khong co, khong biet chieu dai/khoi luong chinh xac duy nhat ngoai so 2010 va khoang lich su, khong tuyen bo Baryonyx hoan toan song duoi nuoc, khong biet nhap-mong rieng, khong biet credit glTF, va khong biet diem so.
