How to work out a percentage

  1. Pick a calculation from the dropdown: a percentage of a number, what percent one number is of another, or a percent change.
  2. Enter the two numbers - the result updates as you type, no button to click.
  3. Switch calculations at any time; the numbers you already entered carry over so you can compare results.
