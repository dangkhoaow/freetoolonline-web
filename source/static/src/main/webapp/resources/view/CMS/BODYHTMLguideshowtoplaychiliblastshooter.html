How to Play Chili Blast Shooter - Step by Step

The Chili Blast Shooter loads a single ~26 KB canvas vertical shooter in your tab. Move with the keyboard; shots fire automatically on a timer.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-09

Move with WASD or arrows, dodge incoming fire, chain kills for a combo multiplier, grab power-ups, and press Enter to start or retry after game over.

Step 1 - start or retry

Press Play on the page, then press Enter to begin. After you lose all three lives, press Enter again to retry the run from wave one.

Step 2 - move and let auto-fire work

Use WASD or the arrow keys to dodge. Your ship fires on a fixed timer toward enemies above - you do not press a separate fire key.

Step 3 - chain kills and protect three lives

You start with three lives. Each enemy you destroy in quick succession raises a combo multiplier on your score. Getting hit costs one life unless a shield power-up blocks that single hit.

Step 4 - collect power-ups

Dropped pickups rotate through spread shot (wider volleys), speed boost (faster movement), and shield (blocks one hit). Grab them before they scroll off the bottom.

Step 5 - survive waves and the ice-cream boss

Enemies arrive in waves that get tougher over time. Every tenth wave spawns an ice-cream boss with extra health. Beat it to continue. Your best score saves locally under the key ftol:chiliblastshooter:hs.
