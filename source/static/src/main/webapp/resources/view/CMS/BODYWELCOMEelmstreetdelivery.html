Welcome to Elm Street Delivery - a free browser courier route game with no account or install. Press Play, click to start, then click houses and the warehouse to pick up and deliver fashion boxes before customers turn unhappy. About 3 MB loads after Play and then stays in the browser cache. The skill is route order and bike capacity, not twitch aiming. Everything runs client-side in a WebGL map, so your progress is session-only on this page. For more free browser games, open the game hub. Related picks: Pipe Rotate Puzzle for path puzzles and The House Pointclick for a longer click-through story.
