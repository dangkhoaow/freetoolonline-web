How to Explore Starlink Constellation Shells 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open Starlink Constellation Shells 3D Explorer, click through each of the five real orbital shells, and read its published altitude, inclination, and satellite count.

  1. Open /space-3d/starlink-constellation-shells.html and wait for Earth to render.
  2. Click "Shell 1" to isolate the 550 km, 53.0-degree shell and read its 1,584-satellite, 72-plane structure in the facts panel.
  3. Click through Shells 2 to 5 to compare each one's altitude, inclination, and satellite count.
  4. Click "Show all shells" to see the full five-shell structure together.
  5. Drag to orbit Earth and scroll or pinch to zoom for a closer look at each shell's tilt.
