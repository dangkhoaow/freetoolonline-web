How to explore Chelyabinsk Meteor 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the shallow entry brighten into an airburst.

  1. Open /space-3d/chelyabinsk-meteor.html and wait for Earth and the entry path to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch the meteor descend along the shallow trail.
  3. Watch brightness rise toward the real airburst, then see the blast shell expand.
  4. Read the live phase label and facts table as the timeline advances.
  5. Drag to orbit and use the speed slider to slow or speed the compressed entry.
