How to Play Orbital Radius Shooter

Orbital Radius Shooter loads a ~170 KB Canvas shmup in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Press Play, click PLAY in the menu, then WASD + mouse aim.

Launch

Click Play on this page, then PLAY on the title screen.

Combat

Move with WASD; aim with the mouse; click or hold to fire.

Options

F toggles autofire; M toggles mute; stats save locally.
