How to Explore Pluto-Charon Double Planet 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Pluto-Charon Double Planet 3D Explorer, scrub orbital phase, then play or pause as Pluto and Charon circle the outside-Pluto barycenter.

  1. Open /space-3d/pluto-charon-double-planet.html and wait for Pluto, Charon, the green barycenter marker, and both orbit paths to paint.
  2. Drag Orbital phase from 0% toward 50% and watch the worlds move to opposite sides of the barycenter while face markers stay pointed at each other.
  3. Press Play (or Pause) and watch phase climb through a full mutual orbit; or keep scrubbing by hand.
  4. Reset to restart at phase 0%, then read the facts panel for 1188.3 km, 606 km, ~8.2:1 mass ratio, ~19,591 km separation, ~2,110 km barycenter distance, and 6.387 days.
  5. Compare mentally with Earth-Moon (barycenter inside Earth) and with a dwarf-planet size lineup on the sibling pages linked below.
