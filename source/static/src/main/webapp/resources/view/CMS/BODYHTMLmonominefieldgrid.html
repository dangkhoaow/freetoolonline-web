Mono Minefield Grid is a monochrome minefield clear in the browser: Easy 9x9 with 10 mines, Medium 16x16 with 40, or Hard 24x16 with 80. Left-click opens, right-click or Shift-click flags, middle-click chords. About ~52 KB after Play, no install, no account.

MONO MINEFIELD

Minefield clear from the open-source Cursor Arcade sweeper module (MIT). About ~52 KB downloads once from this site when you press Play. Best scores stay in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

Left-click open, right-click or Shift-click flag, middle-click chord, R restart, Esc menu. A mouse or trackpad works best.

Press Play to load the game.
Scores use ftol:monominefieldgrid:storage.
