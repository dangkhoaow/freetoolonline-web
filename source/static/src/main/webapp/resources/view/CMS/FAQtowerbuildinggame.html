Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tower Building Game free to play?

Yes, Tower Building Game is completely free to play in your browser. No download, no account, no hidden fees. Just open, play, and try to beat your score.

What devices can I play on?

Tower Building Game works on desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Chrome Mobile, Samsung Internet). Use your mouse on desktop or touch on mobile to rotate and drop blocks.

How do I rotate the blocks?

On desktop, hold the left mouse button on the block to rotate it. On mobile, tap and drag the block to rotate. Release to drop it into place. The game shows rotation arrow guides to help you align blocks.

What happens if my tower falls?

If the tower becomes too unstable or a block falls off, the game ends and shows your final score. Tap the "Try Again" button to start a new round. There's no penalty - each game is independent.

Can I play offline?

The game loads from the web and requires an internet connection. However, once it's fully loaded, short internet hiccups won't interrupt gameplay. For a fully offline experience, you'd need to save the game to your device, which isn't currently supported.

Is there a time limit?

No time limit. Stack blocks at your own pace. The game only ends when your tower becomes too unstable or a block falls. Take your time for precision, or rush for speed - the choice is yours.
