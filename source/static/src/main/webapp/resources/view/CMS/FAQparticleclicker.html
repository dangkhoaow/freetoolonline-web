Frequently Asked Questions

What is Particle Clicker?

A free browser incremental game made at CERN Webfest 2014. You click a detector for particle events, then spend data and funding on research, workers, and upgrades.

How do I play?

Press Play, click the detector canvas for data, open Research to spend data on discoveries, hire workers in HR for automatic data, and buy upgrades with funding.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Lab state, research, workers, upgrades, and achievements use ftol:particleclicker:* keys on this device.

How big is the download?

About ~2 MB of HTML, JS, CSS, fonts, and icons after you press Play.

Does it need an account?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from particle-clicker/particle-clicker (MIT). Google Analytics and runtime CDNs were removed.
