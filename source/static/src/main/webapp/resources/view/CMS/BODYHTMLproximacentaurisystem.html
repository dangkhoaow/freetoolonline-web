About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Press play and drag to orbit the view.

Published figures: Proxima b (discovered 2016) is at least 1.06 Earth masses on an 11.2-day orbit inside the habitable zone; Proxima d (discovered 2022) is about 0.26 Earth masses on a 5.12-day orbit, too close and hot; Proxima c is an unconfirmed roughly 7-Earth-mass candidate on a roughly 5.28-year orbit, too cold.
