Eight Ball Pool is a free browser billiards game. Play 1P vs AI (easy / medium / hard / insane) or 2P local. Aim with the mouse, raise or lower shot power with W/S, and left-click to shoot. Esc returns to the main menu. About ~13 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want table tennis instead? Try Classic Pong. Prefer a head soccer duel? Try Head Soccer Arena.

EIGHT BALL POOL

Aim the cue with the mouse, set power with W/S, then click to shoot. Adapted from henshmi/Classic-Pool-Game (MIT). Music: Bossa Antigua by Kevin MacLeod (CC BY 3.0). About ~13 MB.

Works best with a mouse. Sound can be muted in the menu.

Press Play to load the game.
No account. Session only - no save files.
