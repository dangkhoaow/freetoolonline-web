Como explorar Venus Phases (Galileo) 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch Venus run through its phases as it laps the Sun.

  1. Open /space-3d/venus-phases-galileo.html and wait for the Sun, Venus and Earth.
  2. Watch Venus move on the inner orbit while Earth moves on the outer one.
  3. Follow the preview disc at the top left as it shifts from crescent to full.
  4. Note how a crescent is large and a full disc is small in the readout.
  5. Use the speed slider; read the panel for the 9.9 to 66 arcsec and 584 day figures.
