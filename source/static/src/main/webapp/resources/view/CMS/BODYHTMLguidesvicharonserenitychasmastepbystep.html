How to explore Charon Serenity Chasma 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and rotate Charon so the canyon belt faces you.

  1. Open /space-3d/charon-serenity-chasma.html and wait for Charon to appear.
  2. Press Play and rotate until the blue trench crosses the disk.
  3. Toggle Ocean hint to show the schematic freeze-expansion interior.
  4. Drag to orbit along the belt, then face the far hemisphere.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow or speed the rotation.
