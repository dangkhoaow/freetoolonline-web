About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Scrub the Time slider and drag to orbit the view.

Published figures: current separation about 2.5 million light-years, approach speed about 110 km/s, first close pass in about 3.75-4 billion years, full merger by about 7 billion years (van der Marel et al. 2012). A 2025 study briefly lowered the merger odds to about 50%; a 2026 recalibration raised it back to about 90%, re-confirming the classic timeline shown here.
