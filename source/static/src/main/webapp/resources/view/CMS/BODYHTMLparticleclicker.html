Particle Clicker is a free browser incremental game from CERN Webfest 2014. Click the detector to collect data, spend it on research discoveries, hire lab workers for automatic data, and buy upgrades with funding. Progress saves under ftol:particleclicker keys. About ~2 MB after Play, no install, no account.

PARTICLE CLICKER

Click the detector for events, research particle discoveries, hire HR, and buy upgrades. Adapted from particle-clicker/particle-clicker (MIT, CERN Webfest 2014). Progress uses ftol:particleclicker:* keys. About ~2 MB.

Works with mouse or touch. Landscape mode works best on phones.
