Url Decoder Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

URL Decoder hoat dong theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, nhap lieu va lay ket qua. Trang nay giai thich tung buoc can gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Truy cap URL Decoder. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - nhap lieu

Dung no de tao gia tri query string, giai ma mot lien ket duoc chia se, hoac kiem tra mot tham so co ma phan tram.


Buoc 3 - lay ket qua

Viec ma hoa su dung encodeURIComponent/decodeURIComponent cua trinh duyet va chay cuc bo - khong can tai len va khong can tai khoan.

Khong co gi ban nhap duoc luu; tai lai trang se xoa o nhap.
