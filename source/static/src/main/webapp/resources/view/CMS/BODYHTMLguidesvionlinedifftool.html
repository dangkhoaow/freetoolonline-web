Cong cu thuc thi/developer-tools/text-diff.html

Cong cu diff trong trinh duyet tai /developer-tools/text-diff.html la cong cu nen mo khi can so sanh hai khoi van ban - tep cau hinh, ban ket xuat JSON, ban sua ma nguon, ban nhap van ban, lat cat tep log. No xu ly cuc bo ngay trong tab trinh duyet; van ban dan vao khong duoc tai len may chu tu xa, va trang khong giu lai hai dau vao sau khi dong tab. Khong tai khoan, khong cai dat, khong dang ky. De so sanh nang cao hon (cap do dong vs cap do tu vs cap do ky tu vs che do hop nhat kieu git), huong dan cung chu de tai /guides/en/text-diff-vs-line-diff-vs-word-diff-vs-git-diff.html giai thich che do nao de doc nhat cho tung dang dau vao.
