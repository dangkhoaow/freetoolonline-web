When to Play Bangbang Artillery

Bangbang Artillery fits a short duel session - about ~35 KB after Play, no install.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

When you want aim-and-power practice

Angle and strength matter more than twitch reflexes - good for a calm few minutes.

When you want a learning opponent

The neural net improves within the round, so later shots get harder if you miss early.

When to pick another game

Want paddle scoring? Use Classic Pong. Want quit-or-continue numbers? Use Googol Stopping Game.
