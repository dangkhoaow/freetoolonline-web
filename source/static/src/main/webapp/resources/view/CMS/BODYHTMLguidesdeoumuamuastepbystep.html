So nutzen Sie 'Oumuamua 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the real 2017 flyby.

  1. Open /space-3d/oumuamua.html and wait for the Sun and the object to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch it swing past the Sun on a path that never closes.
  3. Notice the tumbling rotation and the brightness swinging as it turns.
  4. Toggle Outgassing to see the leading, still-unconfirmed explanation for its trajectory bend.
  5. Drag to orbit and compare the hyperbolic path to a normal closed orbit.
