Open the explorer and watch a star vanish behind the Moon's dark limb.

  1. Open /space-3d/occultation-lunar.html and wait for the Moon and the star to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch the star sweep toward the Moon's dark (unlit) edge.
  3. Notice the star vanish almost instantly, then reappear on the far side just as abruptly.
  4. Press the Grazing button to switch to a skimming pass that blinks past lunar mountains.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow down or speed up the sweep.
