When to Play Mono Minefield Grid

Mono Minefield Grid fits short logic sessions where you want Easy/Medium/Hard minefield clears with local best scores - about ~52 KB after Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Short logic breaks

One Medium board is a focused clear. Restart with R and chase a higher score without leaving the tab.

Mouse and keyboard practice

Flagging and chords reward a pointer. Use a desktop or trackpad; phones load but lack a dedicated touch HUD.

Local bests without accounts

Scores stay under ftol:monominefieldgrid:storage. No login. Reset all high scores clears them in Settings.

When to pick another game

Want a neon sliding space puzzle? Use Space Grid Puzzle. Want a slide puzzle? Use Cyber Slide Puzzle.
