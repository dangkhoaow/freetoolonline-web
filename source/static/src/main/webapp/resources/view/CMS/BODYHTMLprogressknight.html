Progress Knight is a free browser medieval life-sim incremental. Start as a beggar, take jobs, learn skills, buy gear, age through a lifespan, then touch the amulet to prestige for stronger next lives. About ~0.12 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a capitalist idle loop instead? Try Idle Capitalist Loop. Prefer a short arcade session? Try Swing Block Tower.

PROGRESS KNIGHT

Text-based medieval incremental: jobs, skills, shop, aging, and amulet prestige. Saves use ftol:progressknight:gameDataSave. Adapted from ihtasham42/progress-knight (Unlicense). About ~0.12 MB.

Works on phone and desktop. Pause, auto-promote, and auto-learn sit in the sidebar after load.

Press Play to load the game.
Progress saves stay on-device (ftol:progressknight:gameDataSave).
