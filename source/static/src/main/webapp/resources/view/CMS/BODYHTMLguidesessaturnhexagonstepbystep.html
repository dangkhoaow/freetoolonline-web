Como explorar Saturn Hexagon 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Saturn Hexagon 3D Explorer, read the 30,000 km / 120 m/s figures, and scrub rotation phase while jet particles ride the hexagon.

  1. Open /space-3d/saturn-hexagon.html and wait for the cyan polar hexagon on Saturn.
  2. Read the facts panel: width ~30,000 km, side ~13,800 km, jet ~120 m/s.
  3. Scrub rotation phase to spin the globe and move the jet particles.
  4. Press Pause spin, then Play spin, to control the motion.
  5. Toggle Hide/Show rings when you want a clearer pole view.
