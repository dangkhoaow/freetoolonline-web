Khi nao viewer trinh duyet hop

Chon viewer khi muon xoay theropod hai chan Trias Muon voi Size vs human ti le khoang 2.5 m, khong cai dat. Khong gui gi len server.


Khi ung dung AR hoac bao tang hop hon

Ung dung AR thang khi can dat tren san phong. Bao tang thang cho bo xuong Ghost Ranch that. Anh chi khi can mot khung chia se.

Bang trade-off - khong xep hang thuong hieu AR, khong bia AR, khong bia khoi luong ngoai 15-25 kg, khong diem so. Xem cach xem 3D va so sanh kich thuoc.
