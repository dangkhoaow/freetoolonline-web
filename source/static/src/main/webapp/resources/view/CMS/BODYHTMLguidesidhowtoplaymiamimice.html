The Miami Mice page loads a ~0.11 MB beat-em-up in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, choose START or HORDE MODE, then fight with WASD + H/J/B.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~0.11 MB). Wait until LOADING clears and the title screen appears.

Step 2 - pick a mode

Click START for the story wave path or HORDE MODE for endless pressure.

Step 3 - fight

WASD moves. H punches. J kicks. B blocks. A gamepad left stick and face buttons also work.

Step 4 - clear waves

Defeat rats and bosses on the canvas stage. Audio uses in-bundle SoundBox players after the first action.
