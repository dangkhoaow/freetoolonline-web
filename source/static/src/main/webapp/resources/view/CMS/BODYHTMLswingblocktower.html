Swing Block Tower is a free browser stacking game. Tap or click to drop a crane-hung block when it swings over the tower. Line up edges for score; three misses end the run. About ~2.0 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want keyboard line-clear stacking? Try Mono Stack Blocks. Want tower defense instead? Try Neon Tower Rush.

SWING BLOCK TOWER

Drop swinging blocks to stack a tower. Tap or click to release. Three missed drops end the run. Background music and drop sounds included. Adapted from iamkun/tower_game (MIT, BMQB Inc). About ~2.0 MB.

Tap, click, or touch to drop. Works on phone and desktop. No save data.
