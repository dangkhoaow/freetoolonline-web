How to use Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer

Companion guide for the Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer.

The walkthrough below works the same way on desktop and mobile: drag or swipe to orbit the camera, scroll or pinch to zoom, and tap or click the buttons under the scene. Most readers finish the five steps in under a minute the first time through.

Everything renders locally through WebGL with the vendored three.js engine - no files are uploaded and no account is required. Once Play, Ring, and Speed feel familiar, the Space 3D collection has more explorers built the same way, including Triton Cantaloupe Terrain 3D Explorer and Hyperion Sponge Moon 3D Explorer.
