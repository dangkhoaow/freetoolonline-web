When the Albertosaurus browser viewer fits

Pick the Albertosaurus browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a Late Cretaceous Alberta tyrannosaurid with Size vs human at the honest 9 m panel length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything. Nothing about the visit is sent to a server.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your living-room floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for walking beside real tyrannosaurid mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame with no interactivity.


What this Albertosaurus comparison is not

This Albertosaurus comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - it is not a ranking of commercial AR brands, it does not invent AR features the page does not have, it does not invent a single mass outside the published 1.8-2.7 t range (panel baseline 2.2 t disclosed), it does not conflate Albertosaurus with Tyrannosaurus rex or Gorgosaurus, it does not claim the optional glTF is a scientifically exact reconstruction, and it does not invent game score or win states.
