Pixel Necromancer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Press Play and a compact pixel necromancer arena loads in the tab. Aim with the pointer, cast spells on click, resurrect fallen enemies with Space, and spend souls on rituals between levels.

StepAction
LaunchPlay on this page to inject the iframe
AimMove the pointer to set spell direction
CastClick or tap to fire a spell (limited charges per level)
ResurrectPress Space when the bar fills to raise corpses as allies
ShopArrow keys plus Enter pick rituals between levels

Adapted from danprince/norman-the-necromancer (Unlicense, JS13K 2022). Procedural Web Audio and a single spritesheet - zero CDN and zero network calls after load. Press P inside the frame to pause.
