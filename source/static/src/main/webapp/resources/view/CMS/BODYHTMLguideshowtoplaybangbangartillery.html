How to Play Bangbang Artillery - Step by Step

The Bangbang Artillery page loads a ~35 KB artillery duel in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Press Play, aim with arrows, set power, then Space to fire at the learning AI cannon.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~35 KB). A full-window canvas boots with you on the right and the computer on the left.

Step 2 - aim and set power

Left/Right rotate. Up/Down change strength. On touch: drag right to aim, drag left for power.

Step 3 - fire

Space (or tap) launches the ball on your turn. Turns alternate after each shot settles.

Step 4 - beat the learning AI

The computer starts random, then trains on misses. Hit it before it hits you. Refresh resets the net.
