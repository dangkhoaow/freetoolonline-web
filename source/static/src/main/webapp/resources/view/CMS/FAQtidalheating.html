Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Tidal Heating 3D Explorer show?

Io flexing with flickering volcanic hotspots and an Europa ocean-cutaway toggle, with Ganymede completing the 1:2:4 resonance chain. Literacy figures include ~400 Io volcanoes and a thin-ice-shell Europa ocean.

How is this different from Orbital Resonance 3D?

Orbital Resonance 3D teaches the 1:2:4 period-ratio mechanics itself. This page teaches the thermal and geological consequence - Io volcanism and the Europa ocean.

How is this different from Galilean Moons 3D?

Galilean Moons 3D gives a general four-moon overview. This page focuses specifically on tidal-heating outcomes for Io and Europa.

Does Io have a global magma ocean?

Recent NASA Juno gravity measurements (2025) indicate Io most likely has a mostly solid mantle with intense localized melting, not a shallow global magma ocean.

Is this an interior-structure simulator?

No. It is an educational schematic - not a magma-flow or interior-structure solver. Io flexing and hotspot flicker are stylized for visibility.

How many active volcanoes does Io have?

An estimated 400, according to NASA Juno mission data, making Io the most volcanically active body known in the solar system.
