Cach Choi Claudicus Quest - Tung Buoc

Trang Claudicus Quest tai mot game nhap vai trung co theo luot ~132 KB trong iframe. Ban kham pha Brannford, Thornwood va Greymoor Crypt, nhan nhiem vu, mua sam trang bi, va luu vao ftol:claudicusquest:save. Nhan Play de bat dau.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

WASD hoac mui ten di chuyen; Space/Enter tuong tac; combat 1 Tan cong, 2 Phong thu, 3 Thuoc, 4 Chay, 5 dac biet; I kho do; Q nhat ky nhiem vu; M tat am; Esc tam dung/luu. Luu vao ftol:claudicusquest:save.

Buoc 1 - nhan Play va bat dau hoac tiep tuc

Nhan Play tren trang game de tai iframe (~132 KB). Tren man hinh tieu de chon Game Moi de chon nhan vat, hoac Tiep tuc khi da co ban luu. Engine la Claudicus cua Brian Benzinger (MIT); LICENSE va CREDITS di kem cac file game.

Buoc 2 - di chuyen va tuong tac tren ban do o vuong

Di bo bang WASD hoac mui ten qua Brannford (trung tam lang), Thornwood (rung) va Greymoor Crypt (ham ngam). Nhan Space hoac Enter de noi chuyen voi NPC, mo ruong, dung quay hang, va di qua cac diem chuyen ban do.

Buoc 3 - chien dau bang menu combat

Khi mot cuoc chien bat dau, nhan 1 Tan cong, 2 Phong thu, 3 Thuoc (hien so luong con lai), 4 Chay, va 5 khi co dac biet (Shield Bash, Ambush, Intimidate, hoac ky nang vu khi nhu Backstab tu level 3+). Combat theo luot voi nhat ky cuon o duoi man hinh.

Buoc 4 - kho do, nhiem vu, va chon lop o level 3

Nhan I de mo man hinh kho do (vu khi, giap, thuoc, danh hieu da dat duoc). Nhan Q de mo nhat ky nhiem vu. Khi dat level 3, game mo man hinh chon lop: WARRIOR (+2 DEF), SCOUT (+2 AGI), hoac BRIGAND (+2 STR) - man hinh ghi ro lua chon nay la vinh vien.

Buoc 5 - tam dung, luu, va ban do

Nhan Esc de tam dung (Tiep tuc, Luu Game, Ve Man hinh Tieu de). Auto-save cung chay sau cac su kien quan trong. Thua tran se hoi sinh ban tai Brannford voi hinh phat mat vang. Nhan M bat cu luc nao de tat am nhac va hieu ung am thanh procedural.
