The Spike Sprint page loads a ~25 KB pixel runner in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, jump with Space or tap, clear pits, and stay ahead of the spiked barrel.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~25 KB). Canvas #gameCanvas boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - jump

Tap, click, or press Space to start a jump. Hold longer (up to about 320 ms) for more height.

Step 3 - clear hazards

Jump over pits and bushes. Speed ramps up as your score climbs.

Step 4 - watch the barrel

The spiked barrel surges when you stall. Touching the spike tip ends the run. Use Restart on the game-over screen. Session high score stays in this tab.
