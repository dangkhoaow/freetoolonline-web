Screen Recorder

Last reviewed: 2026-06-16

Record your screen, camera, or microphone and download the clip without installing an app or creating an account. Pick a source, click Start, and the browser captures the session with Pause, Resume, and Stop controls and a live duration timer; when you stop, the clip appears inline with a download link so you can save it straight away.

For camera and screen recordings you can switch on an optional face blur that hides detected faces on your device before the recorder ever sees the frame, with an adjustable blur strength. When you are done, convert the finished clip from WebM to MP4 right in the browser when you need a more portable format to share or upload elsewhere.

Everything runs in the browser. Your recording stays on your device, is never uploaded, and nothing is kept after you close the page.
