When to Play Idle Capitalist Loop

Idle Capitalist Loop fits low-attention idle sessions where you want click-driven businesses, manager automation, multi-planet expansion, and angel prestige - about ~87 KB after Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Background tab grinding

Leave the tab open with managers hired and cycles auto-collect. Return for offline earnings (up to 12 hours at 25% efficiency) and buy more upgrades.

Short check-in loops

Open the page between tasks: collect bars, buy the next business tier, and watch CPS climb. Space pauses when you need to step away mid-session.
