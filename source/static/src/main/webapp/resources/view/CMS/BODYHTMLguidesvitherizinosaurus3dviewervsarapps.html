Khi nao viewer trinh duyet Therizinosaurus phu hop

Chon viewer trinh duyet khi ban muon xoay herbivore co dai voi mong khong lo, Size vs human o ti le ~9.5 m trong khoang 9-10 m, va bam de doc su that tren mong khong lo, dau, co, tay, duoi hoac chan - khong cai gi. Khong gui chuyen tham len server - tot cho laptop lop hoc hoac kiem tra nhanh so lieu va disclosure thieu hoa thach.


Khi nao ung dung AR hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi can bong tren san phong; viewer nay khong bao gio tuyen bo dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di canh hoa thach tay va mong Therizinosaurus va cam nhan quy mo phong ma man khong dat. Anh tinh thang chi khi can mot khung chia se khong tuong tac.


So sanh Therizinosaurus nay khong phai la
