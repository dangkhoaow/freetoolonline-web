Como jugar Pixel Park Puzzle

Pixel Park Puzzle loads a ~132 KB parking game in an iframe. W/S throttle, A/D steer, Escape pause. Digit2 cycles vehicles. Session-only - no localStorage saves.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Drive each vehicle into its marked bay. Avoid cones and walls. Click on-screen menu buttons to pick levels. Engine MIT (masonlet/pixel-parker).
