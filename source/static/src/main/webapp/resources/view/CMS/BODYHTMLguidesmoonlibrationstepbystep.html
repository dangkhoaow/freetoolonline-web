How to explore Moon Libration 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the explorer and watch the Moon wobble to see why we view 59% of its surface despite tidal locking.

  1. Open /space-3d/moon-libration.html and wait for the wobbling Moon.
  2. Read the facts panel: libration in longitude (7.9 deg), latitude (6.7 deg), and the 59% figure.
  3. Watch the blue sub-Earth dot drift off-centre - that drift is the libration.
  4. Toggle Exaggerate 5x to make the wobble obvious, or highlight the libration zone.
  5. Compare with Moon Phases 3D for the lunar phases, or Tidal Locking 3D for why one face stays toward Earth.
