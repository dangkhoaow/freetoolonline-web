How to Use Constellation Sphere 3D Explorer - Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Start on Orion, switch Ursa Major / Crux / Summer Triangle sticks, scrub Mag limit, and click a star to read RA hours, Dec degrees, and V magnitude on the teaching sphere.

  1. Open Constellation Sphere 3D Explorer and wait for the sphere to paint.
  2. Drag to orbit, scroll or pinch to zoom, and keep the facts panel visible on wide screens.
  3. Tap Orion, Ursa Major, Crux, or Summer Triangle to highlight sticks.
  4. Use Mag limit and Hide grid to control density and the equatorial teaching grid.
  5. Click a named star so RA / Dec / V mag stay the reliable takeaway.
