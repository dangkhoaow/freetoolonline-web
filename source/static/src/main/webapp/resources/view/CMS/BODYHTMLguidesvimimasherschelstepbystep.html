How to use Mimas Herschel Crater 3D Explorer

  1. Open the Mimas Herschel Crater 3D Explorer and wait for the canvas to paint.
  2. Press Play if the moon is paused, then drag to face the large Herschel bowl.
  3. Toggle Peak to show or hide the central mountain marker.
  4. Move Speed to slow the spin while you read the facts panel sizes.
  5. Use Fullscreen on a large display when teaching relative crater scale.
