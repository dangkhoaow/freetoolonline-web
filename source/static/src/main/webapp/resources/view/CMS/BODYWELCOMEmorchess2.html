Welcome to Mor Chess 2 - a free browser chess puzzle you can play without an account or install. Press Play to load the ~13 KB game in a same-origin iframe. Each turn shows a short menu of named positional ideas (for example, a knight outpost, a bishop fianchetto, a rook lift, or a pawn chain); click one to play it out on the board against an opponent called Black Cat. A better evaluation - built from stronger positional choices - raises your chances of finding a checkmate. Click or tap is the only input; no keyboard is needed. Privacy note: nothing leaves this device except the initial page and game files from this site. For more free browser games, open the games hub.
