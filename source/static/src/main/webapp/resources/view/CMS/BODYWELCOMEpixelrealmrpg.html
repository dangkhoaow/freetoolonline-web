Pixel Realm RPG

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Press Play and a top-down RPG loads in the tab. Choose New Game on the title screen, explore maps, fight enemies, loot chests, and manage inventory and equipment.

StepAction
LaunchPlay on this page to inject the iframe
StartNew Game or Continue if a save exists
MoveWASD or arrow keys
CombatX to attack the enemy in front of you
ItemsI inventory, G equipment, E use selected item, [ ] cycle items

Adapted from andy64lol/jsrpg (MIT). Local PixeloidMono font and PNG/GIF sprites, zero CDN after load. Saves persist in namespaced localStorage.
