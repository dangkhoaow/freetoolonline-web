How to Explore Magnetar 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Magnetar 3D Explorer, scrub the field comparison ladder and flare pulse, and read the published field and census figures.

  1. Open /space-3d/magnetar.html and wait for the neutron-star sphere and field lines.
  2. Drag Field comparison from Earth toward Magnetar and watch field-line opacity grow.
  3. Drag Flare pulse upward to brighten the teaching giant-flare flash.
  4. Reset, then step through fridge, MRI-class, and ordinary-pulsar rungs.
  5. Read the facts panel for ~10^14-10^15 G, Earth/fridge contrast, quantum critical ~4.4 x 10^13 G, and ~31 of ~3,000.
