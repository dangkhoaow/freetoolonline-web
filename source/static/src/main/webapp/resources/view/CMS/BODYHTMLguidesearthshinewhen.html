When to use Earthshine 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want to understand the ashen glow on a crescent Moon, and skip it when you want the full cycle of lunar phases or the day-night map of Earth.

  • Explaining the old Moon in the new Moon arms
  • Tracing light from the Sun to Earth to the Moon and back
  • Linking Earth albedo to how bright the glow looks

This is an educational visualization with boosted glow, not to scale.

Walk the controls in the step-by-step guide, or compare options in vs alternatives. Open the Earthshine 3D Explorer.
