Khi Nao Dung Hohmann Transfer 3D Explorer - Phien Tot Nhat va Gioi Han

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Mo Hohmann Transfer 3D Explorer khi ban can chuyen quy dao hai lan dot kinh dien giua cac quy dao tron - LEO sang GEO tong 3.92 km/s (2.45 + 1.47) trong khoang 5.3 gio, hoac Trai Dat sang Sao Hoa khoang 3.6-3.9 km/s trong 259 ngay - ma khong can bo thiet ke nhiem vu day du.


Demo lop hoc nam phut

Demo lop hoc nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Hohmann Transfer 3D Explorer o preset LEO sang GEO, bam Play transfer, va xem tau truot tren elip teal tu lan dot khoi hanh den lan den. Bang su kien liet ke 2.45 va 1.47 km/s that de viet len bang.


Quy mo ve tinh doi hanh tinh trong mot hinh hoc

Quy mo ve tinh doi hanh tinh van doc duoc khi ban chuyen tu LEO sang GEO (gio, kilomet) sang Trai Dat sang Sao Hoa (ngay, AU) va giu cung elip, hai moc, va bang. Bang Trai Dat-Sao Hoa cung ghi cua so phong khoang moi 26 thang (chu ky synodic 779.94 ngay) va Sao Hoa dung khoang 44 do phia truoc Trai Dat luc phong ly tuong.


Phien Hohmann Transfer 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Phien Hohmann Transfer 3D Explorer khong phu hop la thiet ke quy dao nhiem vu that, doi mat phang, gravity assist, hay ngan sach Oberth thap hon so hai lan dot ly tuong. Voi mot quy dao LEO tron dung ISS Orbit Tracker; voi do lech tam dung Kepler Orbits 3D Explorer. Trang can WebGL va khong phai may ke hoach cua so phong.
