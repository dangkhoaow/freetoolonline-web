Tip Calculator

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Split a restaurant or service bill and work out the tip amount per person.

Use it to settle a group bill quickly without doing the math by hand.

The tip amount, the bill-plus-tip total, and the equal per-person share are shown together, so you do not have to work out the split separately.

Does not provide financial advice - results are general estimates and do not account for tax being calculated separately from the tip, or for splitting the tip unevenly across people.

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.
