Barosaurus 3D Viewer shows a long-necked diplodocid sauropod - panel length 26 m, panel mass 17 t. Drag, zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person, click a body region.


Open page and control camera

Open the viewer and wait for the facts panel. Orbit with pointer, zoom with wheel or pinch. Fullscreen keeps controls visible. Optional glTF may swap from CDN; procedural silhouette remains on failure.


Four controls under the canvas

Four controls under the canvas:

  • Fullscreen - fills display keeping buttons visible
  • Stop rotation / Auto-rotate - freezes or resumes auto-spin
  • Idle motion - adds subtle sway; tap again for Stand still
  • Size vs human - shows 1.8 m person at honest panel length ratio (26 m)
