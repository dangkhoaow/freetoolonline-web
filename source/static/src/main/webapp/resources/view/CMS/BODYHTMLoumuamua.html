In October 2017, astronomers found something that had never been confirmed before: an object from outside our Solar System, just passing through. 'Oumuamua came in on a path that never closes, tumbled end over end rather than spinning cleanly, and showed no visible gas or dust at all - yet its actual path bent very slightly more than gravity alone could explain. Watch it swing past the Sun below, then toggle the leading (unconfirmed) explanation for that bend.
