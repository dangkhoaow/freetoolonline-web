Night Swarm Survivor

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play and a full horde roguelite loads in the tab. Click Start on the title screen, then move with WASD, arrow keys, the on-screen touch joystick, or a gamepad. Weapons fire automatically; collect XP orbs to level up, pick weapon upgrades, and survive waves through Forest, Crypt, and Tundra stages with bosses.

Controls at a glance:

ActionInput
MoveWASD, arrows, touch joystick, or left gamepad stick
AttackAutomatic weapons
PausePause button or gamepad Start
SaveslocalStorage ftol:nightswarmsurvivor:*
