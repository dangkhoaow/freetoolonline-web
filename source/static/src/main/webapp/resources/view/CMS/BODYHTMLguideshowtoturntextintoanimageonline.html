Steps

  1. Type or paste your text into the text box.
  2. Pick a font family, size, and weight, and choose italic or oblique if you want it.
  3. Set the text color and background color, then adjust letter spacing, line height, and padding.
  4. Turn on the optional drop shadow and set its color, blur, and offset.
  5. Click Download to save the result as a PNG image, or Copy to put it on your clipboard.

Styling controls

You can pick from 17 font families and any size, with weights from thin to black and an italic or oblique style. The preview redraws live as you change each setting, so you see the result before you download it. Text and background colors, letter spacing, line height, and padding are all adjustable.

Presets

When you find a combination you like, save it as a named preset and reload it later. Presets and your current settings are stored in your browser, so they are there the next time you open the page on the same device.

What you get

The output is a PNG image of your styled text, not copy-paste characters. It does not upload custom font files; it uses the font families your browser already has. There is no SVG, PDF, or animated export - the result is a PNG. You can download it or copy it straight to your clipboard.

Privacy

Everything runs in your browser. There is no upload widget and no account; your text and the image stay on your device, and your presets live only in this browser's storage.
