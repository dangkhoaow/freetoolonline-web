When the Concavenator browser viewer fits

Pick the Concavenator browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a Spanish Early Cretaceous hip-crest theropod with Size vs human at the honest 6.0 m length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything. Nothing about the visit is sent to a server.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your living-room floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for walking beside Early Cretaceous theropod mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame with no interactivity.


What this Concavenator comparison is not

This Concavenator comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - it is not a ranking of commercial AR brands, it does not invent AR features the page does not have, it does not invent one precise length or weight beyond the disclosed about 5-6 m / 320-400 kg ranges, it does not claim a definite camel-style fat hump, it does not claim the optional glTF is a scientifically exact reconstruction, and it does not invent game score or win states.
