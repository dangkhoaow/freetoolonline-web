How to Play Garden Defense - Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Garden Defense is a 5-lane, 9-cell defense game played right in the browser: invaders march in from the right side of each lane, and you plant defenders to stop them before they reach the left edge. This guide covers the cards, the energy economy, placement, and how your best wave is saved.


Pick a card and plant it

Three defender cards are available: Shooter (100 energy) fires down its lane, Battery (50 energy) adds to your energy income each tick, and Wall (50 energy, 260 HP) blocks and absorbs damage. Click or tap a card to select it, then click or tap an open cell in the 5x9 grid to place it there; the cost is deducted immediately, and a card dims when you cannot afford it.


Manage the energy economy

You start with 125 energy, and it ticks up by 25 automatically every 3.5 seconds; every Battery you place adds to that automatic income. The core decision each wave is whether to spend now on offense or save toward a Battery that pays off across later waves.


Survive 5 escalating waves

Placing your first defender starts wave 1 of 5. Each wave sends more invaders, at a faster spawn rate, with a rising share of the tougher purple tier alongside the faster red tier; there is a short breather between waves to reposition. Invaders that reach a Wall eat through it rather than stopping, so a Wall buys time, not a permanent block.


Win, lose, and restart

Clear all 5 waves and the garden is saved; lose the instant a single invader crosses the left edge on any lane. Both the win and lose states draw directly on the canvas, and pressing Space or tapping restarts a fresh run immediately.


Fullscreen, pause, and your saved best wave

One click plays the game fullscreen with the cards and HUD still visible; press Escape to return to the page. Press P to pause mid-run. Your best wave reached is saved automatically to this browser's local storage, so you always know the number you are trying to beat - but it does not sync to another device or browser.
