Age Calculator Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17
freetoolonline.com Editorial Team

Age Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Age Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it to check an exact age for a form, an eligibility cutoff, or just out of curiosity.


Step 3 - get the result

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.

Defaults to today's date for the "as of" field, but you can pick any date to check age as of that day.
