When the Pteranodon browser viewer fits

Pick the Pteranodon browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a crested flying reptile, Size vs human at the honest about 6.5 m wingspan ratio inside the disclosed 5.6-7.6 m adult-male range, and click facts on crest or wing - without installing anything. Nothing about the visit is sent to a server - good for a classroom laptop or a quick desktop check of Late Cretaceous numbers (86-84 Mya; Othniel Charles Marsh 1876; Pteranodon longiceps; diet Carnivore, mainly fish).


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your living-room floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for walking beside mounted Pteranodon bone and feeling hall-scale wingspan the screen cannot match. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame with no interactivity.
