2048 Game - Merge the Numbers Puzzle

Last reviewed: 2026-07-06

Slide the numbered tiles with the arrow keys, WASD, or a swipe - equal tiles merge into their sum, and every move drops a new 2 (or occasionally a 4) onto the board.

Tiles glide with a short animation and pop when they merge, and each tile can only merge once per move - the same one-merge rule the classic puzzle uses.

Your best score is saved in this browser's local storage and shown next to the live score - no accounts, uploads, or leaderboards.
