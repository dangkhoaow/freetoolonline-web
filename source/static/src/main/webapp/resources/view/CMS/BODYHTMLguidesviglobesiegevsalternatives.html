Globe Siege So Voi Cac Game Tower Defense Khac

Globe Siege la game thu thanh tren hanh tinh 3D, chay trong trinh duyet khong can cai (~0.65 MB, WebGL). Diem khac biet la ban co tron - ban bao ve mot qua cau luc giac dang xoay, khong phai mot lan phang. Day la cach no so voi cac game thu thanh khac tren trang nay va voi cac lua chon can cai dat.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

So voi Medieval Wall Defense (2D, tang tien)

Medieval Wall Defense la vong tang tien phong thu tuong 2D: ban giu mot buc tuong phang, tich Research Points va prestige qua hang tram van. Globe Siege thien ve khong gian thay vi tang tien - quyet dinh la xay o dau tren qua cau 3D, va dia hinh quan trong (nui chi nhan phong khong, dai duong cho nang luong). Chon Medieval Wall Defense cho mot lan leo dai kieu idle; chon Globe Siege cho viec dat vi tri theo tung man.

So voi Garden Defense (lan 2D)

Garden Defense phong thu theo cac lan phang. Globe Siege boc chien truong len mot hinh cau, nen cac hanh lang tien vao thanh pho tu nhieu huong cung luc va ban xoay de bao het - mot yeu cau ve nhan thuc khong gian khac voi man hinh 2D co dinh.

So voi tower defense cai dat / tren cua hang

Cac game thu thanh goc (may ban hoac cua hang ung dung) thuong co chien dich lon hon va do hoa 3D, nhung can tai va cai dat, thuong can tai khoan, va doi khi co quang cao hoac mua trong game. Globe Siege danh doi quy mo lay truy cap khong ma sat: mot lan tai ~0.65 MB trong mot tab, khong tai khoan, va moi ban luu deu cuc bo. Can trinh duyet co WebGL va choi tot nhat voi ban phim.
