Khi nao phu hop

Dung khi nen tang dau ra cua ban chap nhan GIONG NOI nhung khong chap nhan VAN BAN - vi du de tao tap tin am thanh tu kich ban, nghe tai lieu hoac kiem tra phat am ma khong can cai dat ung dung.

Nhung gi co the mong doi

Am thanh duoc dieu khien boi cac truong lua chon: toc do, cao do va am luong. Gia tri mac dinh phu hop voi truong hop su dung pho bien nhat. Cac giong noi co san phu thuoc vao he dieu hanh cua thiet bi.

Neu can chuyen nguoc lai, xem phan cong cu lien quan de tim cong cu CHUYEN GIONG NOI THANH VAN BAN.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Van ban cua toi co duoc tai len may chu khong?

Khong. Van ban o lai tren thiet bi cua ban. Cong cu su dung API tong hop giong noi cua trinh duyet - khong co du lieu nao duoc gui di.

Toi co can tai khoan hoac cai dat khong?

Khong. Cong cu chay truc tiep trong trinh duyet, khong can cai dat hay tai khoan.
