Bang facts Apatosaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 21 m (tham chieu NHM; trung binh khoang 21-23 m theo nguon; mau lon nhat biet den toi khoang 30 m), khoi luong khoang 22.4 tan (NHM; trung binh khoang 16.4-22.4 tan; lon nhat toi khoang 33 tan), cua so 152-151 Mya Ky Jura Muon, an co, va phat hien - Othniel Charles Marsh dat ten A. ajax nam 1877 tu Formacion Morrison o tay Bac My. Marsh dat ten mau xuong lon hon nam 1879 la Brontosaurus; ten duoc dong nghia nam 1903 voi Apatosaurus giu uu tien, roi nghien cuu diplodocid 2015 (Tschopp et al.) khoi phuc Brontosaurus la chi rieng - trang bao cao moc thoi gian do, khong phai phan quyet chot. Dinh song co sau va day hon do dang nang hon Diplodocus, van chia se ke hoach co dai thap va duoi dang roi. Mo hinh dung builder sauropod chung khong tallForelimbs (cung nhanh voi Diplodocus - khac biet o so lieu cong bo va copy lich su ten, khong o mesh rieng). Bam ngan - khong keo - o dau, co, than, chan, hoac duoi de xem fact hoa thach ngan; bam cho trong de ve bang loai. Da va mau la phuc dung nghe thuat, khong phai xuong hoa thach chinh xac.
