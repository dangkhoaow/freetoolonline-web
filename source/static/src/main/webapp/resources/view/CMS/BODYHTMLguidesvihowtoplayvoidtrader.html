Cach choi Void Trader - Tung buoc

Trang Void Trader tai mot roguelike thuong mai khong gian Canvas 2D ~50 KB trong iframe. Ban bay tau co vat ly qua tam khu vuc procedural, dao thien thach lay quang, cap ben tram de mua ban hang, nang cap tau, va chien dau cuop bien - mot mang, mot van. Nhan Play de bat dau.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

WASD hoac mui ten di chuyen, chuot ngam, click trai ban phao, click phai hoac X ban ten lua, Space bat khien, E cap ben tram, Tab mo kho, Esc tam dung, R choi lai sau khi chet. Khong luu gi - moi lan vao la van permadeath moi.

Buoc 1 - khoi dong va doc HUD

Nhan Play tren trang game, roi LAUNCH tren menu tieu de trong iframe. HUD hien thi vo (hull), khien, tin dung (ban bat dau voi 500), kho (0/20), khu vuc va do sau. Game duoc thich nghi tu du an open-source Orbitron cua Daniel Silva Perez (giay phep MIT); giay phep va credits nam canh file game.

Buoc 2 - bay, dao va song sot

Dung WASD hoac mui ten de day voi quan tinh, ngam thap phao bang chuot. Click trai ban phao; click phai hoac X ban ten lua tu dan (dan han che). Space bat khien. Thien thach vua la muc tieu dao vua la nguy hiem va cham - dung vao se hao vo. Tieu diet cuop bien de nhan bounty tang theo do sau khu vuc.

Buoc 3 - cap ben va giao dich

Bay gan tram (vong xanh cyan tren ban do) va nhan E de mo panel giao dich, hoac Tab cho kho. Tram mua ban nam mat hang voi gia goc - Titanium Ore (20 Cr), Crystal Shards (45), Plasma Cells (80), Nav Data (150), Quantum Core (300) - moi khu vuc random xung quanh. Kho chua 20 don vi.

Buoc 4 - sua chua, nap lai va nang cap

Tai tram ban cung co the sua vo, nap khien, nap ten lua, va nang cap tau tu Scout sang Fighter roi Cruiser. Moi lop doi toc do lay vo, khien va toc do ban.

Buoc 5 - quet khu vuc va nhay sau hon

Co tam khu vuc tren ban do 4000 don vi. Khu 1 co mot tram; khu sau hon sinh nhieu thien thach, nhieu cuop bien (lop Scout, Fighter, Cruiser) va toi da ba tram. Quet het ke dich trong khu de tu dong nhay sang khu tiep; quet khu 8 de thang. Neu tau bi pha, nhan R de choi lai tu khu 1 voi 500 tin dung - khong co continue.
