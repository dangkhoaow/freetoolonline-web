How to explore Enceladus Geysers 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch jets rise from the tiger stripes.

  1. Open /space-3d/enceladus-geysers.html and wait for Enceladus to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch plume jets rise from the four south-polar stripes.
  3. Toggle Cutaway to reveal the ice shell and ocean layer beneath.
  4. Drag to orbit so the south pole faces you, then face the opposite hemisphere.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow or speed the plume animation.
