Mouse Test (Buttons & Scroll) Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Mouse Test (Buttons & Scroll) takes three short steps: open the page, enable listening, then exercise buttons and scroll on the pad.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Mouse Test (Buttons & Scroll). No install and no login.


Step 2 - enable listening

Click Enable mouse listening (or click the pad once). The pad border turns active and the status line shows Listening.


Step 3 - click, move, and scroll

Move across the pad to see coordinates. Click left, middle, and right - the matching indicators light up. Scroll to see wheel deltaY. Use Reset counters to start over. Nothing left the tab.
