Url Decoder Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

URL Decoder runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to URL Decoder. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it to build a query-string value, decode a shared link, or inspect a percent-encoded parameter.


Step 3 - get the result

Encoding uses the browser's own encodeURIComponent/decodeURIComponent and runs locally - no upload and no account.

Nothing you type is saved; refreshing the page clears the box.
