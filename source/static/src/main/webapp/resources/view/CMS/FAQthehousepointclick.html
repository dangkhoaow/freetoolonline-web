Frequently Asked Questions

What is The House Point and Click?

A free browser point-and-click house adventure. Click hotspots, collect inventory items, and move through linked rooms.

How do I play?

Press Play, then click objects and exits. Open the inventory tray to use items. Follow dialogue choices when they appear.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Scene progress, inventory, and mute prefs use ftol:thehousepointclick:* keys on this device.

How big is the download?

About ~18 MB of HTML, JS, CSS, images, and sound after you press Play. Later visits use the browser cache.

Do I need Flash?

No. The Flash SWF folder was dropped. Sound uses the HTML5 path in SoundManager 2.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from arturkot/the-house-game (MIT, Copyright (c) 2019 Artur Kot). Analytics were removed.
