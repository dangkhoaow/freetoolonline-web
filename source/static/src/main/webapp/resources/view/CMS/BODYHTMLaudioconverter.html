How to convert an audio file

  1. Choose the MP3 bitrate you want (128, 192, 256, or 320 kbps; 192 is the default).
  2. Drop or select your audio file, or a video whose sound you want to keep - conversion starts in the tab using FFmpeg.wasm and nothing is uploaded.
  3. When the status clears, click the download link to save the MP3.

Drop your audio or video file, pick an MP3 bitrate, and download the converted MP3.
