When to Play Neon Cat Chase

Neon Cat Chase loads about ~8 KB in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Best for 3-10 minute breaks when you want a tiny js13k arcade loop instead of a multi-megabyte RPG or bullet-hell run.

Micro session

The entire game is about 8 KB and boots instantly after Play.

Arcade loop

Levels escalate dog count and palette. Chasing high scores rewards repeat tries.

Keyboard first

Desktop WASD or arrows work best; the canvas scales to your viewport.
