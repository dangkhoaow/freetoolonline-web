Doc ket qua o mui gio local cua ban

Bo chuyen render ngay bang locale va mui gio cua trinh duyet, khop voi cai ma mot dong nghiep tren cung may se thay khi goi new Date(value).toString() trong console developer. Day thuong la cai ma mot developer doc timeline su co muon: gio dong ho ma su kien roi vao mui gio lam viec cua ho, khong phai gia tri UTC truu tuong ma log luu. Neu trang mo qua mot chuyen doi gio mua he, cung mot mili giay dau vao se ve gio dong ho khac truoc va sau cat - so nguyen ben duoi khong thay doi, nhung offset ma trinh duyet ap dung thay doi.

De ep doc UTC thay vi mac dinh mui local, doi mui gio he thong (hoac dung cua so duyet rieng tu voi profile khoa UTC) va tai lai bo chuyen. Logic chuyen doi la tat dinh - cung dau vao luon cho cung dau ra voi mot cau hinh mui gio cho truoc - nen trang phu hop de dan vao mot tra loi ticket hoac comment review code khi nguoi doc va nguoi viet dat trinh duyet ve cung mui gio truoc.
