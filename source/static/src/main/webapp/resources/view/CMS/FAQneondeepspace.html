Frequently Asked Questions

What is Neon Deep Space?

A free browser geometry-war roguelite shooter. Fight neon shapes, earn energy, and buy permanent upgrades in the shop.

How do I play?

Press Play, then Start. WASD moves, mouse aims, left click fires, Shift dashes, Space drops a bomb, F switches weapons, Esc pauses.

What weapons can I switch?

F cycles Blaster, Shotgun, and Sniper. Shop upgrades raise damage, health, speed, bombs, dash cooldown, and orbiters.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Energy, upgrades, and high score use ftol:neondeepspace:energy, ftol:neondeepspace:upgrades, and ftol:neondeepspace:high_score in this browser.

How big is the download?

About ~152 KB of HTML, CSS, and JS after you press Play. No CDN assets.

Is this the same as Andromeda Star Shooter?

No. Neon Deep Space is a geometry-war run with a meta shop. Andromeda Star Shooter is a different star-shooter loop on this site.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from ou178/neon-deep-space (MIT). Procedural canvas art and Web Audio; no image CDN.
