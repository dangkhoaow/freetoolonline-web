When to use Stellar Parallax 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Use this explorer when you want to see how the nearest stars distances are measured directly, and skip it when you want brightness-based distances or motion redshift.

  • Classroom demos of parallax and the parsec
  • Showing why closer stars shift more against the background
  • Introducing the first rung of the cosmic distance ladder

This is an educational visualization with a hugely exaggerated parallax angle, not to scale.
