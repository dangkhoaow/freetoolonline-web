Khi nao viewer Dilophosaurus tren trinh duyet phu hop

Chon viewer Dilophosaurus tren trinh duyet khi ban muon keo-orbit theropod mo doi Ky Jura Som, Size vs human o ty le trung thuc khoang 6.03 m trong khoang holotype va mau lon nhat, va bam facts o dau, canh tay, duoi, hoac chan - ma khong cai gi. Khi nao ung dung AR dien thoai hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi ban can siluet tren san phong khach; viewer nay khong bao gio tuyen bo dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di ben xuong Dilophosaurus dung va cam nhan quy mo phong ma man hinh khong dat. Anh tinh chi thang khi ban can mot khung chia se duy nhat khong tuong tac.
