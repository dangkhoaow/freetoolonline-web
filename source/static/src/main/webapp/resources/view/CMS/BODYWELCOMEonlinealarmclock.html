Online Alarm Clock

Last reviewed: 2026-07-02

This alarm clock runs entirely in your browser: pick a time, click Set alarm, and a bold on-screen message appears the moment the clock reaches it. There is nothing to download, install, or sign up for.

The page also shows the current time continuously in HH:MM:SS format with today's date, so you can check the time and set the alarm from the same screen. Set alarm reads the time field and shows a confirmation line; Clear alarm cancels the alarm and resets the status back to "No alarm set."

The alarm is driven by your browser's own clock, not a server or a background service, so there is no upload and no account. It does not play a sound or send a system notification when it fires - watch the tab for the on-screen "Alarm!" message. The tab also has to stay open: closing it or navigating away stops the check, since there is no background process keeping it running.

Use it for things like a short reminder while you are already at the computer, a wake-up check during a nap, or a cue to switch tasks at a set time - anywhere a visual, on-screen alert is enough and you do not need a phone-style push notification.

Prefer a countdown instead of a fixed time, or just want to watch the clock without setting anything? The companion countdown timer counts down from a duration you choose, and the digital clock and analog clock both show the current time as a live display. Nothing you do on this page is uploaded or stored; there is no account and no tracking.
