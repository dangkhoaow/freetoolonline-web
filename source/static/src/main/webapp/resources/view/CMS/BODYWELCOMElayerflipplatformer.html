Layer Flip Platformer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Press Play and a dimension-toggle platformer loads in the tab. Move with A or D, jump with W or Up, and press Space to flip light and dark layers so platforms and spikes swap mid-jump across 25 hand-crafted stages.

StepAction
LaunchPlay on this page to inject the iframe (~44 KB)
MoveA/D or Left/Right to run; W or Up to jump
FlipSpace (or gamepad button 1) toggles the active layer
ProgressSession only - no localStorage or cookies
