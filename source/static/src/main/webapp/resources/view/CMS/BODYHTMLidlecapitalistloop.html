IDLE CAPITALIST LOOP

Idle empire from the open-source Cursor Arcade capitalist module (MIT). About ~87 KB downloads once from this site when you press Play. Your empire save stays in your browser.

Click businesses to buy and collect. Hire managers for auto-run. Space pauses. Keys 1/2/3 switch Earth/Moon/Mars. b/u/a switch upgrade tabs.

Press Play to load the game.
