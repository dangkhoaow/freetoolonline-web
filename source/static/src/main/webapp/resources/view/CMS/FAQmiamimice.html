Frequently Asked Questions

What is Miami Mice?

A free browser beat-em-up with bone-animated cat fighters on a procedural canvas stage.

How do I play?

Press Play, wait for the title screen, then choose START or HORDE MODE. Move with WASD, punch with H, kick with J, and block with B.

Does a gamepad work?

Yes. Left stick moves. Face buttons map to kick, punch, and block (see the on-screen control tip).

What is Horde mode?

An endless wave mode started from the HORDE MODE button on the title screen.

Does it save progress?

No. Play is session-only. There is no localStorage save key in this build.

How big is the download?

About ~0.11 MB of HTML, CSS, and JS after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from DerBenniBanni/js13k2025_miami_mice (MIT). SoundBox music/SFX stay in-bundle.
