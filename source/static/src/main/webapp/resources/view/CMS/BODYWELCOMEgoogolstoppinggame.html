Welcome to Googol Stopping Game - a free browser optimal-stopping puzzle you can play without an account or install. Click numbered tiles that hide enormous values, keep your last pick, and press I'm done when you believe you hold the biggest number of the set. A Computer button demonstrates the classic sample-then-threshold strategy (about n/e). About ~8 KB after Play, session-only, adapted from Victor Ribeiro's MIT googol.
