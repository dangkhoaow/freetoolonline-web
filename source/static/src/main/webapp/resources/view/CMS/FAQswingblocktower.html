Frequently Asked Questions

What is Swing Block Tower?

A free browser stacking game. Drop crane-hung blocks onto a tower and line up edges for score.

How do I play?

Press Play, tap Start inside the game, then tap or click to drop each swinging block when it aligns with the tower.

How many misses are allowed?

Three missed drops end the run. Three heart icons on screen track remaining chances.

Does it save progress?

No. This build has no localStorage. Each run starts fresh after reload.

How big is the download?

About ~2.0 MB of canvas logic, PNG art, and audio after you press Play.

Is there sound?

Yes. Background music and drop effects play when sound is enabled in the engine options.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from iamkun/tower_game (MIT, Copyright (c) 2018 BMQB, Inc). This build adds robots noindex and removes analytics.
