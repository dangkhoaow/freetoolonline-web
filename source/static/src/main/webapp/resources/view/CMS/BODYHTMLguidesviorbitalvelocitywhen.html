Khi Nao Dung Orbital Velocity 3D Explorer - Phien Tot Nhat va Gioi Han

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Mo Orbital Velocity 3D Explorer khi ban can toc do Vis-viva doc theo elip lech tam - v = sqrt(GM(2/r - 1/a)) voi preset toc do trung binh Sao Thuy ~47.36, Trai Dat ~29.78 va Sao Hai Vuong ~5.43 km/s - ma khong can bo truyen n vat the hay may ke hoach delta-v ten lua.


Demo lop hoc nam phut

Demo lop hoc nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Orbital Velocity 3D Explorer o preset Trai Dat, keo do lech tam len, va xem mui ten toc do vang lon gan periapsis xanh va nho gan apoapsis do - ro hon chi viet Vis-viva tren bang.


Toc do trung binh trong doi ngoai trong mot bang

Hieu biet toc do trung binh trong doi ngoai van doc duoc khi ban chuyen Sao Thuy (~47.36 km/s) roi Sao Hai Vuong (~5.43 km/s) va giu doc live km/s tren bang su kien. Kich thuoc quy dao duoc phong theo day hoc; so tren bang la toc do trung binh cong bo.


Phien Orbital Velocity 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Phien Orbital Velocity 3D Explorer khong phu hop la tich hop n vat the day du, tai ephemeris JPL truc tiep, hay ngan sach delta-v phong. Dung Kepler Orbits 3D cho dinh luat elip hoac Escape Velocity 3D cho quy dao thoat. Di qua dieu khien trong huong dan tung buoc Orbital Velocity 3D Explorer.
