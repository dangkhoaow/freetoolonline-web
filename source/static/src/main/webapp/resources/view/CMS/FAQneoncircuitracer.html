What controls does Neon Circuit Racer use?

Use A and D or on-screen lane buttons to change lanes. Hold W or Space (or the nitro button) for nitro boost.

Does the game save progress?

Yes. Your best score and sound preference persist in localStorage on this device.

How big is the download?

About ~43 KB for the iframe bundle after you press Play.

Do I need an account?

No account or server login. The racer runs fully in your browser.
