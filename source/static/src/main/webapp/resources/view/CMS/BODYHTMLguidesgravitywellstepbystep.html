How to Use Gravity Well 3D Explorer - Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Start on Earth (11.2 km/s), switch to Moon / Jupiter / Sun to watch the embedding sheet deepen, then toggle Show r_s (Sun) to read the ~2.95 km Schwarzschild literacy without leaving the browser.

  1. Open Gravity Well 3D Explorer and wait for the sheet to paint.
  2. Drag to orbit, scroll or pinch to zoom, and keep the facts panel visible on wide screens.
  3. Tap Moon 2.38, Earth 11.2, Jupiter 59.5, and Sun 617.5 to compare well depth.
  4. Optional: Show r_s (Sun) and Exaggerated depth - remember on-screen size is teaching scale.
  5. Read the table values aloud so escape speeds stay the reliable takeaway.
