Base64 Encoder Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

Base64 Encoder runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Base64 Encoder. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it to inspect a Base64-encoded token, prep a data URI, or decode a payload from an API response.


Step 3 - get the result

Encoding uses the browser's own btoa/atob (UTF-8 safe) and runs locally - no upload and no account.

Nothing you type or decode is saved; refreshing the page clears the box.
