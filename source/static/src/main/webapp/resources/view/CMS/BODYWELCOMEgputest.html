Last reviewed: 2026-07-05

See what your browser's graphics stack can do - WebGL 1 and 2 availability, the renderer name your browser reports, texture and viewport limits, and WebGPU presence.

The score reflects sustained geometry throughput - tiers advance while the median holds 55 FPS, and frame rates above 60 count as 60 so high-refresh screens stay roughly comparable.

It is honest about limits - an indicative browser benchmark, not a lab-grade suite; device heat, battery mode, and other tabs shift the numbers, so compare scores only between runs on the same device and browser.
