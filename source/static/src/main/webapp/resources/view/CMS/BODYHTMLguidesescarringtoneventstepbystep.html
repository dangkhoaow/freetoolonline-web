Como explorar Carrington Event 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the real 1859 storm unfold.

  1. Open /space-3d/carrington-event.html and wait for the Sun and Earth to appear.
  2. Press Play and watch the flare flash on the Sun.
  3. Watch the eruption cross toward Earth and the aurora ring spread on impact.
  4. Toggle the normal-aurora comparison ring to see how far past normal the 1859 storm reached.
  5. Adjust the speed slider or drag to orbit the scene.
