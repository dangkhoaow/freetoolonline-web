How to Explore Apollo Free-Return Trajectory 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Apollo Free-Return Trajectory 3D Explorer, toggle free-return vs hybrid, then scrub phase through the lunar flyby to see whether the craft aims at Earth or misses with a high perigee.

  1. Open /space-3d/apollo-free-return-trajectory.html and wait for Earth, Moon, and the yellow craft marker to paint.
  2. Leave Free-return selected and press Play, or drag Phase from about 10% outbound toward 50% at the Moon.
  3. Watch the green path swing behind the Moon and return near Earth's wireframe atmosphere shell.
  4. Click Hybrid (Apollo 13 MCC-2 style) and scrub past 60% - the orange path misses Earth with a high-perigee miss.
  5. Read the facts panel for ~110 km early free-return PC, ~250 km Apollo 13 restored PC, 400,171 km furthest Earth distance, ~2,500 nmi hybrid perigee, and the 34.23 s DPS-1 burn.
