TDEE & Calorie Calculator Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-24

TDEE & Calorie Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then read the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to TDEE & Calorie Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Pick your sex, enter age, weight, and height (metric or imperial), then choose an activity level from the dropdown.


Step 3 - read the result

BMR, TDEE, and the loss/gain calorie targets update on the same page as you change any field - no button, and nothing is uploaded.

Uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula for BMR, then applies an activity multiplier to estimate total daily energy expenditure (TDEE), with a loss and gain target 500 kcal/day either side of maintenance.
