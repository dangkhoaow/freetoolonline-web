Cuando jugar Schematic Factory Line

Schematic Factory Line loads about ~456 KB in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Best for 20-60 minute idle sessions when you want a schematic factory loop instead of a short arcade or bullet-hell run.

Idle depth

Production continues offline up to eight hours after you leave the tab.

Meta layers

Research, Patents, and Legacy Marks reward repeat prestige runs.

Desktop first

Mouse and touch both work; the layout scales inside the iframe.
