Medieval Wall Defense

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play and a full incremental defense session loads in the tab. You defend a castle wall against marching waves while gold funds heroes, skills, and research nodes. Between runs you can bank Crowns for prestige bonuses that carry into the next attempt.

Controls at a glance:

ActionInput
Place / selectClick or tap on the field
Skills 1-4Number keys or on-screen buttons
PauseSpace
Research screenR
End runQ
MuteM
Close panelsEsc

Progress, settings, and prestige data save locally under ftol:medievalwalldefense:* keys in your browser only. The game is adapted from Matt's open-source Crownfall project (MIT license); credits and license text ship next to the game files.
