How to Explore Dark Matter Rotation Curves 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Dark Matter Rotation Curves 3D Explorer, drag the radius slider across the disk, and toggle the dark-matter halo to see what each part of the scene represents.

  1. Open /space-3d/dark-matter-rotation-curves.html and wait for the disk of points to paint.
  2. Drag the radius slider to about 8 kpc - close to the Sun's own distance from the galactic center.
  3. Read the facts panel: the observed speed near 220-240 km/s against the visible-mass-only prediction at that radius.
  4. Drag the slider out to 20-25 kpc and watch the gap between the two curves widen.
  5. Click "Hide dark-matter halo (inferred)" to see the scene without the faint halo, then click again to bring it back.
