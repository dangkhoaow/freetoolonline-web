Doc bang su that Deinonychus va bam mot phan co the

Bang liet ke chieu dai khoang 3.3-3.4 m, chieu cao hong khoang 0.87 m, can nang khoang 60-100 kg tuy phuong phap (Paul 1988/2016 khoang 60-73 kg vs Campione et al. 2014 khoang 100 kg), mong liem lon co the rut o ngon thu hai moi chan giu nang de bao le, duoi cung nhu doi trong dong, cua so ~115-93.9 Mya Creta Som den dau Creta Muon o Formation Cloverly (Montana/Wyoming) va Antlers (Oklahoma/Utah/Texas), va lich su dat ten - John Ostrom dat ten chi nam 1969 (loai antirrhopus = doi trong) tu vat lieu Yale Peabody tu 1964; phat hien 1931 cua Barnum Brown gan Billings, Montana chua bao gio duoc mo ta chinh thuc. Lon hon nhieu so voi Velociraptor da ship (khoang 2 m / 0.5 m hong). Velociraptor tren man Jurassic Park duoc mo phong theo kich thuoc va ti le Deinonychus. Rang roi voi di tich Tenontosaurus goi y hanh vi bay dan co the, khong phai su that chot. Long vu suy ra tu dromaeosaurid ho hang, khong quan sat truc tiep tren Deinonychus, va khong render. Bam ngan - khong keo - tren dau, than, co, duoi, chan hoac tay hien su that fossil; bam cho trong de quay lai phieu.
