Mono Stack Blocks is a monochrome line-clear stack game in the browser: stack tetrominoes on a 10x20 grid, clear rows, use hold and ghost preview, and chase level-ups with local high scores. About ~58 KB after Play, no install, no account.

MONO STACK BLOCKS

Line-clear stack from the open-source Cursor Arcade blocks module (MIT). About ~58 KB downloads once from this site when you press Play. High scores stay in your browser.

Arrows or WASD move and rotate. Space hard drop. C or Shift hold. P pause. R restart. Keyboard required - no touch HUD.

Press Play to load the game.
Scores use ftol:monostackblocks:storage.
