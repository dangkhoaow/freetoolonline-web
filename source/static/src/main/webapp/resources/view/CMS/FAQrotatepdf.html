Frequently Asked Questions

What does Rotate PDF do?

Rotate every page of a PDF by 90, 180, or 270 degrees, right in the browser.

When should I reach for rotate pdf?

The angle applies uniformly to the whole document; it does not support rotating individual pages to different angles.

What complementary tools work well alongside rotate pdf?

To split, merge, or compress the PDF instead, see the related-tools section.
