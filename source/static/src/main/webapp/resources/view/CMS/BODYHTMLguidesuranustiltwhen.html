When to Use Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Open the Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer when you need the ~97.8 deg sideways tilt and ~84 yr year - and skip it when you need Earth seasons or gas-giant band weather.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer, toggle Compare Earth, and pause so students see the orange 97.8 deg axis lying almost in the orbital plane next to Earth's upright 23.44 deg ghost.


Obliquity literacy in Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer

Obliquity literacy in Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer stays readable when you keep the facts panel open for 97.8 deg and 84 yr while scrubbing orbit year to see the poles trade sunward facing.


Sessions Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer does not fit are N-body ephemerides, atmosphere modeling, or ring-particle dynamics. Use Seasons Earth 3D for Earth's seasons or Gas Giant Atmosphere 3D for band weather. Walk the controls in the Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer step-by-step guide.
