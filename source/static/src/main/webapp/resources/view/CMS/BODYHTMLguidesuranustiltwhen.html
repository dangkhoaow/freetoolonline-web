When to use Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer

Use the Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer when you need the ~97.8 deg sideways tilt and ~84 yr year - and skip it when you need Earth seasons or gas-giant band weather.

  • Classroom demos of why Uranus is called the sideways planet
  • Contrasting 97.8 deg with Earth's 23.44 deg obliquity before ice-giant units
  • One-screen refresher on decades-long Uranus seasons without a textbook figure

This page does not run N-body gravity, does not model atmospheres, and exaggerates ring clarity for teaching.
