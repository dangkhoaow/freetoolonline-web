How to explore Escape Velocity 3D Explorer

Open the Escape Velocity 3D Explorer, pick Moon Earth Mars Jupiter or Sun, and watch four trajectory classes animate with NASA/SSD escape velocities.

  1. Open /space-3d/escape-velocity.html and wait for the body plus four colored paths to paint.
  2. Leave the body on Earth - read v_esc 11.2 km/s and circular orbit 7.92 km/s (= 11.2/sqrt(2)) in the facts panel.
  3. Watch orange sub-orbital fall back, green circular close, yellow escape bend outward, purple hyperbolic curve steeper.
  4. Switch the dropdown to Moon (2.38 km/s) or Jupiter (59.5 km/s) - the same four trajectory families apply at each body.
  5. Press Pause paths to freeze markers, then Trajectory table for all four speed rules at once.
