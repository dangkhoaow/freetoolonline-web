In 2011 astronomers confirmed a planet that orbits not one star, but two - the first real-life echo of Luke Skywalker's fictional Tatooine. Kepler-16b circles a pair of stars that orbit each other every 41 days, while the planet itself takes 228.8 days to loop around both. Press play to watch the orange and red stars swing around their shared center while the tan planet orbits farther out, then turn on Orbits to see the paths traced out.
