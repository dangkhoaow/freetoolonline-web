Khi nao viewer Triceratops tren trinh duyet phu hop

Chon Triceratops tren trinh duyet khi ban muon keo-quay quanh mot ceratopsian bo tu chan, Size vs human voi chieu dai dai dien khoang 8 m (khoang cong bo khoang 8-9 m; mot so nguon ghi 6-8.5 m cho loai type), va cham frill, dau co sung, hoac chan ma khong cai dat. Khong gi ve lan ghe tham duoc gui len may chu - tot cho laptop lop hoc hoac kiem tra nhanh so lieu Tao Bach Thu o tay Bac My (68-66 Mya; dat ten 1889 boi Othniel Charles Marsh sau holotype Lance Formation 1888).


Khi nao ung dung AR hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi ban can hinh bong tren san phong khach; viewer nay khong bao gio claim dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di ben xuong ceratopsian ghep va cam nhan quy mo phong - gom mau nhu Big John khoang 8 m. Anh tinh chi thang khi ban can mot khung chia se khong tuong tac.


So sanh Triceratops nay khong phai gi

Day la bang trade-off cho viewer da ship - khong xep hang thuong hieu AR, khong biet tinh nang AR trang khong co, va khong ket luan chuc nang frill hay khong che do bay dan / chien dau sung. Huong dan dieu khien xem cach xem Triceratops trong 3D. Chieu dai va can nang ben nguoi 1.8 m xem so sanh kich thuoc Triceratops.
