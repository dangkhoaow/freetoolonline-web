Frequently Asked Questions

What does Head Soccer Arena do?

It is a physics head-soccer duel: move and kick with keyboard controls, play against the CPU or a second player on the same keyboard, score goals, and win by goals-to-win or match timer.

Does it work on a phone?

The page loads on a phone, but players need a keyboard (A/D/W/S/Q and arrows). A desktop or laptop keyboard is the practical way to play.

Is anything saved in my browser?

Yes. High scores and settings use localStorage key ftol:headsoccerarena:storage. Reset all high scores in Settings clears the score map. Nothing is uploaded.

How big is the download?

About ~88 KB of HTML, CSS, and JS after you press Play. The browser caches it for later visits on the same device.

How do I play?

P1: A/D move, W jump, S kick, Q power shot. P2: arrow keys move, Up jump, Down kick, / power shot. Pick up F/I/G/M power-ups. Space pauses, R restarts, Esc opens the menu. Settings sets mode, match length, and goals to win.

Is this open source?

Yes. It is adapted from the soccer module in Cursor Arcade by flancast90 (flancast90/cursor-arcade-games) under the MIT license. This site build stubs the VS Code webview API and ships LICENSE and CREDITS.
