Frequently Asked Questions

Does my chat leave my device?

No. The model runs in your browser and your messages stay on your device.

Why is there a large download the first time?

The AI model weights (hundreds of MB) download once from the model registry and are then cached by your browser, so later visits start faster. The model is not bundled with the page.

Which browsers work?

Any browser with WebGPU - the latest Chrome or Edge on desktop, or Chrome on Android. Browsers without WebGPU show a notice instead; there is no CPU fallback.

Which model should I pick?

TinyLlama 1.1B is the smallest and fastest to load; Llama 3.2 3B and Phi-3 mini answer better but download more. You can switch models at any time.
