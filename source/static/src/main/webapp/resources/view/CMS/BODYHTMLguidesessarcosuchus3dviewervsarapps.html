When the Sarcosuchus browser viewer fits

Pick the browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a sprawled-gait crocodyliform with Size vs human at the honest 9.5 m panel length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything.

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for real mounts.
