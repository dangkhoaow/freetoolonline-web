When the Barosaurus browser viewer fits

Pick the browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a long-necked diplodocid sauropod with Size vs human at the honest 26 m panel length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for real mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame.


What this Barosaurus comparison is not

This comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - not an AR brand ranking, no invented AR features, no single mass outside published ranges (panel 17 t disclosed), it does not use ~45 m / ~60 t Supersaurus-reattributed estimates as Barosaurus figures; does not assert rearing-to-15-m head height as a measured fact, and no game score.
