Frequently Asked Questions

What does Online Stopwatch do?

Times an activity to the hundredth of a second, with Start, Stop, Lap, and Reset controls and an ordered list of recorded laps.

Does clicking Lap stop the clock?

No - Lap only records the current elapsed time into the list below. The stopwatch keeps counting up so you can capture as many splits as you need.

Does the stopwatch keep running if I close the tab?

No. Timing runs only while the tab stays open - there is no background process, so closing the tab or navigating away stops the count.

What complementary tools work well alongside online stopwatch?

The countdown timer counts down from a duration you set, and the digital clock and analog clock both show the current time as a live display.
