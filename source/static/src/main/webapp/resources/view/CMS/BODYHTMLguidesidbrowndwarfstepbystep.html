Cara menjelajah Brown Dwarf 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and drag the mass slider across the real fusion boundaries.

  1. Open /space-3d/brown-dwarf.html and wait for the sphere and the mass ruler to appear.
  2. Drag the mass slider slowly from low to high and watch the color change.
  3. Notice how little the sphere's size changes even as the mass climbs.
  4. Watch the status line switch from gas giant to brown dwarf to star as you cross 13 and 80 Jupiter masses.
  5. Press Play to auto-sweep, or toggle Realistic dim to see the true, much fainter glow.
