Color Contrast Checker (WCAG) Vs Alternatives

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Color Contrast Checker (WCAG) is one of three common ways to check contrast: this browser tool, a desktop accessibility app, or an upload-based online checker. Each trades off differently on where your colors go and what you need installed.
