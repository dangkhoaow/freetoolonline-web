Quet Ma QR - Tung Buoc

Cong cu Quet Ma QR chay trong ba buoc: mo cong cu, cung cap dau vao, lay ket qua. Trang nay huong dan tung buoc can thuc hien.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-04

freetoolonline.com Editorial Team

Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Truy cap vao Quet Ma QR. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.

Buoc 2 - cung cap dau vao

Khi ma chua mot lien ket, cong cu se hien thi URL chinh xac la mot lien ket co the nhan TRUOC khi ban truy cap - cach de kiem tra ma QR kha nghi la phishing.

Buoc 3 - lay ket qua

Sao chep gia tri da giai ma chi voi mot clic - tien loi cho mat khau wifi, so seri, ma cupon hoac tham chieu thanh toan dai.

Anh chup do phan giai cao tren dien thoai van hoat dong - anh lon duoc tu dong thu nho truoc khi giai ma, ngay ca anh 12MP cung giai ma nhanh.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Dinh dang hinh anh nao duoc ho tro?

JPEG, PNG va phan lon cac dinh dang hinh anh pho bien deu hoat dong. Anh dien thoai do phan giai cao cung duoc chap nhan.

Neu ma QR bi mo thi sao?

Cong cu se huong dan ban thu lai voi hinh anh ro hon hoac lon hon thay vi loi im lang.
