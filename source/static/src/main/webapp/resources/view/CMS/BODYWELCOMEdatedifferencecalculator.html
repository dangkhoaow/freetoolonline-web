Date Difference Calculator

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Work out the number of days between two dates.

Use it to count days until a deadline, measure a duration, or check how long ago something happened.

The result also shows a weeks-and-days breakdown, not just the raw day count, and it works in either direction - if the end date is before the start date, the tool still gives you the day count and says so.

Does not provide medical or financial advice - results are general estimates only, and both dates are treated as midnight local time, so the count is a calendar-date difference rather than a precise elapsed-time count across time zones.

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.
