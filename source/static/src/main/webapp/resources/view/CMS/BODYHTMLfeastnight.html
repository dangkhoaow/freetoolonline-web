Feast Night is a free browser medieval first-person game. You are Brother Doen at a village feast: walk with WASD, look with the mouse, talk by nodding or shaking your head, and save souls when guests turn demonic. About ~17 KB, no install, no account.

FEAST NIGHT

Walk a medieval feast, answer with nod or shake, and save souls. Adapted from FEAST NIGHT by Almar (MIT). About ~17 KB, downloads once when you press Play.

Click the canvas to start, WASD or arrows to walk, mouse to look, nod or shake for yes/no, click to fire when demons appear. Best with a mouse and keyboard.

Press Play to load the game.
Session-only play - competition build has no save data.
