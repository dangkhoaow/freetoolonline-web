Tra loi nhanh. Mo Noi Chuyen Thanh Van Ban, nhan Start listening, cho phep truy cap micro, noi va nhan Stop. Van ban xuat hien tren trinh duyet - khong co gi duoc gui len may chu.

Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Mo Noi Chuyen Thanh Van Ban. Khong can cai dat hoac dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.

Buoc 2 - bat dau nghe

Nhan Start listening. Trinh duyet se yeu cau quyen truy cap micro - nhan Cho phep. Phien am bat dau ngay sau khi cap quyen.

Buoc 3 - noi

Noi binh thuong. Van ban xuat hien trong khu vuc phien am theo thoi gian thuc. Ket qua tam thoi hien thi khi ban noi; van ban cuoi cung duoc luu khi ban dung lai.

Buoc 4 - nhan ket qua

Nhan Stop khi hoan tat. Su dung nut Download .txt de luu van ban. Neu can chieu nguoc lai, xem cong cu Chuyen Van Ban Thanh Giong Noi trong phan cong cu lien quan.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Giong noi cua toi co duoc gui len may chu khong?

Khong. Cong cu su dung API nhan dang giong noi cua trinh duyet - moi thu dien ra tren thiet bi cua ban.

Co hoat dong tren Firefox khong?

Khong. Nhan dang giong noi yeu cau trinh duyet dua tren Chromium (Chrome, Edge) hoac Safari. Firefox khong ho tro API nay.

Can tai khoan hoac cai dat khong?

Khong. Cong cu chay truc tiep tren trinh duyet, khong can cai dat hay tai khoan.
