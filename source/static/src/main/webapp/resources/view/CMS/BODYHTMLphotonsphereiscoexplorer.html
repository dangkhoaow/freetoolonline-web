About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Drag the spin slider to change a*.

Every radius above is computed live from the exact Kerr black-hole solution (Bardeen, Press & Teukolsky 1972) at the spin you pick - not looked up from a table. At zero spin the numbers match the textbook Schwarzschild values (photon sphere 1.5 Rs, ISCO 3 Rs); as spin rises toward the astrophysical Thorne limit, the prograde rings pull in toward the horizon while the retrograde rings push outward.
