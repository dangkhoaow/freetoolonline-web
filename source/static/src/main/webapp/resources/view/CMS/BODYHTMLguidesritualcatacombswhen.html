When to Play Ritual Catacombs

Ritual Catacombs fits short desktop sessions where you want a compact WebGL horror shooter - about ~94 KB after Play, no install.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Horror coffee breaks on desktop

Each level is a self-contained catacomb room chain. Gather shards, open the runic door, and move on before enemies overwhelm you.

When you want procedural audio

All music and gunfire use the Web Audio API - no MP3 downloads. Ambience starts after your first click inside the frame.

When to pick another game

Want stealth without shooting? Use Floor Thirteen Horror. Want a voxel FPS arena? Use Voxel FPS Arena.
