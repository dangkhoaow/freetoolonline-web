Chia Video Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Chia Video chay theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, cung cap dau vao, roi tai ket qua. Trang nay giai thich moi buoc can gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Mo Chia Video. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - cung cap dau vao

Tai file video len, roi nhap diem chia vao o "Split at" - ca giay don gian ("90") va dang mm:ss ("1:30") deu dung duoc. Diem do la noi cat xay ra.


Buoc 3 - nhan ket qua

Cong cu tao ra hai doan video rieng: toan bo phan truoc diem chia va toan bo phan sau. Moi doan hien thi thanh mot lien ket tai rieng - khong co buoc dong goi zip, va file goc khong duoc giu lai sau khi xu ly xong.
