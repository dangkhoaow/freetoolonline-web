W / Up accelerates, S / Down brakes, A-D / Left-Right steer - or hold the on-screen pedals. Cruise freely, or start a checkpoint run and follow the arrow over the car.

In a checkpoint run you start with 60 seconds; the arrow over your car points to a glowing ring at an intersection, and every ring you reach scores one and adds 8 seconds.

Bumping a building or a passing car just slows you down with a soft bounce - there is no damage, so you can drive as badly as you like.
