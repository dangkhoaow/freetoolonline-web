How to Use Europa Subsurface Ocean 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Slide Peel shell from 0 to 100 percent and read the real published depth figures in the facts panel as the ice, ocean, mantle, and core are exposed in order.

  1. Open Europa Subsurface Ocean 3D Explorer and wait for the intact icy moon to paint.
  2. Drag to orbit the view; keep the facts panel visible on the right.
  3. Slide Peel shell past 25 percent and watch the ice shell wedge open, revealing the full ocean layer underneath.
  4. Keep sliding past 50 and 75 percent to expose the rocky mantle, then the metallic core, one layer at a time.
  5. Check the Clipper flyby tracks box to see six illustrative ground-track arcs on the ice shell.
  6. Reset Peel shell to 0 and use the four quick buttons - Ice shell, Ocean, Mantle, Core - to jump straight to any layer.
