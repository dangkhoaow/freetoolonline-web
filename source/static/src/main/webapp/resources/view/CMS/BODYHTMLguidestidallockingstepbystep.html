Tidal Locking 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

The Tidal Locking 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit Earth and the Moon, scroll to zoom, and press Play orbit. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - confirm Tidally locked

Leave Tidally locked highlighted. Watch the near-side marker stay Earth-facing while the Moon orbits. The panel lists spin = orbit 27.32 days.


Step 2 - Unlocked spin contrast

Click Unlocked spin and scrub the orbit phase. The far side rotates into view - the didactic contrast that makes synchronous rotation obvious.


Step 3 - read tidal rates

Read lunar recession 3.8 cm/yr and Earth day lengthening 1.7 ms/century in the facts table while fullscreen keeps controls visible.
