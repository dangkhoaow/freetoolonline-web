Open the Kuiper-Guertel und Oort-Wolke 3D Explorer, read the 30-50 AU and 2,000-100,000 AU figures, and switch between Kuiper focus and the Oort shell.

  1. Open /space-3d/kuiper-belt-oort-cloud.html and wait for the green Kuiper disk and Pluto marker.
  2. Read the facts panel: Kuiper 30-50 AU, Pluto 39.5 AU, Oort ~2,000-100,000 AU.
  3. Switch View to Oort shell, then Both.
  4. Scrub Zoom, or press Pause/Play spin.
  5. Compare with Asteroid Belt 3D when you need the inner main belt instead.
