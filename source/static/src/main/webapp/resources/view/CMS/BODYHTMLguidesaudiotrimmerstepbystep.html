Audio Trimmer Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-05

Audio Trimmer runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Open Audio Trimmer in your browser tab. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Provide the audio file plus a start and end time. Trimming does not re-encode - the original quality and format are kept.


Step 3 - get the result

The trimmed audio result downloads once processing finishes in this browser tab; the file is never uploaded to a server, stored beyond the page session, or sent to any analytics or tracking service, and no account is required.

For converting to a different format instead, see the related-tools section.
