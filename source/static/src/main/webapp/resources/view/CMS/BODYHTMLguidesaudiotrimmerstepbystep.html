Audio Trimmer Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-05

Audio Trimmer runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Audio Trimmer. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Trimming does not re-encode - the original quality and format are kept.


Step 3 - get the result

The file is processed in this browser tab and is never uploaded to a server.

For converting to a different format instead, see the related-tools section.
