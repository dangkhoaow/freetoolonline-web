Compound Interest Calculator

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Project how a starting balance and regular contributions grow with compound interest over time.

Enter the starting principal, monthly contribution, annual rate, term, and compounding frequency; the final value, total contributed, and interest earned update as you type - there is no submit button.

Use it to estimate a savings or retirement balance, or compare how compounding frequency changes the result.

Output is a single USD figure with no growth chart or export - for a jurisdiction-specific plan that accounts for taxes and inflation, use this as a quick sanity check, then talk to a licensed advisor.

The math runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.
