This page renders a Majungasaurus as a 3D model you can spin in the browser - drag to rotate, scroll or pinch to zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person beside it for scale, and click the snout, skull horn, or a hind leg to read a fact about that part.

Majungasaurus lived in the Late Cretaceous, about 70 to 66 million years ago, in Madagascar. Typical adults measured about 5.6 to 7 m long and about 0.75 to 1.1 tonnes. It is an abelisaurid with a short, broad snout and a single rounded horn on the skull roof - different from Carnotaurus, which has paired brow horns and lived in South America.
