How to Explore Saros Cycle Eclipse Explorer 3D

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Saros Cycle Eclipse Explorer 3D, step through the series with the slider or Play stepping, and watch each eclipse's ground track march about 120 degrees west of the last.

  1. Open /space-3d/saros-cycle.html and wait for Earth to render.
  2. Drag the eclipse-index slider from 0 to 1 and watch the bright track shift about 120 degrees west of the dim starting track.
  3. Press "Show all tracks so far" to see every step in the series at once, marching westward together.
  4. Press "Jump 3 steps (exeligmos)" to watch the track return near its starting longitude after three steps.
  5. Press "Play stepping" to run automatically through the whole series.
  6. Read the facts panel for elapsed time, cumulative westward shift, and how long a full series lasts.
