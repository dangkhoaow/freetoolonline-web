Como explorar Shepherd Moons 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch two moons herd a ring, then let it fall apart.

  1. Open /space-3d/shepherd-moons.html and wait for the planet, the ring and the two moons.
  2. Note the thin ring held between the inner and outer shepherd moons.
  3. Press the shepherds toggle to off and watch the ring shear and spread.
  4. Toggle it back on and see the band snap into a crisp ringlet.
  5. Use the speed slider; read the panel for the Prometheus, Pandora and epsilon figures.
