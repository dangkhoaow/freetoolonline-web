How to explore Cosmic Ray Air Shower 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch a cosmic ray cascade from the top of the atmosphere down to the ground.

  1. Open /space-3d/cosmic-ray-shower.html and wait for the scene.
  2. Watch the primary track hit the flash point, then the cascade branch downward.
  3. Read the facts panel: primary energy, first-interaction height, and ground muon rate.
  4. Follow the colours: orange hadronic core, cyan electromagnetic sub-shower, magenta muons to the ground.
  5. Pause the cascade or press Replay to fire a fresh shower.
