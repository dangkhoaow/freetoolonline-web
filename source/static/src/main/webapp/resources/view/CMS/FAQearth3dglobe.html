Frequently Asked Questions

What does Earth 3D Globe - Live Day & Night Map do?

See at a glance where on Earth it is day and where it is night RIGHT NOW - the lit half of the globe follows the real sun, computed from your device's clock.

When should I reach for earth 3d globe - live day & night map?

The time slider previews the day/night line up to 12 hours ahead or behind - watch dawn sweep across a continent - and the Now button snaps back to live.

What complementary tools work well alongside earth 3d globe - live day & night map?

A live clock line shows your local time and UTC before the 3D engine even finishes loading, and the panel lists the sun's current overhead point.
