Server Survival TD is a free browser 3D tower defense where you play as a cloud architect. Place CDN, database, firewall, and related services, connect them, and keep reputation from collapsing under traffic surges and malicious requests. Progress saves under ftol:serversurvivaltd keys. About ~13 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a classic map-draft TD instead? Try Neon Tower Rush. Prefer a short plant-and-wave session? Try Garden Defense.

SERVER SURVIVAL TD

Place cloud services, connect traffic paths, and survive RPS surges. Adapted from pshenok/server-survival (MIT). Progress uses ftol:serversurvivaltd:* keys. About ~13 MB.

Mouse works best for placing and wiring services. First load is larger because audio and the 3D engine ship together.

Press Play to load the game.
Save data, sound prefs, locale, and campaign progress stay on this device under ftol:serversurvivaltd keys.
