Como explorar Cepheid Variable Star 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Cepheid Variable Star 3D Explorer, watch the pulse, then switch period presets to compare Leavitt Law brightness.

  1. Open /space-3d/cepheid-variable.html and wait for the pulsing star.
  2. Read the facts panel: formula, computed M_V, Delta Cephei figures.
  3. Press Delta Cephei, short period, and long period to compare.
  4. Pause to freeze the light curve strip.
  5. Compare with Cosmic Distance Ladder 3D for the wider ladder, or Stellar Magnitude 3D for brightness ranking.
