Como jugar Neon Deep Space - paso a paso

The Neon Deep Space page loads a ~152 KB geometry-war shooter in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, Start a run, fly with WASD, and spend energy in the shop between deaths.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~152 KB). Canvas #gameCanvas boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - start and move

Click Start. WASD moves the ship. Mouse aims; left click fires.

Step 3 - use skills

Shift dashes. Space drops a bomb. F switches Blaster / Shotgun / Sniper. Esc pauses.

Step 4 - shop and upgrades

Earn energy from kills. Open the shop to buy permanent upgrades. Saves use ftol:neondeepspace:* keys.
