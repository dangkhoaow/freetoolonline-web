About this scene

The facts panel fills in when the scene loads. Try Proxima, 1 arcsec, and 0.5 arcsec presets to watch distance follow 1/p.

Blue and green sight lines run from opposite ends of Earth's orbit to the star. Star depth scales with 1/p so a larger parallax pulls the star closer on the teaching stage.

Angles are exaggerated for the camera - real stellar parallaxes sit below one arcsecond. This isolates the measurement triangle Solar System 3D Explorer does not teach.
