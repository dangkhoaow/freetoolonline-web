Khi Nao Nen Choi Claudicus Quest

Claudicus Quest la mot RPG dan quan trung co theo luot chay trong tab trinh duyet voi luu cuc bo va khong can tai khoan. Phu hop cho phien choi 20-45 phut qua Brannford, Thornwood, va Greymoor Crypt. Day la khi nao no la lua chon dung.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Khi ban muon mot RPG nho gon co luu game

Toan bo goi la ~132 KB (~105 KB game.js). Ban luu vao ftol:claudicusquest:save; Tiep tuc tren man hinh tieu de se tiep tuc luot choi cua ban. Chon Claudicus Quest khi ban muon nhiem vu va trang bi ma khong can tai lon.

Khi combat menu theo luot hop voi tam trang cua ban

Cac tran chien dung menu danh so (Tan cong, Phong thu, Thuoc, Chay, dac biet) thay vi ngam ban thoi gian thuc. Tot khi ban muon doc nhat ky combat va lap ke hoach cho luot tiep theo tren ban phim desktop.

Khi ban co ban phim san sang

Di chuyen WASD, phim so trong combat, va phim tat I/Q/M lay ban phim lam trung tam. Khong co nut cham tren man hinh, vi vay laptop hoac desktop la thiet bi thuc te.

Khi quyen rieng tu va khong CDN quan trong

Tat ca chay phia client sau lan tai mot lan: khong dang nhap, khong multiplayer, khong goi server khi choi, va khong CDN khi chay. Sprite va am thanh duoc ve va tong hop bang Canvas 2D va Web Audio.

Khi day khong phai lua chon dung

Bo qua neu ban can dieu khien cham va sandbox thi tran lon hon (thu Violence Town o ~1.1 MB voi di chuyen cham). Bo qua neu ban muon muoi lam ban do va bang trang bi (xem Pixel Realm RPG o ~3.8 MB).
