How to explore HR Diagram 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the HR Diagram 3D Explorer, highlight each stellar locus, and read the Sun at Teff ~5,772 K and L = 1 Lsun on the schematic axes.

  1. Open /space-3d/hr-diagram.html and wait for the temperature / log(L/Lsun) axes and colored point clouds.
  2. Read the facts panel: Sun Teff ~5,772 K, main sequence, giants, white dwarfs, luminosity and temperature.
  3. Press Main sequence to highlight the O/B through M dwarf band along the diagonal locus.
  4. Press Giants or White dwarfs to focus high-L giants or hot faint white-dwarf regions.
  5. Press Sun for the G-type anchor; scrub Spectral class O-M or Facts table for the teaching summary.
