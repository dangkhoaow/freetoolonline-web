When to use Precession of the Equinoxes 3D Explorer

Use the Precession of the Equinoxes 3D Explorer when you need a quick browser view of axial precession and north-star migration - and skip it when you need an ephemeris or catalog.

  • Classroom demos of why Polaris will not always be the north star
  • Linking ~25,772 yr period and ~1 deg per ~72 yr to the pole-path ring
  • One-screen refresher before seasons-earth or ecliptic-zodiac siblings

This page does not provide an ephemeris, does not place precise catalog star positions, and uses teaching markers for Polaris and Vega.
