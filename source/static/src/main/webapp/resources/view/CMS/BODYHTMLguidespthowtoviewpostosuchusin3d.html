Como ver Postosuchus em 3D

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Revisado pela equipe editorial FreetoolOnline

Postosuchus 3D Viewer runs a Late Triassic rauisuchian pseudosuchian in your browser - not a dinosaur. Drag to rotate, scroll or pinch to zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person for scale, and click head, body, or tail for a fossil-sourced fact.


Open the Postosuchus page and take the camera

Open the Postosuchus 3D Viewer and wait for the facts panel to fill. Orbit with the pointer and zoom with the scroll wheel or a two-finger pinch. Fullscreen keeps the controls visible beside the scene. After first paint a license-clean glTF may swap in from the CDN; if that load fails the page stays on the procedural silhouette.


Use the four Postosuchus controls under the canvas

Under the Postosuchus canvas, four controls handle view and scale:

  • Fullscreen - fills the display while keeping the buttons visible
  • Stop rotation / Auto-rotate - freezes or resumes auto-spin
  • Idle motion - adds subtle body sway; tap again for Stand still
  • Size vs human - shows a 1.8 m person at the honest panel length ratio (5.5 m)

Read the Postosuchus facts panel and click a body part

The Postosuchus facts panel lists published length about 4-7 m (panel baseline 5.5 m), mass about 250-1,000 kg (panel baseline 500 kg), height about 1.8 m, about 227-208 Mya Late Triassic in Texas (Post Quarry) and North Carolina, carnivore diet, described by Sankar Chatterjee in 1985. Postosuchus was a rauisuchian pseudosuchian - not a dinosaur - a crocodile-line archosaur tens of millions of years before large theropods. Gait (bipedal vs quadrupedal) remains debated; the page and model disclose that openly and show a cautious quadrupedal reconstruction only. Distinct from the shipped Sarcosuchus viewer (sprawled crocodyliform) and Dimetrodon viewer (earlier synapsid). Soft-tissue color is an artistic reconstruction. Optional CC-BY-SA 4.0 glTF (seth the yutyrannus via Printables 764414) is stylized. This model is not a fossil-accurate skeleton.


What the Postosuchus how-to guide is not

This how-to is a controls walkthrough only - it does not place the model in your room (no AR), it does not assert a settled biped vs quadruped gait, it states Postosuchus was not a dinosaur, it does not conflate Postosuchus with Sarcosuchus, and it does not invent game score or win states.
