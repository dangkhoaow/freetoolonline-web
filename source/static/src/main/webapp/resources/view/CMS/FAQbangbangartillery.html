Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bangbang Artillery?

A free browser artillery duel: you aim a cannon by angle and power and try to hit a neural-net opponent that improves after each miss.

How do I play on desktop?

Left/Right rotate the cannon. Up/Down change shot strength. Space fires. You play on the right side; the computer is on the left.

How do I play on a phone?

Drag up/down on the right half to rotate, on the left half to set power, then tap to shoot.

Does the AI save learning between visits?

No. The neural net starts fresh each round and is session-only with no localStorage.

How big is the download?

About ~35 KB of HTML, JavaScript, CSS, and a small favicon after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from bangBang by Victor Ribeiro under the MIT license. This site build strips external OG metas, adds noindex, and ships LICENSE plus CREDITS next to the engine.
