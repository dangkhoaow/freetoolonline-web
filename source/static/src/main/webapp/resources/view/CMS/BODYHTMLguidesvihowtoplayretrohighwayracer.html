Cach choi Retro Highway Racer - tung buoc

Trang Retro Highway Racer tai game lai xe ~34 KB trong iframe. Lai duong cao toc ngau nhien, nhay da va cay, dat moc thoi gian. Nhan Play de bat dau.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Chuot voi pointer lock. Giu click de phanh. Double-click de nhay. R choi lai. Cam ung: keo, cham, cham doi.

Buoc 1 - nhan Play

Play chen iframe (~34 KB). Man hinh RETRO HIGHWAY RACER. Engine Hue Jumper (Frank Force, GPL-3.0); LICENSE di kem.

Buoc 2 - bat dau cuoc dua

Click hoac cham man hinh tieu de. Tren desktop, click dau yeu cau pointer lock. Dong ho bat dau 20 giay.

Buoc 3 - lai, phanh, nhay

Di chuyen chuot de o tren duong. Giu click de phanh. Double-click de nhay cay va da. Ra le duong se cham lai.

Buoc 4 - checkpoint

Moi checkpoint (moi 100.000 don vi) them 10 giay va doi mau canh. Den moc 1000 truoc khi het gio.

Buoc 5 - choi lai

Nhan R. Khong luu gi - moi lan choi tao duong moi, khong tai khoan, am thanh hay mang sau lan tai dau.
