Void Trader vs cac game khong gian khac

Void Trader la roguelike thuong mai khong gian thoi gian thuc chay tren trinh duyet, khong cai (~50 KB, Canvas 2D). Vong lap dac trung la dao, giao dich, nang cap va quet tam khu voi mot mang. Day la cach no so voi cac game khong gian khac tren site va cac lua chon cai dat.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

vs Asteroid Blaster (shooter arcade)

Asteroid Blaster la shooter kieu vector co dien: xoay, day, pha da vo thanh manh nho. Khong co giao dich, kho hay nang cap tau - ca van chi la chien dau va diem. Void Trader nho hon khoang nam lan (~50 KB vs ~245 KB) va them kinh te tram (nam mat hang, nang cap Scout den Cruiser) tren chien dau. Chon Asteroid Blaster cho ban thuan; chon Void Trader khi ban muon gia ca va kho giua cac tran.

vs Gravity Orbit Golf (puzzle vat ly)

Gravity Orbit Golf la golf trong luc n-body (~41 KB): nem sao choi qua he mat troi procedural va ha so cu danh. Khong co cuop bien, hang giao dich hay chien dau permadeath - cu tot nhat luu tren trinh duyet. Void Trader giu tau duoi hoa luc qua tam khu, khong save. Chon Gravity Orbit Golf cho van puzzle yen; chon Void Trader cho van thuong mai mot mang.

vs ung dung thuong mai khong gian cai dat

Game thuong mai khong gian native (cua hang desktop hoac mobile) thuong co chien dich lon, do hoa 3D va cho online, nhung can tai va cai, thuong can tai khoan va doi khi mua. Void Trader doi quy mo lay ma sat bang khong: mot lan tai ~50 KB trong tab, khong tai khoan, khong localStorage, khong WebGL. Choi tot nhat voi ban phim va chuot; khong co dieu khien touch.
