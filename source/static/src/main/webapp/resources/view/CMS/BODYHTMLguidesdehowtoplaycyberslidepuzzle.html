How to Play Cyber Slide Puzzle - Step by Step

The Cyber Slide Puzzle page loads an ~11 KB sliding-tile (n-puzzle) game in an iframe. You shuffle numbered tiles, slide them into the empty cell, and solve 3x3, 4x4, or 5x5 boards while moves and time are tracked.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Pick a difficulty, press Shuffle, then click or tap any tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it. Reset returns to a solved board; winning shows a summary and saves best time per size under ftol:cyberslidepuzzle:best in localStorage only.

Step 1 - press Play and pick a grid size

Press Play on the game page to load the iframe. Use the difficulty dropdown for 3x3 Easy, 4x4 Classic (default), or 5x5 Hard. Changing difficulty reshuffles automatically.

Step 2 - shuffle and read the HUD

Press Shuffle to scramble the board with a guaranteed-solvable layout. Watch Moves and Time in the stats bar; Best shows your record for the current grid size if you have one saved.

Step 3 - slide tiles with click or tap

Only tiles next to the empty cell move. Click or tap a neighboring tile to slide it into the gap. The move counter starts on your first slide; the timer ticks every second after that.

Step 4 - win, save best times, play again

When every number is in order, a win overlay shows moves and elapsed time. Faster solves update Best for that grid size in ftol:cyberslidepuzzle:best. Play Again closes the overlay and shuffles a new board. No account, no cloud sync, zero server calls during play.
