Frequently Asked Questions

What is Orbital Order?

A free browser Aufbau electron puzzle. Guide electrons into atomic orbitals with cursor attract and repel physics.

How do I play?

Press Play, then move the cursor. Blue electrons follow you; orange electrons flee. Seat each electron in an empty orbital.

What happens if I hit a filled orbital?

Hitting an occupied orbital twice ejects the electron. Keep shells clean while you fill the Aufbau sequence.

What keys work?

M toggles mute. I or ? toggles help. L toggles the log. Esc closes the open panel.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Tutorial and fastest-time flags use ftol:orbitalorder:tutorialDone and ftol:orbitalorder:fastestTime in this browser.

How big is the download?

About ~32 KB of HTML, CSS, and JS after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from aftongauntlett/js13k-demo (MIT). Procedural Web Audio; no CDN assets.
