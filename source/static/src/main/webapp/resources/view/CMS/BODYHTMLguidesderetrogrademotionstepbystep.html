Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit the Sun, Earth, and Mars, scroll to zoom, and press Play. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - find the overtakes

Earth is the blue inner world; Mars is the red outer. The yellow line is the Earth-to-Mars line of sight.


Step 2 - near opposition

Click Near opposition and leave Sky trail on so the purple path shows the loop stretch while the badge flips to Retrograde now.


Step 3 - scrub the synodic cycle

Drag Day across hundreds of days to see direct motion return, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while you orbit the view.
