Como jogar Bangbang Artillery - passo a passo

Bangbang Artillery - ~35 KB iframe duel.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Pressione Play, mire com as setas, ajuste a forca e aperte Space para atirar no canhao da rede neural.

1 - Play

Play -> iframe (~35 KB). You right, computer left.

2 - Aim

Arrows rotate / set power. Touch: drag right aim, left power.

3 - Fire

Space / tap on your turn.

4 - Learn

AI trains on misses within the round. Refresh resets.
