How to explore Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer, locate the termination shock and heliopause spheres, and scrub the ~400 km/s wind readout.

  1. Open /space-3d/solar-wind-heliosphere.html and wait for the Sun plus nested cyan/magenta spheres.
  2. Read the facts panel: termination shock ~94 AU, heliopause ~121 AU (Voyager 1, 2012), wind ~400 km/s.
  3. Click the cyan shock sphere, magenta heliopause, or Voyager marker for boundary details.
  4. Scrub Wind between 200 and 800 km/s and watch the outward particle stream rate change.
  5. Press Pause wind to freeze particles, Hide labels to clear AU callouts, then AU table for the summary.
