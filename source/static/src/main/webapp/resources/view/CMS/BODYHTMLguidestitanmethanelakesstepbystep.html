How to Explore Titan Methane Lakes 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-22

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Titan Methane Lakes 3D Explorer, toggle the haze and the cutaway, and read the published atmosphere, sea, and ocean figures.

  1. Open /space-3d/titan-methane-lakes.html and wait for the hazy moon sphere to render.
  2. Press Haze: Off to fade the orange haze and reveal the polar seas Cassini's radar mapped.
  3. Press Cutaway: On to fade the ice shell and see the subsurface ocean and core.
  4. Drag to orbit and scroll or pinch to zoom on the seas or the cutaway layers.
  5. Read the facts panel for atmosphere composition, surface pressure/temperature, sea sizes, and the Huygens landing.
