Frequently Asked Questions

What case styles can I convert to?

Seven: UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, snake_case, and kebab-case. Paste your text, click the button for the style you want, and the result appears in the box below.

Is my text uploaded anywhere?

No. The conversion runs in your browser with plain JavaScript - nothing is uploaded, and no file is created or downloaded. Your text disappears when you leave the page.

What if I need word counts or hashing instead of case conversion?

Those are separate tools: the word counter reports word and character counts, and the MD5 hash generator turns text into an MD5 hash. This tool only reformats the case of the text you paste.
