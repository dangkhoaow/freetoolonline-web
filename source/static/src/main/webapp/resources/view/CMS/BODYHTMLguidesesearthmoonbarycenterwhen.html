Use this explorer when you want to see where the Earth-Moon balance point is, and skip it when you want tides or a full gravity sandbox.

  • Showing the barycenter sits inside Earth, 4,671 km from the centre
  • Linking Earth monthly wobble to how exoplanets are detected

This is an educational visualization; the Moon distance is compressed and the motion is sped up.
