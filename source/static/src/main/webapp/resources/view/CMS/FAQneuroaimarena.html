Frequently Asked Questions

What is Neuro Aim Arena?

A free browser neuroevolution aim duel. You fight robots controlled by neural networks that evolve via a genetic algorithm each generation.

How do I play?

Click the canvas to start. WASD or arrow keys move. Mouse aims. Hold mouse button to shoot. On phones, on-screen sticks appear via GuiControls.

What do the bars above bots mean?

Red is health. Green is the shoot cooldown meter - empty means you cannot fire until it regenerates.

Does it save progress?

No. There is no localStorage. Refreshing the page restarts at generation 1.

How big is the download?

About ~160 KB of HTML, JavaScript, CSS, artwork, controls overlay, and one shoot sound after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from Aim and Shoot by Victor Ribeiro under the MIT license. This site build adds noindex and ships LICENSE plus CREDITS next to the engine.
