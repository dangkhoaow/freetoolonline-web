Jwt Decoder Khi Nao Dung

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

JWT Decoder dang de mo khi ban can doc xem mot JSON Web Token thuc su chua gi truoc khi tin dung no trong code, mot bao cao loi, hoac mot loi goi API. Trang nay noi ro khi nao phu hop va khi nao khong.


Khi nao phu hop

Phu hop bat cu khi nao ban can kiem tra token mang nhung claims nao, xac nhan timestamp exp da qua han chua, hoac xac minh mot chuoi co thuc su la JWT khong - ba phan cach nhau boi dau cham - thay vi mot blob base64 trong giong. Khi mot token co claim so exp hoac iat, cong cu se doi truc tiep sang ngay de doc, kem theo nhan EXPIRED hoac not-yet-expired, nen ban khong can tu quy doi timestamp Unix bang tay.


Nen mong doi gi

Viec giai ma JWT o day chi dien ra cuc bo: no chay hoan toan trong tab trinh duyet nay, khong can tai len va khong can tai khoan. Header va payload hien thi duoi dang JSON de doc; signature hien thi duoi dang van ban thuan nhung khong duoc xac minh, vi viec do can khoa bi mat hoac khoa cong khai cua nguoi phat hanh, thu khong bao gio den duoc trinh duyet nay.

Khong co gi ban dan vao duoc luu; tai lai trang se xoa o nhap.
