Pomodoro Timer Online Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Pomodoro Timer Online can ba buoc: dat so phut Work/Break, nhan Start, va de nhan phase tu doi. Trang nay giai thich tung buoc.


Buoc 1 - dat so phut Work va Break

Mo Pomodoro Timer Online. Hai truong so da san sang truoc khi bat dau: Work (1-120 phut, mac dinh 25) va Break (1-60 phut, mac dinh 5). Doi mot trong hai neu ty le 25/5 mac dinh khong hop voi phien lam viec cua ban.


Buoc 2 - nhan Start

Nhan Start de bat dau khoang Work. Nhan phase hien 'Work' va dong ho dem lui tu so phut Work da chon. Pause giu dong ho dung yen ma khong doi phase; nhan Start lan nua se tiep tuc.


Buoc 3 - de tu doi giua Work va Break

Khi dong ho Work chay ve khong, nhan phase doi thanh 'Break' va dong ho nap lai tu so phut Break; khi Break ket thuc no quay lai Work, va lap lai nhu vay chi khi ban de tab mo. Reset bat cu luc nao se dua ve dau khoang Work hien tai.

Moi thu chay cuc bo ngay trong tab trinh duyet nay - khong tai len va khong can tai khoan. Dong hoac tai lai tab se dung dong ho; no khong tiep tuc chay hay bao nen.
