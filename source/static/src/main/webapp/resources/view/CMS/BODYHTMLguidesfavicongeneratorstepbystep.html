Favicon Generator Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Open Favicon Generator, choose a local image, then download favicon.ico or individual PNGs.


Steps

  1. Click Choose File and select a PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, or BMP.
  2. Wait for the 16, 32, 48, 180, 192, and 512 previews to fill in.
  3. Download favicon.ico for browsers, or grab 32x32 / 180x180 PNG for common placements.
  4. Use the per-size PNG links when you need a single dimension only.

What stays private

Resizing runs in the browser. Your image is not uploaded.
