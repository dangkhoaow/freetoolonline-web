How to Explore Apophis 2029 Flyby 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open Apophis 2029 Flyby 3D Explorer, drag the day slider through the real 13 April 2029 close approach, and read the live distance from Earth against the geostationary satellite ring.

  1. Open /space-3d/apophis-2029-flyby.html and wait for Earth and the geostationary ring to render.
  2. Drag the day slider from -3 to +3 days and watch the asteroid marker move along the flyby path.
  3. Click "Jump to closest approach" to snap directly to 13 April 2029 and read the real ~31,600 km minimum distance from Earth's surface in the facts panel.
  4. Check whether the marker sits inside or outside the geostationary ring at each day offset.
  5. Click "Reset" to snap back to day -3, or drag to orbit Earth and scroll/pinch to zoom for a closer look.
