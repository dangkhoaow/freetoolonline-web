Quando usar Supernova Remnant 3D Explorer

Use the Supernova Remnant 3D Explorer when you need the expanding shell - and skip it when you need a full stellar timeline or pulsar beams alone.

  • Classroom demos of SN 1054 / Crab Nebula literacy
  • Linking ~6,500 ly and ~1,500 km/s before remnant labs
  • One-screen refresher on shell growth after a core-collapse event

This page does not solve hydrodynamics, does not map real filament catalogs, and compresses scales for teaching.
