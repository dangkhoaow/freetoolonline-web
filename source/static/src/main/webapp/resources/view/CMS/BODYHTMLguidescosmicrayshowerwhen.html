When to use Cosmic Ray Air Shower 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Use this explorer when you want to see how one particle becomes a shower of billions, and skip it when you want stellar fusion or a gravity effect.

  • Classroom demos of extensive air showers and particle cascades
  • Showing why muons reach the ground while other particles do not
  • Connecting pion decay to what ground detectors measure

This is an educational visualization with illustrative branching, not a Monte-Carlo shower simulation.
