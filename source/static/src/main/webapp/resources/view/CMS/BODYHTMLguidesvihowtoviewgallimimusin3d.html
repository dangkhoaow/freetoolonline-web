Doc bang su that Gallimimus va bam co hoac mo

Bang su that Gallimimus liet ke chieu dai khoang 6 m (ca the lon nhat den 7-8 m o mot so nguon; Size vs human dung 6 m), chieu cao hong khoang 1.9 m, khoi luong khoang 400-490 kg (uoc tinh khac thap den 227 kg), toc do chay uoc khoang 42-56 km/h (Thulborn, 1982), cua so ~70 Mya Creta muon (Maastrichtian) o Formacion Nemegt sa mac Gobi, Mong Co, va lich su dat ten - Halszka Osmolska, Ewa Roniewicz va Rinchen Barsbold dat ten chi nam 1972 tu holotype IGM 100/11, phat hien 1964 boi Zofia Kielan-Jaworowska o Tsaagan Khushuu. Ten nghia la bat chuoc ga. Co co dai (feats.longNeck) va mo keratin khong rang (teeth: beak). Che do an chua giai quyet.
