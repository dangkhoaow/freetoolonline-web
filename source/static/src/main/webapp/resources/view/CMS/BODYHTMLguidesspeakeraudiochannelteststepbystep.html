Speaker & Audio Channel Test Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Follow these steps on the Speaker & Audio Channel Test page. The tone starts only after you click a Play button (browser autoplay rules).


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Speaker & Audio Channel Test. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - pick a frequency

Move the Hz slider if you want a different tone (default 440 Hz). The label next to the slider shows the current value.


Step 3 - play a channel

Click Play Left, Play Right, or Play Both. A short sine tone plays on that channel for about 2.5 seconds. The status line under Channel cue updates so you can see the test running even without headphones.


Step 4 - stop or repeat

Click Stop to end the tone early, or play another channel. Everything runs in the browser - no microphone, no upload, and no account.
