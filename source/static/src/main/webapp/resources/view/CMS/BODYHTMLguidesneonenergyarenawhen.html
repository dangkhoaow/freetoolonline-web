When to Play Neon Energy Arena

Neon Energy Arena fits short arena dogfights where you want mouse aim, four weapons, and a permanent Energy shop between runs - about ~90 KB after Play, saved only in your browser.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Short session arena runs

Start run, survive geometric waves, bank Energy on death, then open the shop. A single run is self-contained; meta progress carries across visits on the same device.

Mouse-aim combat practice

WASD plus mouse aim and LMB fire reward pointer accuracy more than tap-only runners. Use this when you have a mouse or trackpad; phones load but lack a dedicated touch HUD.

Local meta without accounts

Energy, upgrades, and high score stay under ftol:neonenergyarena:* in localStorage. No login, no cloud. Reset save clears everything after confirm.

When to pick another game

Want horde auto-attack survival instead of arena aiming? Use Night Swarm Survivor. Want a vertical STG with a chili pilot? Use Chili Blast Shooter.
