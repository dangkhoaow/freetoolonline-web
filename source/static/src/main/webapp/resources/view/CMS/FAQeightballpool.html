Frequently Asked Questions

What is Eight Ball Pool?

A free browser classic eight-ball billiards game. Play vs AI or a second local player with mouse aim and W/S power.

How do I play?

Press Play, choose 1P or 2P, aim the cue with the mouse, set power with W/S, and left-click to shoot. Esc opens the main menu.

Does it save progress?

No. Matches are session-only. There is no localStorage save file.

How big is the download?

About ~13 MB of HTML, JS, sprites, and sounds after you press Play.

Is the music free to use?
