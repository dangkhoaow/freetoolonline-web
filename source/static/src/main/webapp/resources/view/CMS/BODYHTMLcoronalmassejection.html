About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Scrub CME speed or progress to update travel time.

NOAA SWPC lists CME speeds from slower than 250 km/s to nearly 3000 km/s. The fastest Earth-directed events can arrive in 15-18 hours; slower and typical arrivals take several days, often about 1-3 days. NASA GSFC describes masses in the billions-of-tons class (~1e16 g).

Drag to orbit and scroll or pinch to zoom. Press Play or Pause, Reset, or scrub speed and progress. For one famous historical storm see the Carrington Event 3D Explorer; for the ambient wind bubble see the Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer.
