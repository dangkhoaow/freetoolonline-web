Neon Energy Arena is a neon arena roguelite in the browser: fly with WASD, aim with the mouse, shoot with LMB, switch four weapons with F, dash with Shift, drop bombs with Space, and spend Energy on permanent shop upgrades. About ~90 KB after Play, no install, no account.

NEON ENERGY ARENA

Neon arena roguelite on the open-source neon-deep-space engine (MIT). About ~90 KB downloads once from this site when you press Play, then the browser caches it. Energy and upgrades stay in your browser; nothing is uploaded.

WASD move, mouse aim, LMB shoot, F switch weapon, Shift dash, Space bomb, Esc pause. Pointer aim is required - a desktop or trackpad works best.

Press Play to load the game.
Esc pauses. Shop upgrades and high score use ftol:neonenergyarena:* keys.
