Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Haumea elongated?

Its spin period is only about 3.92 hours - among the fastest for large Solar System bodies - so centrifugal stretch produces a triaxial, football-like shape.

What did the 2017 occultation show?

A multi-chord stellar occultation constrained projected axes near 1704 by 1138 km, a largest axis of at least ~2322 km, and a ring ~70 km wide at ~2287 km radius (Nature).

What do Play, Ring, and Speed do?

Play starts or pauses spin. Ring shows or hides the schematic ring. Speed changes how fast the animation runs.

Is this a shape model from a spacecraft?

No. It is a procedural schematic grounded in published occultation sizes. No spacecraft has flown by Haumea.

Where are the moons?

Hi'iaka and Namaka are real satellites but are not modeled here so the elongated body and ring stay readable.
