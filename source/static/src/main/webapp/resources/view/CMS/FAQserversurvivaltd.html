Frequently Asked Questions

What is Server Survival TD?

A free browser 3D cloud-infrastructure tower defense. Place CDN, database, firewall, and related services to survive rising traffic.

How do I play?

Press Play, choose a mode, place services on the grid, connect them so traffic can flow, and keep budget and reputation healthy.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Save data, sound prefs, locale, tutorial, and campaign progress use ftol:serversurvivaltd:* keys on this device.

How big is the download?

About ~13 MB of HTML, JS, CSS, Three.js, and audio after you press Play. Later visits use the browser cache.

Is this the same as Neon Tower Rush or Garden Defense?

No. Those are classic map or plant-wave tower defense. Server Survival TD is a cloud-infra sim with services, RPS load, and reputation.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from pshenok/server-survival (MIT, Copyright (c) 2025 Kostyantyn Pshenychnyy). Runtime CDNs were vendored locally.
