When to use Magnetic Reconnection 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want the trigger behind solar flares and the aurora, and skip it when you want the Sun magnetic loops themselves or Earth magnetosphere shape.

  • Explaining how field lines break and rejoin at an X-point
  • Connecting reconnection to flares, mass ejections, and the aurora
  • Showing why plasma jets out sideways like a slingshot

This is an educational visualization - stylized, schematic field lines, not to scale.
