When to Play Cyber Slide Puzzle in the Browser

Cyber Slide Puzzle is a lightweight n-puzzle for short focus breaks - about 11 KB, no install, and saves that never leave your browser.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Good moments: a two-minute brain break between tasks, a commute with spotty connectivity, or teaching sliding-puzzle mechanics without downloading an app. Best times per grid size persist under ftol:cyberslidepuzzle:best until you clear browser data.

When the tiny payload matters

At roughly 11 KB total, the game loads almost instantly on slow networks. That beats multi-megabyte puzzle apps when you only need one quick board.

When local-only saves are a feature

No sign-in means no tracking and no cloud dependency - ideal on shared PCs. Clearing browser storage resets best times, so export or screenshot records if you care about long-term trophies.

When click/tap control is enough

There are no arrow-key bindings - every move is a direct click or tap on a tile. That suits touch phones and mouse desktops alike but is weaker if you prefer keyboard-only play.

Skip this page when you want physics tilt (try Marble Maze) or falling-block reflex (try Hex Puzzle Blocks). Choose installed puzzle apps when you need daily challenges with cloud leaderboards.
