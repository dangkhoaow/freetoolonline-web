Layer Flip Platformer vs cac platformer khac

Layer Flip Platformer la platformer lat lop (~44 KB) noi Space lat sang/toi qua 25 man. Day la cach so voi cac platformer khac tren site va lua chon cai dat.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

vs One Tap Platformer (dash)

One Tap Platformer la platformer mot cham/click (~34 KB) voi bon man va song dich. Ho tro touch va chuot, co the luu tutorial/man trong localStorage. Layer Flip lon hon mot chut (~44 KB vs ~34 KB), chi ban phim/gamepad, 25 man puzzle, khong bao gio luu. Chon One Tap cho dash touch; Layer Flip cho puzzle lat lop bang Space.

vs Pixel Spike Run (runner)

Pixel Spike Run la runner pixel endless (~25 KB): nhay ho, dich va thung gai. Khong danh sach man, khong lat lop. Layer Flip la platformer huu han 25 man. Chon Pixel Spike cho diem endless; Layer Flip cho puzzle tung man.

vs ung dung platformer cai dat

Ung dung native thuong co chien dich lon, save cloud va touch, nhung can tai tren cua hang. Layer Flip doi quy mo lay ma sat bang khong: mot lan tai ~44 KB trong tab, khong tai khoan, khong localStorage, khong WebGL.
