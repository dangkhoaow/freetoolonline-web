About this model

The facts panel fills in when the scene loads. Click parts of the dinosaur to read about them.

Psittacosaurus was a Late Cretaceous Early Cretaceous ceratopsian from Asia, often restored as a small biped without the large frill of later horned dinosaurs; turn on the human figure to see how a person compares to the reconstructed body used here.

Fossils preserve bone, not soft tissue or color. The full-body shape on this page follows related relatives such as Protoceratops and Triceratops. This is an artistic reconstruction, not a fossil-accurate skeleton. Panel figures use mid-range values within published length and mass ranges.
