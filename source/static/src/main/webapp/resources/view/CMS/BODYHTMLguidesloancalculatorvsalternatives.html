When to choose Loan Calculator

Choose Loan Calculator when you want a quick estimate without sending any numbers to a lender or building your own formulas. Change the amount, rate, or term and the payment recalculates instantly.

Choose a bank's own calculator when you want a quote tied to that bank's actual rates. Choose a spreadsheet when you need to customize the formula beyond a standard fixed-rate loan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose the browser tool instead of a bank's calculator?

Nothing is sent to a server, and the amortization table is one click away instead of a paid feature on some bank sites.

Is the result the same as a bank's official quote?

No. This is illustrative math only - it does not include taxes, insurance, or lender fees, and does not replace an actual quote from a lender.
