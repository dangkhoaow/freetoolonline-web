Curiosity Rover Traverse 3D Explorer vs alternatives

Curiosity Rover Traverse 3D Explorer animates 6 real published mission milestones along one route through Gale Crater so a reader can see how far the rover has driven since 2012 at a glance - NASA's own mission-status maps win as the live, continuously updated official source; the Mars Terrain 3D Explorer wins for exploring the relief of all of Mars rather than one rover's path inside one crater.
