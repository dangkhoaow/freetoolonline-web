When to Play Retro Arcade Shooter

Retro Arcade Shooter fits when you want a ~120 KB WebGL top-down Underrun run with keyboard and mouse, three short levels, synthesized audio, and no browser saves.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

When you have a keyboard and mouse

WASD move, mouse aim, hold click to fire. No touch controls and no gamepad - phones load the page but cannot play.

When you want a tiny one-time download

About 120 KB total. Music and SFX are synthesized in the tab (Sonant-X). Zero network calls after load.

When short arcade runs are enough

Exactly three levels. Death reloads the level after a few seconds. Nothing is saved between sessions.

When to pick something else

Want a tiny canvas twin-stick without WebGL maps? Try Chili Blast Shooter (~26 KB). Want a true first-person campaign with saves? Use Retro FPS Online.
