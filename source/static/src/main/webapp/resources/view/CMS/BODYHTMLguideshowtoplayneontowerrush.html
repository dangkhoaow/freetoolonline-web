How to Play Neon Tower Rush - Step by Step

The Neon Tower Rush page loads a ~1.2 MB vanilla Canvas TD in an iframe. Draft up to seven towers, defend one of six maps across 30 waves, and chase a high score before the base leaks out.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play on the page, then use the in-game draft screen: select towers, choose a map, place on grass tiles, press Start Next Wave, upgrade towers in the side panel, and use pause/restart buttons in the HUD. Audio saves under ftol:neontowerrush:* only.

Step 1 - launch and draft

Click Play on the game page. Inside the iframe, pick 1-7 towers from the pre-match pool, then press Select Map. The UI is Chinese but icons and tower art are self-explanatory; hover tower cards for stats.

Step 2 - map and first builds

Choose one of six maps (each changes enemy HP/speed). Spend starting gold by clicking grass tiles to place drafted towers. Open a placed tower to upgrade along its 4-9 level track toward an EX form with a signature skill.

Step 3 - waves and economy

Press Start Next Wave when ready; starting early adds speed score. Kills grant gold for more towers and upgrades. Summoners appear on waves 10/15/20/25; the wave-30 boss ends the run if defeated before your base health reaches zero.

Step 4 - HUD controls

Pause and quick-restart sit in the settings dock. Abandon ends the current run. Open the in-game Wiki button for tower/enemy reference. Mute and volume sliders persist via ftol:neontowerrush:audio-settings. No online leaderboard on this host.
