Solo Battlefield is a free browser last-alive combat game. You are the last fighter standing: clear eight escalating levels of melee foes and archers on a square battlefield. Move with WASD or the arrow keys, click to strike, and press Space after a clear to advance. About ~12 KB, no install, no account.

Want a longer strategy defense instead? Try Machine Guard Corps.

SOLO BATTLEFIELD

Clear 8 levels as the last fighter alive. Adapted from Only One by Cemal Gonultas (MIT). About ~12 KB, downloads once when you press Play.

WASD or arrows to move, click/tap to strike, Space to advance after a clear. Best with a mouse; touch works for attacks.

Press Play to load the game.
Session-only play - no save data in this build.
