Void Trader

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17
freetoolonline.com editorial team

Void Trader is a real-time space trading roguelike. You start with 500 credits, a light ship, and one life. Each sector has asteroids to mine, stations to dock at, and pirates that hunt you. Clear a sector to jump deeper; die once and the run ends. Press Play to launch.

Controls at a glance:

ActionInput
Move shipWASD or arrow keys
Aim / fireMouse / left click
Missile / shieldRight click or X / Space
Dock / cargoE (near station) / Tab
Pause / restartEsc / R

Stations buy and sell five goods - Titanium Ore, Crystal Shards, Plasma Cells, Nav Data, and Quantum Core - and also repair hull, recharge shields, restock missiles, and upgrade the ship from Scout to Fighter to Cruiser. Mining fills your 20-slot cargo; asteroid hits damage the hull. Pirates scale with depth and pay a bounty.

Eight sectors. Clear every enemy to jump deeper; clear sector 8 to win. Nothing is saved - each visit is a fresh permadeath run. The game is a single 50 KB HTML file (Canvas 2D + Web Audio) and plays best on a desktop. It runs on Orbitron by Daniel Silva Perez under the MIT license, rebranded here as Void Trader.
