A free space trading roguelike in the browser tab: fly a ship through procedurally generated sectors, mine asteroids for ore, dock at stations to buy and sell goods, upgrade your hull and weapons, and push deeper until you clear all eight sectors or die. Canvas 2D, no install, no account, nothing uploaded.

Prefer a pure arcade shooter with no trading? Try Asteroid Blaster.

VOID TRADER

A deep-space trading roguelike on the open-source Orbitron engine. About 50 KB downloads once from this site when you press Play, then the browser caches it.

Best on a desktop with a mouse and keyboard: WASD to fly, mouse to aim, click to fire, E to dock, Tab for cargo.

Press Play to load the game.
Press Esc to pause. R restarts after death.
