How to explore Sun Structure 3D Explorer

Open the Sun Structure 3D Explorer, highlight each solar layer, and read core ~15.7 million K, photosphere ~5,772 K, and corona ~1-3 million K in the facts panel.

  1. Open /space-3d/sun-structure.html and wait for the cutaway hemispheres and corona halo.
  2. Read the facts panel: core ~15.7 million K, radiative zone, convective zone, photosphere ~5,772 K, corona.
  3. Press Core to highlight the fusion region at the center of the schematic.
  4. Press Radiative or Convective to focus energy-transport layers between core and photosphere.
  5. Press Photosphere or Corona for ~5,772 K surface and ~1-3 million K outer atmosphere, or All for the full cutaway.
