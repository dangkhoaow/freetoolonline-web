When to choose BMI Calculator

Choose BMI Calculator for a one-off BMI check when you do not want to install anything and your numbers stay on your device. Metric and imperial units are both supported from the same dropdown, with no manual conversion.

Choose a health/fitness app when you want to track BMI over time alongside other health metrics. Choose an upload-based service only if your data is already in a format that service processes.
