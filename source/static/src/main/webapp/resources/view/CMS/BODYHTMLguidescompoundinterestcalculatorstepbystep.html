Compound Interest Calculator Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Compound Interest Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, enter five numbers, then read the result - it updates live, with nothing to click or download.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Compound Interest Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - enter your numbers

Fill in the starting principal, the monthly contribution, the annual interest rate, the number of years, and pick a compounding frequency (monthly, annually, or daily). Each field updates the result as you type or change it - there is no submit button.


Step 3 - read the result

The output shows three figures: the final value, the total amount contributed, and the interest earned. The math runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.

Results are an estimate based on a constant rate; actual returns vary and this is not financial advice.
