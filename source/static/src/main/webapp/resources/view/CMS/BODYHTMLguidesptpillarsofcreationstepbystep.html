Como explorar Pillars of Creation 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and compare both real telescope views.

  1. Open /space-3d/pillars-of-creation.html and wait for the pillars to appear.
  2. Look at the default Hubble (visible-light) view - dense, dark, opaque columns.
  3. Press the telescope toggle to switch to James Webb (infrared).
  4. Watch the pillars become more transparent and many hidden red stars appear.
  5. Drag to orbit and compare the two views from different angles.
