This page is the hub for every FreetoolOnline how-to guide - English, Indonesian, and Portuguese entries side by side - grouped by job-to-be-done rather than by tool name. Each linked guide runs its tool in your browser, so pick the one that matches the file in your hands.

Every guide runs its tool locally in your browser - the bytes you work with are never uploaded to a server. Indonesian and Portuguese-language guide titles appear in their natural-language form alongside English equivalents, so a reader searching for kompres file zip or comprimir uma pasta finds their walkthrough on the same page without a separate locale hub.

The eight topic blocks are sorted by the input you are holding: ZIP and folder compression; HEIC and image-format conversions; device-test guides for screen, camera, microphone, and keyboard; developer-utility guides for hashes, JSON, and regex; PDF guides for merge, split, compress, and protect; video, GIF, and FFmpeg; a behind-the-scenes block; and a closing privacy section. Find the block that names your input, scan the summary lines, and click through.

The Behind-the-scenes block holds three kinds of article for readers who finished the primary task and want context: case-study pieces on how the browser-only stack behaves at scale; vs-articles comparing near-synonym tasks (JPG vs JPEG, LCD vs LED, JSON vs YAML, MD5 vs SHA-256); and troubleshooting pages listing specific failure modes with exact fixes (camera test shows a black screen, microphone reports no sound, GIF frame extractor output looks wrong).

Pre-call and pre-return device checks live together in one block: webcam framing, microphone level, screen dead-pixel sweep, keyboard test. Run camera test first to confirm webcam framing (front or rear), then microphone test for the audio level, then LCD test for the full-screen color cycle that surfaces dead pixels, and finally keyboard test to confirm every key registers. A flaky webcam caught at step one saves the time you would have spent on steps two through four; the order is deliberate, not alphabetical.
