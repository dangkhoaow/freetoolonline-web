About this scene

The facts panel fills in when the scene loads. Click objects in the scene to read about them.

The time slider previews the day/night line up to 12 hours ahead or behind - watch dawn sweep across a continent - and the Now button snaps back to live.

The map is drawn in the clean vector style of an airline seatback display: real coastlines from the public-domain Natural Earth dataset, a 30-degree grid, and a soft atmosphere rim.
