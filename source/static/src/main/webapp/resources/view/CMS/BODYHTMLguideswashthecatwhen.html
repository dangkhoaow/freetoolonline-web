When to Play Wash The Cat

Wash The Cat fits short breaks where you want a logic puzzle about water routing - about ~50 KB after Play, no install.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Pipe-logic coffee breaks

Each level is a self-contained tank-and-valve board. Plan the flow, then click valves as the cat walks toward the shower.

Progressive campaign

Nine levels introduce combo tanks, linked pipes, and position-gated valves. Saved progress under ftol:washthecat:lj lets you resume on the same device.

When to pick another game

Want rotate-to-connect pipes? Use Pipe Rotate Puzzle. Want a tap grid puzzle? Use Black Hole Square.
