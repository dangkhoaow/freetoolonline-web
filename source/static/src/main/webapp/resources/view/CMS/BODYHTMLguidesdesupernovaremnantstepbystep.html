So nutzen Sie Supernova-Uberrest 3D Explorer

Open the Supernova-Uberrest 3D Explorer, read the 6,500 ly / 1,500 km/s figures, and scrub age while the shell expands around the neutron-star core.

  1. Open /space-3d/supernova-remnant.html and wait for the orange shell and bright core.
  2. Read the facts panel: ~6,500 ly, ~1,500 km/s, SN 1054.
  3. Scrub Age (yr) from a young remnant toward ~970 yr (Crab-like age).
  4. Press Pause expand, then Play expand, to control growth.
  5. Toggle Hide/Show filaments for a clearer wireframe shell.
