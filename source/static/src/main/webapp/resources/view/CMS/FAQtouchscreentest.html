Frequently Asked Questions

What does Touchscreen Test check?

Touch and pointer contacts on a local pad - up to five finger dots, move counts, and max fingers seen. Everything stays in the tab.

Do I need to allow camera or microphone access?

No. This tool uses touch and pointer events only - no getUserMedia and no camera or mic permission prompt.

Can I use a mouse on a desktop?

Yes. Click and drag on the pad after Enable - the same readout updates with a pointer fallback so you can verify the page without a touchscreen.

Is my touch input uploaded?

No. Counters and coordinates stay in the page session. Reset clears them; closing the tab drops them.
