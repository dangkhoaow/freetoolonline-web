Khi nao choi Void Trader

Void Trader la roguelike thuong mai khong gian thoi gian thuc chay trong tab trinh duyet, khong cai dat va khong tai khoan. Phu hop phien 15 - 40 phut tren desktop khi ban can bang dao, giao dich va chien dau qua tam khu vuc. Day la khi nao no la lua chon dung.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Khi ban muon giao dich va ke hoach, khong chi ban

Khi ban muon giao dich va ke hoach, khong chi ban, Void Trader phu hop: vong lap la dao quang, cap ben ban, mua nang cap, roi day sau hon - chien dau la mot cot cung giao dich va dieu huong.

Khi ban o desktop co chuot

Di chuyen dung WASD hoac mui ten, nhung ngam va ban dung chuot, cap ben dung E. Khong co nut touch tren man hinh, nen laptop hoac desktop la cach thuc te de choi het van. Tren dien thoai trang van tai, nhung dieu khien ban phim va chuot khong chuyen tot.

Khi van mot mang la muc tieu

Khi van mot mang la muc tieu, Void Trader dap ung: khong save va khong bang diem cao - moi lan vao bat dau voi 500 credit va ket thuc khi vo tau ve khong hoac ban xong sector 8.

Khi quyen rieng tu va tai nhe quan trong

Moi thu chay client-side sau mot lan tai ~50 KB: khong dang nhap, khong upload, va khong goi server trong khi choi. Khong ghi localStorage. Khong can WebGL - Canvas 2D thuan - nen chay tren trinh duyet yeu voi game 3D nang.

Khi khong phai lua chon dung

Void Trader khong phai lua chon dung neu ban chi co dien thoai, neu muon chien dich luu giua cac lan choi, hoac neu muon shooter phan xa thuan khong giao dich - Asteroid Blaster phu hop hon.
