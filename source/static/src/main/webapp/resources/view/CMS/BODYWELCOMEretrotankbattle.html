Retro Tank Battle

Last reviewed: 2026-07-04

Drive your tank with the arrows or WASD, fire with Space, and clear the wave of 6 enemy tanks before they destroy your base or your 3 lives run out.

Enemy tanks steer themselves toward your base and shoot when they line up with you, so standing still is a losing strategy.

Your best destroyed-tanks count is saved in this browser and shown in the score line - no account, no leaderboard.

Play it fullscreen with one click - the lives and score line stay visible, and Escape returns to the page.
