Doc panel su that Giganotosaurus va bam cac phan co the

Panel su that Giganotosaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 12-13 m o holotype (toi khoang 13.2 m o mau tham chieu MUCPv-95), chieu dai so khoang 1.53-1.80 m o holotype va toi khoang 1.95 m o mau tham chieu lon hon, khoi luong khoang 4.2-13.8 tan voi nhieu uoc tinh quanh 7-8 tan, cua so 99.6-95 Mya Cenomanian som o Formacion Candeleros tai Patagonia, Argentina, va phat hien - Ruben Dario Carolini tim thay hoa thach dau tien gan Villa El Chocon nam 1993; Rodolfo Coria va Leonardo Salgado dat ten loai nam 1995. Holotype MUCPv-Ch1 duoc giu tai Ernesto Bachmann Municipal Paleontological Museum o Villa El Chocon.
