Doc bang su that Therizinosaurus va bam mong khong lo

Bang su that Therizinosaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 9-10 m, chieu cao khoang 4-5 m, can nang khoang 5-10 tan tuy phuong phap (Natural History Museum, London, khong cam ket mot so khoi luong duy nhat), loi xuong mong tay vua tren 50 cm - mong dai nhat cua bat ky dong vat tren can nao da biet - cua so ~72-66 Mya Creta Muon (Maastrichtian, co the Campanian muon) o Formation Nemegt sa mac Gobi, Mongolia, va lich su dat ten - Evgeny Maleev dat ten chi nam 1954 tu holotype PIN 551-483, phat hien 1948. Du mong giong thu an thit, no la dong vat an thuc vat (browser cao).
