Cach choi Medieval Wall Defense - Huong dan tung buoc

Medieval Wall Defense tai game phong thu tuong tang ~631 KB mot file trong iframe. Ban giu tuong thanh chong song, dung vang thue hero, tich RP khi ket thuc run va dung Crowns cho bonus prestige lau dai.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Click/cham o ban do de dat hero, bam 1-4 (hoac cham nut skill) de cast, Space tam dung, R mo sach nghien cuu, Q ket thuc run de giu RP, M tat am, Esc dong panel. Tien trinh luu local duoi ftol:medievalwalldefense:castle*.

Buoc 1 - bat dau tu man hinh title

Bam Play tren trang game, roi Start trong overlay ben trong. Tutorial ngan co the huong dan hero dau - dong bang Esc hoac lam het buoc. Game adapt tu du an Crownfall ma nguon (MIT); credit nam canh file game.

Buoc 2 - dat hero va doc HUD song

Click/cham o trong de thue hero tu panel ben. Moi loai co tam ban, sat thuong va upgrade khac nhau - cung, phap su va ho tro tao combo khi song tang. Vang tu kill mua them hero. Nut skill va panel to de cham tren dien thoai.

Buoc 3 - dung skill bang 1-4 hoac cham

Slot skill mo dan qua nghien cuu va prestige. Phim 1-4 hoac nut tren man hinh de arm skill, roi click ban do de nhaim. Esc huy ma khong ban. Cooldown hien tren thanh - timing boss quan trong hon spam mob yeu.

Buoc 4 - tam dung, nghien cuu va ket thuc run

Space bat/tat pause va mo thong ke run. R mo sach nghien cuu de tieu RP vao node vinh vien - sat thuong, kinh te, slot, auto-buy. Q ket thuc som; xac nhan, giu RP da kiem va quay ve man nghien cuu.

Buoc 5 - prestige Crowns va cai dat

Khi run ket thuc hoac pause, tieu Crowns tren cay prestige - khoi dau nhanh hon, hero manh hon, RP offline va ancestry. M bat/tat am; volume luu ftol:medievalwalldefense:castleMuted. Cai dat gom toc do, giam chuyen dong, auto-prestige va nhac backup.

Buoc 6 - offline va luu local

Dong tab van sinh RP offline - game luu thoi gian va toc do o ftol:medievalwalldefense:castleLastCloseAt va castleOfflineRpRate. Moi tien trinh - Crowns, nghien cuu, commanders, diem hang ngay, stats - o trinh duyet qua ftol:medievalwalldefense:castle*. Khong tai khoan, khong cloud, 0 goi server.
