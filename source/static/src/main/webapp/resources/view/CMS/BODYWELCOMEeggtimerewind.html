Welcome to Egg Time Rewind - a free Nokia-style arena shooter where dying rewinds time and leaves parallel lives on the field to help you. Break space eggs for big points, chase a high score saved only in this browser, and reset anytime with R. About ~60 KB after Play, adapted from Frank Force's GPL-2.0 EggTimeRewind (Nokia 3310 Jam). Privacy note: the only save is the local high-score key. For more free browser games, open the games hub. Related titles include Bounce Back and Rock Paper Neural.
