Percentage Calculator Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-06

Percentage Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Percentage Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it for a discount, a tax add-on, a grade, or comparing two values as a percent change.


Step 3 - get the result

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.

Switch calculations at any time; the two number fields carry over so you can compare results.
