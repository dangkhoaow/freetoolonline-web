City Time Machine 3D

Last reviewed: 2026-07-05

Drag the 1945-2055 timeline and watch one city block's buildings, vehicles, signs, street lights, and people transform live in front of you.

Each lot keeps its own footprint through time, so the small 1945 corner shop grows into the same corner's 1985 office and 2055 tower.

The change is animated, not a cut - buildings shrink and regrow one lot after another over about two seconds while the sky, light, and fog crossfade to the era.
