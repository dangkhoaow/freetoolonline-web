How to Explore Impact Crater Formation 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-19

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Impact Crater Formation 3D Explorer, compare Barringer, Chicxulub, and Vredefort at a shared scale, then drag the impactor slider to grow a test crater.

  1. Open /space-3d/impact-crater-formation.html and wait for the ground plane plus three crater rims to paint.
  2. Click the smallest rim - Barringer Crater, about 1.19 km across and 50,000 years old.
  3. Click the mid-size rim - Chicxulub, about 180 km across and 66 million years old, the end-Cretaceous impact.
  4. Click the largest rim - Vredefort Dome, about 300 km across (original estimate) and 2.02 billion years old.
  5. Drag the impactor-diameter slider from 50 m toward 15 km and watch the red test crater grow using the 20x scaling rule.
