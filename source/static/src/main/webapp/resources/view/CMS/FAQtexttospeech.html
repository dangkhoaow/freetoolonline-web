Frequently Asked Questions

What does Text to Speech do?

Type or paste your text, pick a voice, and press Speak to hear it read aloud in your browser. Nothing is uploaded and there is no file to download.

When should I reach for text to speech?

Reach for it when you would rather listen than read - hearing a message, an article, or a draft out loud. Use the Rate and Pitch sliders to adjust how the voice sounds.

What complementary tools work well alongside text to speech?

If you need the reverse direction, look at the related-tools section for the SPEECH-to-TEXT converter.
