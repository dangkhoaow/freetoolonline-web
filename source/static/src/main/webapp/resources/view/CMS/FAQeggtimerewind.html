Frequently Asked Questions

What is Egg Time Rewind?

A free low-res Nokia-style shooter: when you die, time rewinds and your parallel lives remain to help clear the arena.

How do I move and shoot?

Move with WASD, arrow keys, numpad, mouse, or gamepad. Shoot with Space, Shift, mouse click, or a gamepad face button.

What happens when I die?

Time rewinds and prior lives stay on the map as helpers. Break the space egg for 50 points; other kills are 1 point each.

Does it save progress?

Only a high score in this browser under ftol:eggtimerewind:highScore. Press R to reset the run. Levels are randomly generated each game.

How big is the download?

About ~60 KB of HTML, JavaScript, and tiny pixel art tiles after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from EggTimeRewind by Frank Force under GPL-2.0. This site build strips debug/CCapture tooling, adds noindex, and ships LICENSE plus CREDITS next to the engine.
