When to use Expanding Universe 3D Explorer

Use the Expanding Universe 3D Explorer when you need the expansion-of-space mechanism and Hubble Law literacy - and skip it when you only need a single-source Doppler shift or the wider distance ladder.

  • Classroom demos of why farther galaxies recede faster
  • Introducing the Hubble tension between early- and late-universe H0
  • Contrasting space-stretch expansion with motion-through-space Doppler shift

This page does not solve the Friedmann equations and compresses distances for teaching.
