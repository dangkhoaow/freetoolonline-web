How to Play Neon Energy Arena - Step by Step

The Neon Energy Arena page loads a ~90 KB neon arena roguelite in an iframe. You fight geometric enemies, switch four weapons, bank Energy, and buy permanent upgrades. Press Play to start.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

WASD move; mouse aim; LMB shoot; F switch weapon; Shift dash; Space bomb; Esc pause. Energy and upgrades save to ftol:neonenergyarena:*.

Step 1 - press Play and Start run

Press Play to inject the iframe (~90 KB). On the start menu pick Start run for combat, or Upgrade shop to spend Energy. The engine is neon-deep-space by ou178 (MIT); LICENSE and CREDITS ship beside the game files.

Step 2 - fly, aim, and shoot

WASD moves the ship. Aim with the mouse and hold LMB to fire. Press F to cycle Pulse Blaster, Blast Shotgun, Photon Sniper, and Plasma Rail. Watch Weapon Lv and Speed on the HUD.

Step 3 - dash, bombs, and pickups

Press Shift to dash when Ready. Press Space to drop a bomb. Collect health, weapon, freeze, bomb, orbiter, and speed pickups that enemies drop. Dash cooldown shows as a bar when not Ready.

Step 4 - spend Energy in the shop

Between runs, open Upgrade shop. Energy buys Tactical Bomb, Orbiter Escort, Weapon Boost, Quantum Drive, Nano Armor, Time Dilate, Orbiter Expand, Cryo Core, and Recoil Damper. Each upgrade has a max level and rising cost.

Step 5 - pause, mute, and reset

Esc opens pause (Resume, Restart, Main menu). Mute toggles procedural audio. Reset save in the shop clears ftol:neonenergyarena:energy, upgrades, and high_score after confirm.
