Cara menjelajah Earthshine 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch the dark part of a crescent Moon glow with reflected earthlight.

  1. Open /space-3d/earthshine.html and wait for the crescent Moon.
  2. See the bright crescent lit by the Sun and the faint glow on the rest of the disk.
  3. Find the blue Earth marker - that is where the earthshine comes from.
  4. Slide the phase from a thin crescent toward half and watch the glow change.
  5. Toggle the earthshine off to see the night side go dark, and read the facts panel.
