How to explore Zodiacal Light 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and see why the faint triangle of light leans up the ecliptic.

  1. Open /space-3d/zodiacal-light.html and wait for the night sky.
  2. Find the soft glow at the horizon where the Sun has set.
  3. Follow the triangular cone leaning up along the ecliptic.
  4. Toggle the gegenschein to see the faint backscatter glow opposite the Sun.
  5. Drag to look around and read the facts panel for the cause and best viewing times.
