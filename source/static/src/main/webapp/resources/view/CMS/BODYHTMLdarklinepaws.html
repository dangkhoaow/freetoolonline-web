DARKLINE PAWS

Three modes in one menu: a first-person maze, Tic-Tac-Toe vs an unbeatable cat, and a rooftop runner. From DarkLine-Paws by shaurya6903 (MIT, JS13K). About ~36 KB downloads once when you press Play.

Keyboard: WASD/arrows move in the maze, Shift runs, Space jumps in the runner, click cells in Tic-Tac-Toe. Best with a keyboard.

Press Play to load the game.
Session-only play - no save data in this build.
