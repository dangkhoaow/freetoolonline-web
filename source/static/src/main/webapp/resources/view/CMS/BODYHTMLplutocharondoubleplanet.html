About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Scrub phase to update the mutual orbit.

NASA New Horizons and JPL figures put Pluto's mean radius at 1188.3 km and Charon's at 606 km. Masses are about 1.303e22 kg and 1.586e21 kg (~8.2:1). Center-to-center separation is ~19,591 km; the barycenter sits ~2,110 km from Pluto's center - outside Pluto's surface. The synchronous period is 6.387 days and both bodies are tidally locked.
