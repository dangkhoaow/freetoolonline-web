Como jogar Star Fuel Battle - passo a passo

The Star Fuel Battle page loads a ~0.67 MB canvas spaceship dogfight in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, click Start game, then fly with WASD and fire with Space while managing fuel.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~0.67 MB). Focus the frame so keyboard input reaches the ship.

Step 2 - start the run

Inside the iframe, click Start game on the rules screen when loading finishes.

Step 3 - fly and fire

WASD moves the ship. Space fires. Collect falling fuel bottles (+15, max 30). Fuel drops 1 per second from a start of 15.

Step 4 - score carefully

Enemy hits give +5. Planets need two hits for +10. Hitting friends costs -10 score. Colliding with enemies costs 15 fuel. P pauses; M mutes. Enter a name after the run for on-device ranking (ftol:starfuelbattle:gameData).
