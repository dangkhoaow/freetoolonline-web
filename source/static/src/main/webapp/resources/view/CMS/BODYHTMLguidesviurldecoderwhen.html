URL Decoder dang dung mo khi ban can xu ly nhanh ma khong can cai dat gi. Trang nay giai thich khi nao phu hop va khi nao khong.


Khi phu hop

URL Decoder phu hop khi ban can tao gia tri query string, giai ma mot lien ket duoc chia se, hoac kiem tra mot tham so co ma phan tram.


Nhung gi can biet

URL Decoder la cong cu xu ly van ban tren trinh duyet, ma hoa va giai ma ky tu phan tram cuc bo, su dung encodeURIComponent/decodeURIComponent cua trinh duyet - khong can tai len va khong can tai khoan.
