When to Use Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Open the Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer when you need giant-planet magnetosphere scale - ~420 uT field, ~20,000x Earth's volume, magnetotail past Saturn - and skip it when you only need Earth's ~6-10 Re case.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer, press Compressed, and pause so students see the orange magnetopause shrink from ~90 Rj toward ~50 Rj while the magnetotail rings keep stretching past the Saturn marker.


Scale literacy in Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer

Scale literacy in Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer stays readable when you keep the facts panel open for ~20,000x and ~9.5 AU while toggling the solar-wind particle stream.


Sessions Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer does not fit are MHD modeling, live solar-wind data, or Io plasma-torus physics. Use Earth Magnetosphere 3D for the ~6-10 Re case or Galilean Moons 3D for Io. Walk the controls in the Jupiter Magnetosphere 3D Explorer step-by-step guide.
