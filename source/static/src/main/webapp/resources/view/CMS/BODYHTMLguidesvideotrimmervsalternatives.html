Online Video Trimmer Vs Alternatives

Last reviewed: 2026-07-03

Online Video Trimmer is one of three common ways to do this: a browser-based tool like this one, a desktop application, or an upload-based online service. Each trades off differently on where your file goes and what you need installed.


How Online Video Trimmer compares

Online Video Trimmer runs in the browser without uploading your file. Here is how that compares to desktop apps and server-based services across the tradeoffs that matter most.

Trimming does not re-encode - it runs in FFmpeg's stream-copy mode, so the original resolution, bitrate, and codec are kept exactly as they were.

The file is processed in this browser tab (FFmpeg runs as a WebAssembly module loaded once per session) and is never uploaded to a server.
