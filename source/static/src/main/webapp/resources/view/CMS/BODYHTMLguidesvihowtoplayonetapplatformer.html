How to Play One Tap Platformer

One Tap Platformer loads about ~34 KB in an iframe after Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Press Play, then tap or click inside the frame to dash through stages.

Launch

Click Play on this page to inject the game iframe.

Dash

Tap or click to launch a dash in the aimed direction; timing matters against enemies.

Progress

Tutorial, stage, and wave progress persist in namespaced localStorage.
