When to use Sunspot Magnetic Loops 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Use this explorer when you want to see the magnetic structure over a sunspot region - and skip it when you want the 11-year sunspot count cycle or the Sun interior layers.

  • Classroom demos of coronal loops and bipolar sunspot pairs
  • Connecting the umbral field strength to why sunspots are dark
  • Showing that loops trace invisible magnetic field lines

This is an educational visualization with illustrative loop shapes and stylized plasma flow.
