Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rock Paper Neural?

A free browser rock-paper-scissors game where a small neural network trains on your move history and tries to beat your next choice.

How do I play?

Click or tap Rock, Paper, or Scissors. On a keyboard press J for Rock, K for Paper, L for Scissors. Scores for player, computer, and ties update each round.

When does the AI start learning?

The first few rounds are mostly random. After enough history, the net predicts your next move and picks the counter. It only trains when it did not win the previous round.

Does it save my history?

No. Training is session-only in memory. Refresh clears the model and scores.

How big is the download?

About ~20 KB of HTML, JavaScript, CSS, and a small sprite sheet after you press Play.
