Welcome to Progress Knight - a free medieval life-sim incremental with no account or install. Climb jobs from beggar upward, learn skills, buy shop gear, watch age advance against your lifespan, then prestige on the Amulet tab for stronger next lives. About 0.12 MB loads after you press Play. Progress stays on-device under ftol:progressknight:gameDataSave and is never uploaded. Use Pause in the sidebar when you step away. For more free browser games, open the games hub.
