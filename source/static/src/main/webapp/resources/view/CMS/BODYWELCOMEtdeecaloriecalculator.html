TDEE & Calorie Calculator

Last reviewed: 2026-07-24

Estimate daily calorie needs from sex, age, weight, height, and activity level.

Use this calculator when you want a calorie target for maintaining, losing, or gaining weight - it is not the same check as the BMI calculator (weight-for-height only, no calorie number).

Uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula to estimate BMR (calories at rest), then applies your activity level to get TDEE (total daily energy expenditure) and a loss/gain target 500 kcal/day either side of that.

The calculation runs locally in the browser - no upload and no account.

Does not account for medical conditions, medications, or individual metabolic variation - this is a population-average estimate, not medical or nutritional advice.
