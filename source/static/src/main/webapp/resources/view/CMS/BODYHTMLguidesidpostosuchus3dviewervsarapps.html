When the Postosuchus browser viewer fits

Pick the browser viewer when you want drag-orbit with Size vs human at the honest 5.5 m panel length ratio and click facts - without installing anything.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for real mounts.


What this Postosuchus comparison is not

This comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - not an AR brand ranking, no invented AR features, no settled gait claim, states Postosuchus was not a dinosaur, and no game score. For controls see how to view Postosuchus in 3D. For size see Postosuchus size comparison.
