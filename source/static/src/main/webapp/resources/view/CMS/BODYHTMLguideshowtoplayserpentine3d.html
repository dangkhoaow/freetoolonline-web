How to Play Serpentine 3D - Step by Step

The Serpentine 3D page loads a ~650 KB WebGL 3D snake game in an iframe. Three difficulty presets, particle effects, and per-difficulty local high scores.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play on the page, choose Easy/Medium/Hard, press PLAY then START. Steer with arrow keys or WASD on desktop, or the on-screen joystick on phones. Pause with P; open settings for quality and particle sliders.

Step 1 - launch and difficulty

Click Play on the game page. Inside the iframe, pick one of the three difficulty cards (each changes speed, wall mode, and scoring). Press PLAY to open the briefing screen.

Step 2 - controls and HUD

Press START to enter the arena. The HUD shows score, high score, length, speed, and wall mode. Toggle joystick vs mouse mode with the control button on touch devices.
