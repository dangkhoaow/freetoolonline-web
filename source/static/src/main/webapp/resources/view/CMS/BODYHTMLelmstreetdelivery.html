Elm Street Delivery is a free browser courier game. You are Joe on a bicycle - pick up fashion boxes, plan a short route, and deliver before customers turn unhappy. About 3 MB after Play. No account.

Want a different planning puzzle? Try Pipe Rotate Puzzle. Prefer a short point-and-click story? Try The House Pointclick.

ELM STREET DELIVERY

WebGL courier route game. Click houses and the warehouse to pick up and deliver boxes before customers lose patience. Adapted from zalando/elm-street-404 (MIT). About 3 MB.

Mouse or tap to click destinations. On a phone, landscape helps. First load is larger, then cached.
