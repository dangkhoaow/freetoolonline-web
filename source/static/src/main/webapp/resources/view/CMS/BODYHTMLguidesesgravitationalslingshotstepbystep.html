Como explorar Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Gravitational Slingshot 3D Explorer, read the ~+10 km/s Voyager figure, and scrub periapsis while the craft flies the hyperbola.

  1. Open /space-3d/gravitational-slingshot.html and wait for the planet, green velocity arrow, and blue path.
  2. Read the facts panel: ~+10 km/s teaching delta-v and periapsis in R.
  3. Scrub Periapsis (R) to bend the flyby more or less.
  4. Scrub Flyby phase, or press Pause/Play flyby.
  5. Compare with Hohmann Transfer 3D when you need two burns instead of an assist.
