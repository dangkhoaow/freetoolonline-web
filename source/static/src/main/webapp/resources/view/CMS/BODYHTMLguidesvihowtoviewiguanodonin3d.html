Doc bang su that Iguanodon va bam gai ngon cai

Bang su that Iguanodon liet ke chieu dai khoang 9-13 m (thuong trich khoang 10 m; Size vs human dung 10 m), chieu cao hong khoang 2-3 m khi di bon chan (cao hon khi di hai chan), khoi luong khoang 3-5 tan, cua so 126-113 Mya Creta som (Barremian-Aptian) o vung nay la Chau Au, va lich su dat ten - Gideon Mantell dat ten chi nam 1825 tu rang va xuong roi o Cuckfield, Sussex, mot trong ba chi khung long dau tien duoc dat ten chinh thuc (voi Megalosaurus 1824 va Hylaeosaurus 1833), nhieu nam truoc khi Dinosauria duoc dat nam 1842. Mantell ban dau dung lai gai ngon cai hinh non thanh sung mui; loi do duoc sua sau phat hien Bernissart, Bi nam 1878 voi khoang 38 bo xuong gan day du. Moi ban tay mang gai ngon cai lon hinh non - chuc nang chinh xac van tranh luan (phong thu, kiem an, hoac danh nhau giua doi thu) va khong co giai thich nao duoc chung minh. Mot cum bam ngan - khong keo - tren gai ngon cai, dau, tay, duoi, hoac chan se hien su that hoa thach ngan; bam cho trong de quay lai to loai.
