How to Play The House Point and Click - Step by Step

The The House Point and Click page loads a ~18 MB point-and-click house adventure in an iframe from the FreeToolOnline game CDN.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, click hotspots, collect items, and move through linked rooms.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the CDN iframe (~18 MB). The game boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - explore the starting room

Click furniture, views, and exits. Drag the room if the view is larger than the stage.

Step 3 - use the inventory

Open the inventory tray, pick an item, then click a hotspot that accepts it.

Step 4 - follow scenes

Dialogue and scene changes unlock new rooms. Use reset game in settings only if you want a fresh start.
