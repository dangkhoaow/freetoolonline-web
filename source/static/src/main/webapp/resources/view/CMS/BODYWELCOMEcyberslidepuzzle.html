Cyber Slide Puzzle

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play and a full sliding-tile puzzle loads in the tab. Choose 3x3, 4x4, or 5x5, shuffle the board, then slide tiles into the empty cell until every number sits in order. Moves and elapsed time track automatically; beating your best time per grid size is saved locally.

Controls at a glance:

ActionInput
Move a tileClick or tap a tile adjacent to the empty space
Change difficultyDifficulty dropdown (3x3 / 4x4 / 5x5)
ShuffleShuffle button
ResetReset button
Play again after winPlay Again on the win overlay

Best times per board size save locally under ftol:cyberslidepuzzle:best in your browser only. The game is adapted from Alfred Ang's open-source OpenClaw Puzzle Game (MIT license); credits and license text ship next to the game files.
