Como explorar Planetary Rings Comparison 3D Explorer

Open the Planetary Rings Comparison 3D Explorer, click through each planet, then read the facts panel for the real ring literacy behind each one.

  1. Open /space-3d/planetary-rings-comparison.html and wait for all four planets to appear.
  2. Click Saturn to see its 7 major rings and water-ice composition.
  3. Click Uranus to compare its 13 narrow, dark rings.
  4. Click Neptune to see the partial ring arcs, then Jupiter for its faint dusty ring.
  5. Compare with Saturn Rings 3D for a deep dive into just one ring system.
