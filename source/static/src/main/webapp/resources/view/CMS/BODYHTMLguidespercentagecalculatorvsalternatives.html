Percentage Calculator is one of three common ways to calculate a percentage: a browser-based tool like this one, a desktop application, or an upload-based online service. Each trades off differently on where your numbers go and what you need installed.

When to pick Percentage Calculator

Pick Percentage Calculator for a one-off discount, tax add-on, grade, or percent-change calculation when you do not want to install anything and the numbers stay on your device. The three calculation types - percentage of a number, what percent one number is of another, and percent change - are all available from the dropdown with no formula to write.

Pick a desktop option when you need offline access or plan to run many calculations in a batch. Pick an upload-based service only if your data is already in a format that service processes.
