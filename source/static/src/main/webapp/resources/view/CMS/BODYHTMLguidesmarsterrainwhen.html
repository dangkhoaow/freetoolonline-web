When to Use Mars Terrain Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Open the Mars Terrain Explorer when you need real NASA MOLA elevation literacy - Olympus Mons near ~21.2 km, Hellas near ~-8.2 km, and Valles Marineris about 3,870 km long - without installing planetary GIS software.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Mars Terrain Explorer, drag Relief + so Olympus Mons rises, then click the globe for a real elevation reading - clearer than listing four MOLA numbers on a slide alone.


Relief versus planetary scale

Relief versus planetary scale stays readable when you toggle Real scale and see how subtle true relief is against Mars mean diameter 6,779 km. The facts panel keeps the published elevation range -8,201 m to +21,241 m relative to the areoid.


Sessions Mars Terrain Explorer does not fit

Sessions Mars Terrain Explorer does not fit are full Mars imagery browsers, rover-path planners, shapefile editors, or live telemetry. Use Earth 3D Globe for Earth lighting geography or Planet Size Comparison for plain spheres. Walk the controls in the Mars Terrain Explorer step-by-step guide.
