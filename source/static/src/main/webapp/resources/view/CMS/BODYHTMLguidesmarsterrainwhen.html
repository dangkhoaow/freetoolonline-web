When to Use Mars Terrain Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-15

Open Mars Terrain Explorer when you need real Olympus Mons and Hellas Planitia elevation literacy - without installing planetary GIS software.

Use it for a classroom warm-up on why "tallest mountain in the solar system" is true in absolute terms but nearly invisible at planetary scale, homework comparing highland and lowland elevations, or a quick click-around to read real MOLA numbers.

  • Best for: real elevation-data literacy and relief-vs-scale intuition
  • Skip if: you need full Mars imagery, rover routes, or GIS-grade analysis
  • Privacy: runs in the browser - no upload, no account
