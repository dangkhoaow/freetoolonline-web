Welcome to Feast Night - a free browser medieval first-person game you can play without an account or install. Press Play to load the ~17 KB WebGL build in a same-origin iframe. Click the canvas to begin, walk with WASD or arrow keys, look with the mouse, answer villagers by nodding or shaking your head, and fire when demons appear so you can save souls and reach the gate. Privacy note: nothing leaves this device except the initial page and game files from this site. For more free browser games, open the games hub.
