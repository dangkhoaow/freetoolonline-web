Doc bang su that Titanoboa va nhap mot phan

Bang su that Titanoboa liet ke chieu dai khoang 12.8 m trung binh (toi da khoang 14.3 m; Head et al. 2009), chieu cao giua than khoang 0.7-1.0 m (panel 0.8 m), khoi luong khoang 1135 kg trung binh (khoang 730-1819 kg; Head et al. 2009), cua so Paleocene khoang 60-58 trieu nam truoc o Formacion Cerrejon Colombia, thuc an thit (thu san thuy; thuong suy ra sinh thai giong anaconda), va dat ten tu Head et al. 2009 (loai type T. cerrejonensis). Mot nhap ngan - khong keo - tren mot vung co the hien su that hoa thach ngan. Mau mo mem la tai tao nghe thuat.
