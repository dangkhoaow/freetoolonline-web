Egg Time Rewind is a free low-res Nokia-style arena shooter. When you die, time rewinds and your parallel lives stay on the field to help. Break the space egg for 50 points. About ~60 KB after Play, no install.

Want rock-paper-scissors vs a neural net? Try Rock Paper Neural. Want a related Frank Force arena? Try Bounce Back.

EGG TIME REWIND

Move with WASD, arrows, mouse, or gamepad. Shoot with Space, Shift, click, or a gamepad button. Die and time rewinds - parallel lives remain. R resets. Adapted from Frank Force's EggTimeRewind (GPL-2.0). About ~60 KB.

Keyboard or mouse preferred. High score saves in this browser only (ftol key).

Press Play to load the game.
High score uses localStorage key ftol:eggtimerewind:highScore.
