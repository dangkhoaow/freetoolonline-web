When the Brontotherium browser viewer fits

Pick the Brontotherium browser viewer when you want drag-orbit on a Late Eocene brontothere mammal with Size vs human at the honest about 4.6 m panel length ratio, and click facts - without installing anything. Nothing about the visit is sent to a server.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your living-room floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for walking beside real brontothere mounts. A static image wins only when you need a single shareable frame with no interactivity.


What this Brontotherium comparison is not

This Brontotherium comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - it is not a ranking of commercial AR brands, it does not invent AR features the page does not have, it does not call Brontotherium a dinosaur, it does not invent one precise mass beyond the disclosed range, it does not hide the Megacerops synonymy, it does not claim the optional glTF is a scientifically exact reconstruction, and it does not invent game score or win states.
