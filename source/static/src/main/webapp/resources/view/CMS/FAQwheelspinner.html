Frequently Asked Questions

What does Wheel Spinner Online do?

Splits a list of names or options into equal slices on a wheel, spins it, and lands on one entry as a fair random winner.

Is every entry equally likely to win?

Yes - the wheel divides into one equal-size slice per entry, and the winning slice is chosen with crypto.getRandomValues, so list order does not affect the odds.

Is the list saved between visits?

No. The list you type lives only in the page - refreshing or leaving clears it, and nothing is uploaded or stored on a server.

What complementary tools work well alongside wheel spinner online?

The countdown timer and stopwatch pair well when the spin decides who goes first in a timed activity, and the to-do list tracks the tasks you just assigned.
