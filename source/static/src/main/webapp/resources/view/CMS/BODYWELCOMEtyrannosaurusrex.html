Tyrannosaurus rex 3D Viewer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

This page renders a Tyrannosaurus rex as a 3D model you can spin in the browser: drag to rotate, pinch to zoom, add a 1.8 m person for scale, and click a body part for a fact.

Tyrannosaurus rex lived in the Late Cretaceous, roughly 68-66 million years ago, in western North America - about 12.3 m long, 3.7 m tall at the hips, weighing near 8.9 tonnes, with around 60 serrated teeth up to 20 cm long.

The first named skeleton was described in 1902 from a Montana find by Barnum Brown. The model shows its short two-fingered arms and heavy, head-balancing tail.
