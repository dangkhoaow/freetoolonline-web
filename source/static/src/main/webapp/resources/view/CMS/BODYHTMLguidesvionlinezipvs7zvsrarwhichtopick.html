Zip Online vs 7z vs Rar - Chon Cai Nao

Cap nhat lan cuoi: 2026-04-27. Mo cong cu zip de tao kho luu tru trong trinh duyet ma khong tai len.

Cau tra loi 30 giay. Zip khi nguoi nhan tren Windows, macOS hoac bat ky dien thoai nao - day la dinh dang duy nhat mo ma khong can phan mem bo sung. 7z khi ban kiem soat cong cu cua nguoi nhan va muon kho luu tru nho nhat. Rar khi ban phai mo mot cai.

Moi dinh dang la gi

  • Zip. Dinh dang cua Phil Katz nam 1989. Native tren Windows tu XP, native tren macOS, native tren Android va iOS. Tuong thich pho quat, nen vua phai.
  • 7z. Dinh dang cua Igor Pavlov nam 1999. Su dung LZMA2 mac dinh - thuong nho hon zip 10-30% tren kho luu tru nhieu van ban. Can 7-Zip, The Unarchiver hoac app tra phi de mo.
  • Rar. Dinh dang doc quyen tu 1993. Mien phi de trich xuat, tra phi de tao bang WinRAR chinh thuc. Nen manh tuong tu 7z.

De gui: dung zip tru khi ca hai ben co thu gi do tot hon duoc cai dat. Xem zip vs zipx vs rar vs 7z, giai thich de so sanh sau hon.
