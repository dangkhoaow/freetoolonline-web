Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mochi and the Midnight Escape?

A free browser pixel platformer with nine levels. Guide Mochi, collect stars, and reach each exit flag.

How do I play?

Press Play, then use WASD or arrow keys to move, Up/W to jump, and Enter/Space to confirm title screens. A standard gamepad also works.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Your current level is stored in this browser under the key ftol:mochimidnightescape.

How big is the download?

About ~52 KB of HTML with inlined canvas art and logic after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from Jasper Renow-Clarke's js13kGames 2025 entry (MIT). This build adds robots noindex and namespaced localStorage.

Is it good on a phone?

It runs, but there are no on-screen touch buttons. A keyboard or gamepad works best.
