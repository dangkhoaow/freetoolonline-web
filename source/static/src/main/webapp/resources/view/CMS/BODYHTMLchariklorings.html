About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Toggle the scale comparison to see Chariklo next to Saturn's rings.

Astronomers found Chariklo's rings on 2013-06-03 by watching it pass in front of a distant star (a stellar occultation) - the star's light flickered twice on each side, a signature that had never been seen around anything smaller than a giant planet before.
