Sky Gates Flight

Last reviewed: 2026-07-05

Fly a little plane over the ocean and steer through the gates - green ones add their number to your power, red ones subtract it, and the run ends at zero.

The world ramps up as you fly - speed climbs every ten seconds, red gates get harsher with time, and an occasional gold x2 gate doubles your power.

Your best distance and peak power are saved in this browser's local storage and shown before you even start - no accounts, uploads, or leaderboards.
