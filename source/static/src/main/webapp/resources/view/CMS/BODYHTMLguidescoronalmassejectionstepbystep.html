How to Explore Coronal Mass Ejection 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Coronal Mass Ejection 3D Explorer, scrub CME speed, then play or drag progress as the cloud expands from the Sun toward Earth and read the travel-time estimate.

  1. Open /space-3d/coronal-mass-ejection.html and wait for the Sun, Earth, and orange particle cloud to paint.
  2. Drag CME speed toward 2500-3000 km/s and note the travel-time estimate moving into the 15-18 hour fast-arrival ballpark.
  3. Press Play (or Pause) and watch progress climb; or drag Progress from 0% toward Earth.
  4. Reset to restart from the Sun, then try a slower scrub near 250-800 km/s to see multi-day arrivals.
  5. Read the facts panel for <250 to nearly 3000 km/s, 15-18 hours, several days / ~1-3 days, and billions of tons (~1e16 g class).
