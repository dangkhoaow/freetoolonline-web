How to Explore the Photon Sphere & ISCO 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-24

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Photon Sphere & ISCO 3D Explorer, drag the Kerr spin-parameter slider, and watch the black hole's four orbit rings and its ergosphere update live from the exact Kerr formulas.

  1. Open /space-3d/photon-sphere-isco-explorer.html and wait for the black sphere, the four color-coded rings, and the purple ergosphere to render.
  2. Drag the Kerr spin parameter slider (a* = 0 to 0.998) to change the black hole's spin.
  3. Click "Reset (a*=0)" to jump to the non-spinning Schwarzschild case, or "Thorne limit (a*=0.998)" to jump to the astrophysical spin ceiling.
  4. Watch the cyan/magenta photon-orbit rings and the green/orange ISCO rings pull in (prograde) or push out (retrograde) as spin changes.
  5. Watch the purple frame-dragging arrows near the ergosphere spin faster as spin increases.
  6. Read the facts panel for the exact computed radii, in both M (gravitational-radius) and Rs (Schwarzschild-radius) units.
