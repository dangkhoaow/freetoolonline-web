When to Use Exoplanet Atmosphere Spectroscopy 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-23

Open the Exoplanet Atmosphere Spectroscopy 3D Explorer when you need a real transmission-spectroscopy lesson with published WASP-39b molecule detections - without a live JWST data pipeline.


A molecule-detection lesson

A lesson starts when you toggle a molecule and scrub the transit slider, watching the absorption bumps on the spectrum panel appear only while the planet actually crosses its star - the same reason real transmission spectroscopy only works during a transit.


Sessions this explorer does not fit

Sessions this page does not fit are lessons about detecting a planet at all and sizing it from the light-curve dip alone - use the Exoplanet Transit 3D Explorer for that. Walk the controls in the step-by-step guide.
