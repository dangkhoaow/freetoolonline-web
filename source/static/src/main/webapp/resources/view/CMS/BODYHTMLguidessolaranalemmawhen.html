When to use Solar Analemma 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Use the Solar Analemma 3D Explorer when you need the noon-Sun figure-8 - and skip it when you need Earth seasons geometry or sidereal vs solar day length alone.

  • Classroom demos of why fixed-time Sun photos form a figure-8
  • Linking obliquity 23.44 deg and equation of time before sundial units
  • One-screen refresher on EoT ~+/-16 min without a textbook plate

This page does not run an ephemeris, does not set a geographic latitude, and exaggerates curve scale for teaching.
