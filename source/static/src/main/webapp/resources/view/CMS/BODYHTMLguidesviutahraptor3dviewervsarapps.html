Khi nao viewer trinh duyet Utahraptor phu hop

Chon viewer trinh duyet khi ban muon xoay raptor lon nhat da biet, Size vs human o ti le 6.1 m trung thuc, va bam de doc su that - khong cai gi. Khong gui chuyen tham len server - tot cho laptop lop hoc hoac kiem tra nhanh so lieu va ghi chu khoi mo 2001.


Khi nao ung dung AR hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi can bong tren san phong; viewer nay khong bao gio tuyen bo dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di canh xuong Utahraptor lap. Anh tinh thang chi khi can mot khung chia se khong tuong tac.


So sanh Utahraptor nay khong phai la

Day la bang trade-off cho viewer da ship - khong xep hang thuong hieu AR, khong bia tinh nang AR trang khong co, khong bia mot can nang chinh xac ngoai khoang 280-700 kg, khong tuyen bo san bay dan da xac nhan, khong tuyen bo Utahraptor la mo hinh Velociraptor Jurassic Park, khong bia credit glTF, va khong bia diem.
