How to explore Comet Tail Types 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch a comet grow two tails as it rounds the Sun.

  1. Open /space-3d/comet-tail-types.html and wait for the Sun and the comet.
  2. Spot the straight blue ion tail and the broad curved yellow dust tail.
  3. Watch both tails point away from the Sun as the comet swings around.
  4. Note the tails growing near the Sun and shrinking far away.
  5. Use the speed slider and the Sun-line toggle to follow the geometry.
