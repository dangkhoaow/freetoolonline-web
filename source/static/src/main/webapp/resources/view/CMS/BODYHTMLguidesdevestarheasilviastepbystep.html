How to explore Vesta Rheasilvia Basin 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and spin Vesta so the south pole faces you.

  1. Open /space-3d/vesta-rheasilvia.html and wait for Vesta to appear.
  2. Press Play and rotate until the blue basin bowl faces the camera.
  3. Toggle Peak to highlight the central mountain rising from the floor.
  4. Drag to orbit around the equator, then return to the south pole.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow or speed the rotation.
