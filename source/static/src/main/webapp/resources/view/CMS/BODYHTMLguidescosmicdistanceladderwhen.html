When to use Cosmic Distance Ladder 3D Explorer

Use the Cosmic Distance Ladder 3D Explorer when you need a quick browser view of AU, ly, and pc on one log zoom - and skip it when you need a full extragalactic distance pipeline.

  • Classroom demos comparing 1 AU, 1 ly, and 1 pc figures
  • Placing parallax reach (~1000 pc) before naming Cepheids as the next rung
  • One-screen refresher before parallax-distance or stellar-magnitude siblings

This page does not solve Cepheid distances, does not catalog galaxies, and uses a teaching log scale rather than a true universe layout.
