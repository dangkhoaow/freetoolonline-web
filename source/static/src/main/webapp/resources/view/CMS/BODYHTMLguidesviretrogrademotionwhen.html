Mo Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer khi ban can hinh hoc vuot cua Trai Dat khien Sao Hoa co ve ve mot vong lap tren troi - hop trieu khoang 779.9 ngay (~26 thang) va cung vong cung khoang 72 ngay - ma khong can ephemeris chinh xac hay lich doi dien co ngay cu the.

So hop trieu trong Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer

Kien thuc hop trieu trong Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer van de doc khi ban giu bang thong tin mo trong khoang 779.9 ngay va khoang 72 ngay trong khi Sao Hoa van giu quy dao thuan quanh Mat Troi - doan lui chi la hien tuong nhin thay, khong phai chuyen dong nhat tam thuc.


Buoi ma Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Buoi ma Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer khong phu hop la ephemeris chinh xac, ban do chom sao toi nay, luc hap dan N vat the, hoac tour tam hanh tinh. Hay dung Solar System 3D Explorer, Seasons Earth 3D Explorer, hoac Kepler Orbits 3D Explorer thay the. Di qua cac dieu khien trong huong dan tung buoc Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer.
