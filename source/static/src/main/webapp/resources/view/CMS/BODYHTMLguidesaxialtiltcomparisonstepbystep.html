How to Explore Axial Tilt Comparison 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Axial Tilt Comparison 3D Explorer, compare all eight planets at a shared reference size, then click each one for its exact tilt angle.

  1. Open /space-3d/axial-tilt-comparison.html and wait for eight spinning spheres to paint in a row.
  2. Click Earth (the third sphere, mid-tone blue) for its 23.44-degree tilt and the seasons note.
  3. Click Uranus (pale cyan) for its 97.77-degree tilt - tipped nearly onto its side.
  4. Click Venus (pale gold) for its 177.4-degree figure and the retrograde-rotation explanation.
  5. Toggle the "Show tilt-axis lines" checkbox off and on to see the white axis guides disappear and reappear.
