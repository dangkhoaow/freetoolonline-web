When you want a beat-em-up with cats

Bone-animated feline fighters, punch/kick/block timing, and wave pressure.

When you want keyboard or gamepad

WASD + H/J/B on keyboard, or a standard gamepad with stick + face buttons.

When to pick another game

Want a platformer with levels and saves? Use Mochi Midnight Escape. Want a short bounce reflex loop? Use Bounce Back.
