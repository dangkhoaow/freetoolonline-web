So nutzen Sie Sonnenfleckenzyklus 3D Explorer

Open the Sonnenfleckenzyklus 3D Explorer, read the ~11 yr / ~25 d / ~35 d / Maunder 1645-1715 figures, and scrub the cycle year slider while watching spot counts change.

  1. Open /space-3d/sunspot-cycle.html and wait for the photosphere, latitude belts, and spot groups.
  2. Read the facts panel: Schwabe ~11 yr, equator ~25 d, poles ~35 d, Maunder 1645-1715.
  3. Scrub cycle year from 0 toward 5-6 to see more umbra/penumbra spots at maximum.
  4. Press Play cycle, then Pause cycle, to auto-advance one Schwabe period.
  5. Toggle Maunder Minimum to compare a nearly spot-free era (1645-1715).
