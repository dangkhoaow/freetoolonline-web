Frequently Asked Questions

What does City Time Machine 3D do?

Drag the 1945-2055 timeline and watch one city block's buildings, vehicles, signs, street lights, and people transform live in front of you.

When should I reach for city time machine 3d?

Each lot keeps its own footprint through time, so the small 1945 corner shop grows into the same corner's 1985 office and 2055 tower.

What complementary tools work well alongside city time machine 3d?

It is a stylized, artistic take on generic architectural eras with invented shop names - not a historical record of any real city, and 2055 is openly speculative fiction.
