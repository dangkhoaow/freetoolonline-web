Como jugar Boing Cat Platformer

Boing Cat Platformer loads a ~130 KB auto-run platformer in an iframe. The cat runs forward on its own; Space jumps over spikes and gaps across three levels.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Three fixed levels, session-only (no save data). Engine MIT (BoingKat, Make Classic Games, JS13K 2025).
