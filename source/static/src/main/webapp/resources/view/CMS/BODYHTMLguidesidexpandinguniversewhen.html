When to Use Expanding Universe 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Open the Expanding Universe 3D Explorer when you need the space-stretch expansion mechanism - Hubble Law v=H0d, the 67.4-73.5 km/s/Mpc H0 tension - and skip it when you only need a single-source Doppler shift or the wider distance ladder.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Expanding Universe 3D Explorer, press Play, and pause so students see the far orange marker's recession speed climb faster than the near blue marker's while the grid stretches uniformly.


Hubble Law literacy in Expanding Universe 3D Explorer

Hubble Law literacy in Expanding Universe 3D Explorer stays readable when you keep the facts panel open for the H0 tension range and 13.8 billion year age while toggling the recession vectors.


Sessions Expanding Universe 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions Expanding Universe 3D Explorer does not fit are Friedmann-equation solving, single-source Doppler wavelength shift, or a full AU-to-parsec distance ladder. Use Redshift Doppler 3D or Cosmic Distance Ladder 3D instead. Walk the controls in the Expanding Universe 3D Explorer step-by-step guide.
