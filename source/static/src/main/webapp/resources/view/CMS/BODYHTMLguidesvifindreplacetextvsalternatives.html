Find And Replace Text So Sanh Voi Cac Lua Chon Khac

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

Find and Replace Text la mot trong ba cach pho bien de lam viec nay: cong cu chay tren trinh duyet nhu the nay, ung dung desktop, hoac dich vu online phai tai len. Moi cach danh doi khac nhau ve noi file cua ban di den va can cai dat gi.

Dung de doi mot ten trong tai lieu da dan, sua mot loi go lap lai, hoac cap nhat mot gia tri placeholder trong khoi van ban.

Tim kiem la khop van ban theo dung nghia (khong phai bieu thuc chinh quy); o "Match case" dieu khien viec tim co phan biet hoa thuong hay khong.

  • Nhan Replace All va o output hien thi so lan thay the da thuc hien theo thoi gian thuc.
  • Cach nhanh de xac nhan viec thay the da bat dung moi lan xuat hien, dieu ma khong phai hop thoai desktop nao cung hien thi.
