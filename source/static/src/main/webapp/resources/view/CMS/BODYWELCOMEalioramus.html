Alioramus 3D Viewer

This page renders an Alioramus as a 3D model you can spin in the browser - drag to rotate, scroll or pinch to zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person beside it for scale, and click the head, a limb, or the tail to read a fact about that part.

Alioramus lived in the Late Cretaceous of Mongolia (Nemegt Formation), about 72-66 million years ago. Sergei Kurzanov named Alioramus remotus in 1976; a second species, A. altai, was named by Brusatte and colleagues in 2009. It is a slender, long-snouted tyrannosaurid in the tribe Alioramini, distinct from the much larger contemporary Tarbosaurus.
