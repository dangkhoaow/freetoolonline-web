How to use Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer

  1. Open the Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer and wait for the canvas to paint.
  2. Press Play if paused, then drag to see the long axis sweep.
  3. Toggle Ring Off and On to isolate the body shape.
  4. Move Speed while you read the axes and ring table in the facts panel.
  5. Use Fullscreen when teaching occultation results on a large display.
