Doc bang su that Utahraptor va bam mot phan co the

Bang liet ke chieu dai khoang 6-7 m (Size vs human dung 6.1 m; NHMU khoang 20 ft), chieu cao hong khoang 1.5 m, can nang khoang 280-700 kg tuy mau va phuong phap (Kirkland et al. 1993 khoang 280-360 kg; uoc tinh sau toi khoang 500-700 kg), mong liem rut o chan toi 24 cm - lon nhat ghi nhan cho bat ky dromaeosaurid nao - cua so ~139-135 Mya Creta Som (Berriasian-Valanginian) o Formation Cedar Mountain (Yellow Cat Member) gan Moab, Utah, My, va lich su dat ten - James Kirkland, Robert Gaston va Donald Burge dat ten chi nam 1993; loai ban dau goi ostrommaysi, ton vinh John Ostrom va Chris Mays, sau sua thanh ostrommaysorum. Lon hon nhieu so voi Velociraptor (khoang 2 m) va Deinonychus (khoang 3.3-3.4 m) da ship. Phat hien mo 2001 goi Utahraptor block giu di tich it nhat bay ca the - mot truong thanh, bon non va mot con non khoang 1 m - goi y hanh vi xa hoi hoac cham soc bo me co the, khong phai su that san bay dan chot. Che do an la san phuc kich tren dong vat an thuc vat lon nhu ankylosaur va iguanodontian. Long vu suy ra tu dromaeosaurid ho hang, khong quan sat truc tiep tren Utahraptor, va khong render. Bam ngan - khong keo - tren dau, than, co, duoi, chan hoac tay hien su that fossil; mong liem duoc cong bo trong bang va van, khong phai muc tieu bam rieng. Mau da la tai tao nghe thuat; mo hinh procedural chua co glTF Utahraptor mien phi tai-host.
