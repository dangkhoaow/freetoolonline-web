When to Use Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Open the Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer when you need the outer solar-system bubble - termination shock ~94 AU, heliopause ~121 AU, Voyager 1's 2012 crossing - and skip it when you need a full MHD heliosphere run.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer, scrub the wind speed toward 400 km/s, and pause so students see the particle stream pass through the termination shock before reaching the heliopause where Voyager 1 crossed.


Boundary literacy in Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer

Boundary literacy in Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer stays readable when you keep the facts panel open for ~94 AU and ~121 AU while clicking the Voyager 1 marker for its 2012 readout.


Sessions Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer does not fit are MHD equation solving, heliosheath structure forecasting, or precise Earth-position geometry. Use Solar System 3D for the inner planets or Aurora 3D Explorer for solar-wind effects at Earth. Walk the controls in the Solar Wind Heliosphere 3D Explorer step-by-step guide.
