Open the Planetary Nebula 3D Explorer, read the 2,600 ly / 20-30 km/s figures, and scrub age while the shell grows around the white dwarf.

  1. Open /space-3d/planetary-nebula.html and wait for the green ring and bright core.
  2. Read the facts panel: ~2,600 ly, ~20-30 km/s, white-dwarf core.
  3. Scrub Age (kyr) from a young shell toward ~7 kyr (Ring-like age).
  4. Press Pause expand, then Play expand, to control growth.
  5. Toggle Hide/Show outer shell for a clearer inner ring.
