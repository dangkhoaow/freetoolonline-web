Khi app AR hoac bao tang phu hop hon

App AR thang khi can hinh bong tren san phong; viewer nay khong tuyen bo dat AR. Bao tang thang de di can vat lieu hoa thach tyrannosaurid that.


So sanh nay khong phai gi
