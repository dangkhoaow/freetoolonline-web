How to Play Starlight Breaker - Step by Step

The Starlight Breaker page loads an ~84 KB Canvas brick-breaker in an iframe. You control a deer paddle, bounce a star ball through bricks, and clear the board before you run out of three lives.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press Play on the page, then START GAME inside the iframe. Move the paddle with mouse or touch drag. Esc or Pause opens the pause menu. High scores persist under ftol:starlightbreaker:highScores in localStorage only.

Step 1 - launch the iframe

Press Play on the game page. The iframe loads index.html, styles.css, game.js, deer.svg, and start.svg (~84 KB total).

Step 2 - start a run

Click START GAME on the title menu. On mobile, dismiss the touch guide if shown - preference saves to ftol:starlightbreaker:hasSeenTouchGuide.

Step 3 - control the deer paddle

Move the mouse over the canvas or drag your finger on touch devices. The paddle follows horizontally; keep the star ball above the paddle.

Step 4 - pause, scores, and saves

Press Esc or tap Pause to freeze play. Open High Scores from the main menu to view local records. Clear Scores wipes ftol:starlightbreaker:highScores. No account, no cloud sync, zero server calls during play.
