Khi Nao Dung Aurora 3D Explorer - Phien Tot Nhat va Gioi Han

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Mo Aurora 3D Explorer khi ban can hieu oval cuc quang va do cao phat xa - oxy xanh 557.7 nm khoang 90-150 km, oxy do 630.0 nm khoang 200-300 km, va Kp day hoc tu 0 den 9 - khong can planetarium hay du bao truc tiep.


Demo lop hoc nam phut

Demo lop hoc nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Aurora 3D Explorer, dat lai Kp day hoc 3, bam Play spin, roi tang Kp + cho den khi oval gian ve phia xich dao. Tam dung va doc do cao xanh doi do trong bang su kien - nhanh hon chi mot ban ve oval tinh.


Mau sac doi do cao

Mau sac doi do cao van doc duoc khi bang su kien mo: oval yen gan 65-70 do vi do tu, N2+ 427.8 nm o ria duoi tia, va dai do cao khoang 80-300 km. Canh dung so lieu NOAA SWPC OVATION; khong phat song magnetometer truc tiep.


Phien Aurora 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Phien Aurora 3D Explorer khong phu hop la kiem tra du bao NOAA SWPC truc tiep, lap ke hoach di quan sat, hay bo giai precipitacion hat. Dung dich vu thoi tiet khong gian cho viec do. De dia ly va anh sang khong rem, mo Earth 3D Globe. Trang aurora can WebGL va khong phai tro choi thang/thua.
