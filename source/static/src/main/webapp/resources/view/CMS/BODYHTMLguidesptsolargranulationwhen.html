Quando usar Solar Granulation 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want to see the Sun convecting surface, and skip it when you want the deep interior layers or the magnetic loops.

  • Classroom demos of convection and the photosphere
  • Showing why the surface looks grainy and boils
  • Comparing granules with the larger supergranules

This is an educational visualization with illustrative sizes and timing, not a convection simulation.
