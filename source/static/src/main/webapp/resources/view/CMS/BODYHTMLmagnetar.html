About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Scrub field comparison or flare pulse.

Magnetars are neutron stars with the strongest known magnetic fields - about 10^14 to 10^15 Gauss (~a million billion Gauss). NASA Chandra contrasts that with Earth (~1 Gauss) and a fridge magnet (~100 Gauss). Ordinary pulsars sit near 10^8 to 10^12 Gauss. As of the 2020 Swift discovery of J1818.0-1607, about 31 magnetars were known among roughly 3,000 known neutron stars.
