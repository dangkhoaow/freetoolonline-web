Quando usar Earth Magnetosphere 3D Explorer

Use the Earth Magnetosphere 3D Explorer when you need the large-scale field and magnetopause boundary - and skip it when you need aurora curtains or AU-scale heliosphere structure.

  • Classroom demos of day-side compression (~6-10 Re)
  • Linking ~11 deg dipole tilt to magnetic-latitude literacy before aurora lessons
  • One-screen refresher on surface field ~25-65 uT without a textbook figure

This page does not run MHD, does not forecast storms, and exaggerates shell clarity for teaching.
