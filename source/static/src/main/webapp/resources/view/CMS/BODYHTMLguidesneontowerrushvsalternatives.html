Neon Tower Rush vs Medieval Wall Defense, Garden Defense, and Installed TD Apps

This table contrasts Neon Tower Rush with other tower-defense options when you want a browser tab instead of a store download.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Neon Tower Rush is the deepest classic TD on this site (24 towers, six maps, 30 waves). Medieval Wall Defense is incremental prestige; Garden Defense is a short five-wave intro. Installed apps add cloud saves at much larger size.

Versus Medieval Wall Defense

Medieval Wall (~631 KB) is incremental wall defense with Research/Crowns prestige across hundreds of runs. Neon Tower Rush (~1.2 MB) is map-based classic TD with a draft pool and wave-30 boss per run - better for one sitting, worse for idle meta.

Versus Garden Defense

Garden Defense finishes in under fifteen minutes with five fixed waves. Neon Tower Rush targets 20-45 minutes with six maps and EX upgrade trees - more complexity, larger payload.
