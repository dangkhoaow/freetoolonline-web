Head Soccer Arena is a monochrome head-soccer duel in the browser: jump and kick with keyboard controls, play 1P vs CPU or hotseat 2P, pick match length and goals to win, and grab power-ups. About ~88 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Prefer a paddle duel from the same arcade pack? Try Mono Paddle Duel.

HEAD SOCCER ARENA

Pitch duel from the open-source Cursor Arcade soccer module (MIT). About ~88 KB downloads once from this site when you press Play. Best scores stay in your browser.

P1: A/D move, W jump, S kick, Q power shot. P2: arrows move, / power shot. Space pause, R restart, Esc menu.

Press Play to load the game.
Scores use ftol:headsoccerarena:storage.
