How to use Hyperion Sponge Moon 3D Explorer

  1. Open the Hyperion Sponge Moon 3D Explorer and wait for the canvas to paint.
  2. Press Play if paused, then watch the Chaos tumble for a few seconds.
  3. Toggle Chaos Off to compare a steadier spin against the tumble.
  4. Move Speed while you read the porosity and size table in the facts panel.
  5. Use Fullscreen when teaching the sponge-crater idea on a large display.
