When to use Hyperion Sponge Moon 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want to show Hyperion sponge porosity and chaotic tumble together.

  • Comparing a porous irregular moon to round icy moons
  • Introducing Titan resonance as a driver of chaos
  • Teaching why deep craters can survive on a rubble-pile body

This is an educational visualization, not a dynamics simulator.
