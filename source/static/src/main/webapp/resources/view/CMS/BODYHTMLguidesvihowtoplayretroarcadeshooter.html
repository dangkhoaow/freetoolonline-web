Cach choi Retro Arcade Shooter - tung buoc

Trang Retro Arcade Shooter tai Underrun trong iframe (~120 KB). Don co so: WASD di chuyen, chuot ngam, giu click ban, reboot moi CPU qua ba man. Nhan Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Chi ban phim + chuot. Can WebGL. Khong cam ung - dien thoai tai duoc nhung khong choi duoc.

Buoc 1 - nhan Play

Play chen iframe (ma Underrun + ban do PNG). Underrun cua Dominic Szablewski (MIT); nhac Andreas Loesch; LICENSE kem theo.

Buoc 2 - am thanh va man 1

Click canvas de mo am thanh (Sonant-X tong hop trong tab). Chu boot; roi man 1.

Buoc 3 - di chuyen va ban

WASD hoac mui ten. Chuot ngam. Giu chuot trai (plasma co cooldown). Nhen melee; thap ban. Nhat health pack.

Buoc 4 - reboot CPU

Reboot moi CPU de mo loi ra. Ba man. Chet thi man tai lai sau ~3s.

Buoc 5 - khong co gi

Khong save, diem, tai khoan. Khong cam ung hay gamepad. Mot sung. Goc top-down nghieng, khong phai FPS (Retro FPS Online).
