BMI Calculator runs in three steps: open the tool, enter your numbers, then read the result. This page walks through what each step expects.

Quick answer. Open BMI Calculator, pick metric or imperial, enter weight and height, and read the result - it updates as you type.

Step 1 - open the tool

Open BMI Calculator. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.

Step 2 - enter your numbers

Pick metric (kg/cm) or imperial (lb/in), then enter your weight and height.

Step 3 - get the result

The BMI value and category update as you type - there is no button to click. The calculation runs locally in the browser; nothing is uploaded.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account to use it?

No. The tool runs directly in the browser, with no install or login.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. The numbers you enter stay on your device and are never sent to a server or an analytics service.

Is the result medical advice?

No. The result is a general screening estimate only; it does not account for muscle mass, age, or body composition.
