When to Use HR Diagram 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Open the HR Diagram 3D Explorer when you need a browser schematic of main sequence, giants, and white dwarfs with the Sun at Teff ~5,772 K and L = 1 Lsun - without a Hipparcos or Gaia catalog dump.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open HR Diagram 3D Explorer, press Main sequence then Giants then White dwarfs, and finish on Sun so students see the G-type anchor against all three loci - clearer than a static textbook plot alone.


Temperature and luminosity in one panel

Temperature and luminosity literacy stays readable when you scrub Spectral class O through M along the main-sequence band and open Facts table for the teaching ranges (giants ~10 to 1e5+ Lsun; white dwarfs hot but faint). Axes keep hot left / cool right and log(L/Lsun) vertical.


Sessions HR Diagram 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions HR Diagram 3D Explorer does not fit are full stellar catalog queries, isochrone fitting, or binary evolutionary tracks. Use Star Lifecycle 3D for stage walks or Black Body Radiation 3D for temperature-color literacy alone. Walk the controls in the HR Diagram 3D Explorer step-by-step guide.
