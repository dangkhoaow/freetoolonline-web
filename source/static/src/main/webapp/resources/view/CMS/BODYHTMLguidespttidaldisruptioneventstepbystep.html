Como explorar Tidal Disruption Event 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch a star fall in, spaghettify, and flare as a black hole shreds it.

  1. Open /space-3d/tidal-disruption-event.html and wait for the scene.
  2. Read the facts panel: the tidal-radius formula, the half-bound rule, and the t^-5/3 flare.
  3. Watch the star cross the blue tidal-radius ring and stretch into a debris stream.
  4. See the accretion disk brighten as debris falls back, then fade.
  5. Compare with Black Hole 3D for the black hole itself, or Roche Limit 3D for a moon torn by a planet.
