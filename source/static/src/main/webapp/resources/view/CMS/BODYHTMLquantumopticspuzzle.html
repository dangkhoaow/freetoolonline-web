Quantum Optics Puzzle is a free browser photon puzzle. Place mirrors, polarizers, splitters, and detectors so photons reach the goals. Levels use real quantum optics ideas such as superposition and polarization. About ~1.2 MB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a different quantum-flavored puzzle? Try Quantum Shift. Prefer a space grid logic loop? Try Space Grid Puzzle.

QUANTUM OPTICS PUZZLE

Pick a level, drag optics tiles onto the board, and run the photon simulation. Progress uses ftol:quantumoptics:* keys. Adapted from stared/quantum-game (MIT). About ~1.2 MB.

Works best with a mouse or trackpad. Sound effects are optional.
