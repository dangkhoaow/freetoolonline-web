Frequently Asked Questions

How do I flip layers?

Press Space (or gamepad button 1) to toggle the active layer. Platforms and spikes belonging to the other layer appear or disappear so you can open a path mid-jump.

What are the controls?

Move with A/D or Left/Right. Jump with W or Up. Flip layers with Space. A gamepad is also supported. There is no touch control scheme in this build.

How many levels are there?

There are 25 hand-crafted stages plus a title and controls screen. Progress is tracked by stars collected on the stage counter in-session.

Does it save my progress?

No. Layer Flip Platformer does not write localStorage or cookies. Closing the tab resets progress.

How big is the download?

About ~44 KB total (HTML + JS + CSS) after the Play click. No install and no account.
