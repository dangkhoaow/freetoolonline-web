vs Solar System 3D Explorer

Solar System 3D Explorer prioritizes readable orbits - sphere sizes and distances are compressed. Planet Size Comparison 3D Explorer keeps radii scale-accurate in one row: Jupiter 69,911 km beside Earth 6,371 km with no orbital motion.


vs textbook diagrams

A textbook page shows static circles once. Planet Size Comparison 3D Explorer adds drag-to-orbit viewing, highlight buttons, and a spin toggle while listing nine NASA mean radii in the panel - still free and browser-only.


vs planetarium apps

Planetarium apps target tonight's sky positions and often need install. Planet Size Comparison 3D Explorer is a focused scale lesson with published radii (Sun 696,340 km, Mercury 2,440 km) and no ephemeris - load the URL and click a world.
