Neuro Aim Arena is a free browser neuroevolution aim duel. You fight robots controlled by neural networks that evolve each generation. Move with WASD or arrow keys, aim with the mouse, and click to shoot. Borders hurt you - push bots into them when you can. About ~160 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a calmer neural toy? Try Rock Paper Neural. Want artillery aiming instead? Try Bang Bang Artillery.

NEURO AIM ARENA

Click the canvas to start. WASD or arrows move, mouse aims, hold click to shoot. Kill the neural bots before they evolve into tougher generations. Borders damage you. Adapted from Victor Ribeiro's Aim and Shoot (MIT). About ~160 KB.

Desktop mouse recommended. Touch gets on-screen GuiControls sticks.

Press Play to load the game.
Session only - no localStorage; refresh restarts generation 1.
