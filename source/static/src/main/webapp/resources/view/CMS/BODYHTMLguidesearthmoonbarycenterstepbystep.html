How to explore Earth-Moon Barycenter 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch Earth swing around the point it shares with the Moon.

  1. Open /space-3d/earth-moon-barycenter.html and wait for Earth, the Moon and the yellow dot.
  2. Note the yellow barycenter dot sitting inside the see-through Earth.
  3. Watch Earth centre trace a small circle while the Moon loops the far side.
  4. Follow the lever line joining Earth, the dot, and the Moon.
  5. Use the speed slider and read the panel for the 4,671 km and 81 to 1 figures.
