What a person would see is a frilled ceratopsian whose panel length is 6 m - shorter than a 1.8 m person end-to-end - with skull length about 2.3 m in the panel silhouette. Turn on Size vs human and orbit beside a hind leg to feel how a Late Cretaceous herbivore with a huge frill compares to you.


What the Pentaceratops size numbers are not

What these size numbers are not is a blended composite: the panel headline figures (6 m / 2.3 m skull hip / 2.5 t) are never merged with the German juvenile range (about 0.7-0.9 m / 0.3-0.6 kg) or treated as if every specimen matched the French adult. Soft-tissue color in the viewer is still an artistic reconstruction. This model is not a fossil-accurate skeleton. For the control walkthrough see how to view Pentaceratops in 3D.
