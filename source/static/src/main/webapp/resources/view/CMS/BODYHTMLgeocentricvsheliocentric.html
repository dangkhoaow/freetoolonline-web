About this scene

The facts panel fills when the scene loads. Toggle the model or scrub the day slider.

Both views use the same real orbits: Earth at 1 AU with a 365.25-day year, Mars at 1.52 AU with a 686.98-day year (NASA Planetary Fact Sheet). Only the point you are viewing them from changes.

Drag to orbit and scroll or pinch to zoom. Switch models, press Play or Pause, or drag the day slider to any point across two Mars synodic cycles.
