How to Play Pixel Pipeline Reflex - Step by Step

The Pixel Pipeline Reflex loads a ~48 KB ES-module reflex game in your tab. Match Fetch, FIFO, Mix, and Light pipeline stages with keys 1-4, taps, or clicks before the scan beam times out.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Press 1-4 (or tap/click the matching stage) for Fetch/FIFO/Mix/Light, stabilize enough frames without too many glitches, chain correct hits for combo score, and use daily or custom seeds.

Step 1 - start a frame

Press Play on the page, then click Start frame inside the game or press Enter/Space. Pick Chill, Arcade (default), or Meltdown before you begin - each changes frame target, glitch cap, beam speed, and total time.

Step 2 - read the next stage and beat the beam

Watch the Next stage label and the pixel queue. A scan beam sweeps across the canvas - press the key that matches the lit stage (1 Fetch, 2 FIFO, 3 Mix, 4 Light), tap its button, or click it before the beam expires.

Step 3 - stabilize frames without glitches

Win by stabilizing the target frame count: 10 on Chill, 12 on Arcade, 14 on Meltdown. Wrong inputs and beam timeouts count as glitches; exceed the cap (6/5/4 by difficulty) and the run ends.

Step 4 - chain combos and pick a seed

Correct matches in a row raise a combo multiplier on your score. The daily seed uses the Shanghai date key; load a custom seed via ?seed= in the URL or the in-page seed field. Copy link to share the same challenge.

Step 5 - sound, pause, and local bests

Sound is optional and off by default - toggle with the Sound button (stored under ftol:pixelpipelinereflex:sound-enabled). Press P to pause. Best score per seed and difficulty saves locally as ftol:pixelpipelinereflex:{seed}:{difficulty}.
