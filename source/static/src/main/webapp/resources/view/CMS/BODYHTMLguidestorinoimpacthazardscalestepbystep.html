How to Explore the Torino Impact Hazard Scale 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-24

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Torino Impact Hazard Scale 3D Explorer, pick one of three real historical near-Earth-object cases, and scrub or play the approach path while the asteroid's beacon changes color to match that case's real assigned Torino band.

  1. Open /space-3d/torino-impact-hazard-scale.html and wait for Earth, the approaching asteroid, and the 0-10 band grid to render.
  2. Use the Historical case selector to pick Apophis Dec 2004 rating (Torino 4), Apophis refined Aug 2006 rating (Torino 0), or 2023 DW 2023 rating (Torino 1).
  3. Drag the Approach slider (0-100%) or click "Play Approach" to watch the asteroid move toward Earth.
  4. Watch the beacon color change to that case's real band - white, green, yellow, orange, or red.
  5. Look at the 0-10 band grid below the scene - the outlined cell is the picked case's real assigned rating.
  6. Switch cases and compare how the beacon color and outlined cell change for each.
