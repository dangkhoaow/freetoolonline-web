Frequently Asked Questions

What does 2048 Game - Merge the Numbers Puzzle do?

Slide the numbered tiles with the arrow keys, WASD, or a swipe - equal tiles merge into their sum, and every move drops a new 2 (or occasionally a 4) onto the board.

When should I reach for 2048 game - merge the numbers puzzle?

Tiles glide with a short animation and pop when they merge, and each tile can only merge once per move - the same one-merge rule the classic puzzle uses.

What complementary tools work well alongside 2048 game - merge the numbers puzzle?

The board ends only when no move would change anything; reaching the 2048 tile is celebrated but you can keep merging for a higher score.
