Khi ung dung AR hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi can bong tren san phong khach; viewer nay khong nhan dat trong phong. Bao tang thang voi mau Fossil Marine Reptiles Gallery (Natural History Museum, London). Anh tinh chi thang khi can mot khung chia se khong tuong tac.
