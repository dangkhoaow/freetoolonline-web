How to Play Black Hole Square - Step by Step

The Black Hole Square page loads a ~40 KB tap puzzle in an iframe. Clear every square into the black hole. Press Play to start.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Tap marked squares to absorb or shove pieces. Swipe left to skip, swipe right to reset or go back. Saves: ftol:blackholesquare:storage.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~40 KB). Swipe left on the title screen to enter the welcome text, then swipe left again for the first puzzle. Upstream is Quinten/black-hole-square (MIT, js13k 2021).

Step 2 - read the board

The black hole sits on the 6x6 grid. Other tiles include blank squares, X squares, arrow squares, and neutron stars. Each puzzle lists a move budget in the top-right.

Step 3 - tap to clear

Tap a marked square. Arrows shove pieces along a line into the hole or empty space. X removes itself. Neutron stars convert into a black hole and pull. Stay within the move count.

Step 4 - swipe between puzzles

Swipe left to skip ahead. Swipe right to reset the current board or go back when you have not made moves. Progress dots under the board open solved levels.

Step 5 - resume later

Current level, solved list, and solutions save under ftol:blackholesquare:storage. Clear site data for that origin if you want a fresh start.
