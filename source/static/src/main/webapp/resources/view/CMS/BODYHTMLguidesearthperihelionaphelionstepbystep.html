How to Explore Earth Perihelion & Aphelion 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-22

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Earth Perihelion & Aphelion 3D Explorer, scrub the day-of-year slider or press Play year, and read the real Sun-Earth distance as it rises and falls across the year.

  1. Open /space-3d/earth-perihelion-aphelion.html and wait for the Sun and Earth to render.
  2. Press "Jump to perihelion" to snap to early January, when Earth is about 147.1 million km from the Sun - its closest point.
  3. Press "Jump to aphelion" to snap to early July, when Earth is about 152.1 million km away - its farthest point.
  4. Scrub the day-of-year slider slowly and watch the distance curve below the scene trace the swing between the two.
  5. Press "Play year" to run one full year automatically.
  6. Read the facts panel for the live distance, the published figures, and why this swing does not cause the seasons.
