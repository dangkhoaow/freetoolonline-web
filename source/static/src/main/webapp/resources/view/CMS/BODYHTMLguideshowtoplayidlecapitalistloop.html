How to Play Idle Capitalist Loop - Step by Step

The Idle Capitalist Loop page loads a ~87 KB idle tycoon in an iframe. Buy businesses, hire managers, prestige with angels, and expand to Moon and Mars. Press Play to start.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Click businesses to buy and collect. Hire managers for auto-run. Space pauses. Saves: ftol:idlecapitalistloop:storage.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~87 KB). The engine auto-opens the idle capitalist game. Upstream is the Cursor Arcade capitalist module by flancast90 (MIT).

Step 2 - buy and collect

On Earth, start with a lemonade stand. Click Buy on a business row to purchase units. When the progress bar fills, click Collect (or hire a manager so cycles auto-collect).

Step 3 - managers and upgrades

Open the Managers tab to hire automation for each business. Cash Upgrades multiply revenue. Angel Upgrades cost angel investors and stack permanent boosts.

Step 4 - Moon and Mars

Unlock Moon and Mars from planet tabs when you can afford the unlock cost. Each planet has its own businesses, managers, and upgrade trees. Keys 1/2/3 switch planets on desktop.

Step 5 - angel prestige

When Reset shows available angels, prestige to wipe planet progress for angel investors. Angels boost all profits (2% each base, plus upgrade bonuses). Offline earnings apply up to 12 hours at 25% efficiency.
