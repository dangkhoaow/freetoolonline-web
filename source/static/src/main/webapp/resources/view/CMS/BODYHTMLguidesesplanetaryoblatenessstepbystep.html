Como explorar Planetary Oblateness 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch a planet bulge at the equator as it spins.

  1. Open /space-3d/planetary-oblateness.html and wait for the planet.
  2. Notice the bright equator ring bulging past the faint perfect-sphere outline.
  3. Slide the flattening down toward 0 to make a round ball, or up to squash it like Saturn.
  4. Match the markers: Earth 0.0034, Jupiter 0.065, Saturn 0.098.
  5. Press Play to spin the planet, and read the facts panel for the spin periods.
