Frequently Asked Questions

How big is Serenity Chasma?

More than 200 kilometers long, typically about 40 to 50 kilometers wide, and roughly 5 to 7 kilometers deep - part of a ~1,800 kilometer chasm belt.

Is Serenity Chasma an official name?

It is widely used but often listed as informal, named after the ship Serenity from Firefly. The broader tectonic belt is the main mapped system.

Why did Charon crack?

NASA's leading idea is that an ancient subsurface ocean froze and expanded, stretching the crust into pull-apart faults. That remains a scientific interpretation.

How does it compare with the Grand Canyon?

NASA notes the Charon chasm belt is far longer (~1,800 km vs ~446 km) and deeper (up to ~7.5 km vs ~1.6 km).

When was it imaged?

New Horizons imaged the encounter hemisphere during the July 14, 2015 flyby.

Is the ocean still liquid?

The hypothesis is that the ocean has long since frozen. The scene ocean hint is schematic history, not a present-day liquid layer claim.

Is this a physics simulation?

No. It is an educational visualization with compressed canyon relief, not a tectonic or freeze solver.
