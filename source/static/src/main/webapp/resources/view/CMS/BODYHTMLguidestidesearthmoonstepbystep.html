How to explore Tides: Earth-Moon 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Tides: Earth-Moon 3D Explorer, watch the red marker sweep under the two ocean bulges, and cycle Spring, Normal, and Neap tide modes.

  1. Open /space-3d/tides-earth-moon.html and wait for Earth, the Moon, and the pale-blue bulge shell to paint.
  2. Watch the red marker on Earth's surface - the info panel reports High tide, Low tide, Rising, or Falling as Earth spins.
  3. Press the tide-mode button to cycle Spring tide (about 1.5x), Normal (Moon only), and Neap tide (about 0.5x) and see the bulge grow and shrink.
  4. Click Earth, the Moon, or the small Sun marker for a facts panel on each body's role in the tide.
  5. Press Range table for real numbers from the global average to the Bay of Fundy.
