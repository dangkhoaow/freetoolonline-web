Como jugar Pixel Realm RPG

Pixel Realm RPG loads about ~3.8 MB in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Click Play, press New Game inside the frame, then explore with WASD or arrows, attack with X, interact with F, and open inventory with I.

Launch

Click Play on this page, then New Game on the in-game title screen.

Exploration

Move across 8x8 tile maps. Interact with doors, chests, and fountains using F or click.

Combat

Face an enemy left or right and press X to attack. Level up and equip gear from the G equipment panel.

Saves

Progress auto-saves to localStorage. Use the settings gear to download or import a JSON save.
