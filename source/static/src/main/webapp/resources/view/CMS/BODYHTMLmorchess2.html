Mor Chess 2 is a free browser chess puzzle: instead of dragging pieces move by move, you pick from a short menu of named positional ideas (fianchetto, rook lift, knight outpost, pawn chain, king safety, and more) each turn, and the game plays that idea out on the board against an opponent called Black Cat. A stronger sequence of choices raises your position's evaluation and your chances of forcing checkmate. About ~13 KB, no install, no account.

Want a different kind of puzzle instead? Try Mystic Card Paw.
