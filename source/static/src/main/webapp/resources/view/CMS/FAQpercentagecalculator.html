Frequently Asked Questions

What does Percentage Calculator do?

Work out a percentage of a number, the reverse (what percent one number is of another), or a percent change.

When should I reach for percentage calculator?

Use it for a discount, a tax add-on, a grade, or comparing two values as a percent change.

What complementary tools work well alongside percentage calculator?

Switch calculations at any time; the two number fields carry over so you can compare results.
