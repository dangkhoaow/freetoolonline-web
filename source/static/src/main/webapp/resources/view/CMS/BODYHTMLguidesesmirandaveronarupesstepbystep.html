How to explore Miranda Verona Rupes 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and rotate Miranda so Verona Rupes faces you.

  1. Open /space-3d/miranda-verona-rupes.html and wait for Miranda to appear.
  2. Press Play and rotate until the blue cliff face is edge-on to the camera.
  3. Toggle Fall to watch the slow free-fall marker descend the scarp.
  4. Drag to orbit around the far side, then return to the cliff.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow or speed the rotation and fall.
