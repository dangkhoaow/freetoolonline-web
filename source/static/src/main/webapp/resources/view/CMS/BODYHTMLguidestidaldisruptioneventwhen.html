When to use Tidal Disruption Event 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Use this explorer when you want to see how a supermassive black hole shreds a star - and skip it when you want the black hole itself or a moon torn apart by a planet.

  • Classroom demos of spaghettification and the tidal radius
  • Connecting the flare decline to the t^-5/3 fallback rate
  • Explaining why very massive black holes swallow stars whole

This is an educational visualization with illustrative sizes and a compressed timeline.
