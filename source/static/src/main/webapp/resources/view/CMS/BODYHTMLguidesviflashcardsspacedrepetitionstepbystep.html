The Ghi Nho (Lap Lai Ngan Quang) Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

The Ghi Nho (Lap Lai Ngan Quang) chay theo ba buoc: mo cong cu, them the, roi hoc chung. Trang nay noi chi tiet dieu moi buoc can.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Vao The Ghi Nho (Lap Lai Ngan Quang). Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - them the cua ban

Go cau hoi vao "Front (question)", go cau tra loi vao "Back (answer)", roi bam "Add card" (hoac nhan Enter o mot trong hai o). Lap lai cho moi the ban muon co trong bo.


Buoc 3 - hoc bo the

Khu vuc hoc hien mat truoc cua mot the tai mot thoi diem. Bam "Show answer" de lat the, roi danh dau la "I knew it" de tam gac lai trong lan nay, hoac "Still learning" de giu no trong hang doi; Shuffle xao tron thu tu hoc.

The duoc luu trong trinh duyet nay qua bo nho cuc bo, nen chung ton tai qua lan tai lai trang; xuat ban sao luu (JSON) truoc khi xoa du lieu trinh duyet hoac doi thiet bi, vi ca hai deu khong tu dong dong bo.

Day la mot cong cu ho tro on tap don gian, khong phai bo lap lich on tap ngan quang - khong co thuat toan nao tinh thoi diem mot the xuat hien lai; "Study all again" chi xoa co "da biet" cua moi the, nen toan bo bo the quay lai hang doi.
