What a person would see beside a Pachyrhinosaurus

What the Pachyrhinosaurus size numbers are not

What these size numbers are not is a claim of one precise length or weight: the Pachyrhinosaurus size comparison discloses the about 6-8 m / 3-4 t figures alongside Britannica's smaller about 6 m / 1.8 t note and uses 8 m only for the Size vs human scale; it does not claim long horns, does not invent uniform boss shape for every individual or species, and this is not a win/lose game.
