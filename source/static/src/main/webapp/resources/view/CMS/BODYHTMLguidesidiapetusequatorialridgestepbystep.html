How to explore Iapetus Equatorial Ridge 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and rotate Iapetus so the equatorial ridge is edge-on.

  1. Open /space-3d/iapetus-equatorial-ridge.html and wait for Iapetus to appear.
  2. Press Play and pause when the orange ridge ring is edge-on.
  3. Toggle Albedo off and on to compare uniform ice versus dark/bright hemispheres.
  4. Drag to face the poles, then return to the equator.
  5. Use the speed slider to slow or speed the rotation.
