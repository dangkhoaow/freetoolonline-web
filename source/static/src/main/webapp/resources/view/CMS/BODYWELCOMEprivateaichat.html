Private AI Chat

Last reviewed: 2026-07-01
Free Tool Online Editorial Team

Chat with an AI model that runs entirely on your device, in your browser. It suits quick drafting, brainstorming, or asking a question you would rather keep off a server.


How it works

Pick a model and click Load model. The first time, the model downloads to your browser (hundreds of MB) and is cached for next time; after that it runs entirely on your device. Your messages are never uploaded, there is no account, and nothing is sent to a server. A WebGPU browser is required - the latest Chrome or Edge on desktop work well.


Choosing a model

Smaller models like TinyLlama 1.1B load fastest; larger ones like Llama 3.2 3B or Phi-3 mini give stronger answers but take a bigger first download. Switch models at any time.

Key features

  • Streaming replies: answers stream in token by token, generated on your own hardware.
  • In-session history: the chat history stays in this tab for the session and is not saved or synced anywhere.
