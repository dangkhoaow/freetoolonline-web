When the Kentrosaurus browser viewer fits

Pick the browser viewer when you want drag-orbit with Size vs human at the honest 4.25 m panel length ratio and click facts on plates and tail spikes - without installing anything.


When a phone AR app or museum fits better

A phone AR app wins when you need the silhouette on your floor; this viewer never claims room placement. A museum wins for real mounts.


What this Kentrosaurus comparison is not

This comparison is a trade-off table for the shipped viewer - not an AR brand ranking, no invented AR features, no single mass outside the published 700-1,600 kg range, and no game score. For controls see how to view Kentrosaurus in 3D. For size see Kentrosaurus size comparison.
