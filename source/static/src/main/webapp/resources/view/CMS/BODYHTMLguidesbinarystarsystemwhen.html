When to use Binary Star System 3D Explorer

Use the Binary Star System 3D Explorer when you need a quick, browser-only view of two real pairs orbiting a barycenter - and skip it when you need telescope ephemerides or a full triple-star model.

  • Classroom demos of the barycenter and mass-ratio offset with published Sirius or Alpha Centauri figures
  • Quick refresher before splitting Sirius or reading Alpha Centauri AB ephemerides in a planetarium app
  • Comparing a compact visual binary (Sirius) against a wider eccentric Sun-like pair (Alpha Centauri)

This page does not replace professional ephemerides, does not include Proxima Centauri, and does not solve n-body gravity. Angular separations on the sky are not claimed.
