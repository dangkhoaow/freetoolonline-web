Wheel Spinner Online

Last reviewed: 2026-07-02

This wheel spinner turns a list of names or options into a fair random pick, right in your browser. There is nothing to download, install, or sign up for - type your entries and the wheel is ready to spin.

Type one name or option per line into the list box, then click Spin. The wheel splits into equal slices, one per entry, and spins to a stop; the slice under the pointer is the winner. Edit the list at any time and spin again for a fresh round - there is no limit on how many times you can spin.

The spin result is chosen using your browser's crypto.getRandomValues, a fairness source built into every modern browser, so every entry has an equal chance regardless of list order or entry length. Everything runs locally on your device - no upload and no account - and the list is not saved once you leave the page, so refreshing clears it.

Use it for raffles and giveaways, deciding who goes first in a game or meeting, assigning a random task to a team member, or picking a random winner from a shortlist without any bias toward the order names were typed in.

Need to time the activity the spin just decided? The countdown timer and stopwatch are useful companions, and the to-do list tool is handy for tracking the tasks you just assigned.
