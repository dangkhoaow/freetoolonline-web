Cach choi Pixel Pipeline Reflex - Huong dan tung buoc

Pixel Pipeline Reflex tai game phan xa ~48 KB bang ES module trong tab. Khop cac giai doan Fetch, FIFO, Mix, Light bang phim 1-4, cham hoac click truoc khi tia quet het thoi gian.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-10

Bam 1-4 (hoac cham/click dung giai doan) cho Fetch/FIFO/Mix/Light, on dinh du frame ma khong vuot glitch, lien tiep dung de nhan combo, dung seed hang ngay hoac tuy chinh.

Buoc 1 - bat dau frame

Bam Play tren trang, roi Start frame trong game hoac Enter/Space. Chon Chill, Arcade (mac dinh) hoac Meltdown - moi muc thay doi muc tieu frame, gioi han glitch, toc do tia va tong thoi gian.

Buoc 2 - doc giai doan tiep theo va danh bai tia quet

Xem nhan Next stage va hang doi pixel. Tia quet chay tren canvas - bam phim giai doan dang sang (1 Fetch, 2 FIFO, 3 Mix, 4 Light), cham nut hoac click truoc khi het gio.

Buoc 3 - on dinh frame khong bi glitch

Thang bang cach on dinh muc tieu: 10 o Chill, 12 o Arcade, 14 o Meltdown. Nhap sai va het gio tinh la glitch; vuot gioi han (6/5/4) thi ket thuc.

Buoc 4 - combo va seed

Dung lien tiep tang he so combo. Seed hang ngay dung ngay Shanghai; seed tuy chinh qua ?seed= tren URL hoac o nhap tren trang. Copy link de chia se thu thach.

Buoc 5 - am thanh, tam dung va diem cao local

Am thanh tuy chon va mac tat - bat/tat bang nut Sound (luu ftol:pixelpipelinereflex:sound-enabled). P de tam dung. Diem cao theo seed va do kho luu ftol:pixelpipelinereflex:{seed}:{difficulty}.
