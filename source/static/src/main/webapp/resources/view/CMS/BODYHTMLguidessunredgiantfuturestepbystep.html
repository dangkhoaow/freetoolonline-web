How to explore Sun Red Giant Future 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-24

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Sun Red Giant Future 3D Explorer, drag the epoch slider, and read how the Sun's radius, color, and Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars visibility change across 8 real published milestones.

  1. Open /space-3d/sun-red-giant-future.html and wait for the Sun and the four planet rings to appear.
  2. Drag the epoch slider from 0 (Today) toward 7 (white dwarf) one stop at a time.
  3. Read the facts panel: the epoch label, the years-from-now figure, and the fact sentence for that stop.
  4. Watch Mercury and Venus disappear from the scene once the rapid-expansion epoch engulfs them.
  5. Check the per-planet table for Earth's status - shown as disputed, not answered as fact, at the red-giant-branch tip and after.
  6. Drag inside the canvas to orbit, and scroll or pinch to zoom.
