How to Play Elm Street Delivery - Step by Step

The Elm Street Delivery page loads a ~3 MB WebGL courier map in an iframe from the FreeToolOnline game CDN.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, click to start, then click houses and the warehouse to pick up and deliver boxes before customers lose patience.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the CDN iframe (~3 MB). The Elm WebGL map boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - start the shift

Click the in-game start prompt. You play as Joe the courier on a bicycle with limited box space.

Step 3 - plan pickups and deliveries

Click the warehouse and houses. Pick up fashion boxes, watch inventory capacity, and deliver before customer patience runs out.

Step 4 - keep customers happy

Unhappy customers end the run. Short routes and careful order beats racing every pin at once.
