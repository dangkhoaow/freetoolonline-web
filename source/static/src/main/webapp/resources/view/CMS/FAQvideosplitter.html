Frequently Asked Questions

What does Video Splitter do?

Split a video into smaller pieces.

When should I reach for video splitter?

Reach for it when you have one clip and need to cut it into two pieces at a single point - lifting an intro off the front of a recording, or breaking a long take in half. Each resulting clip downloads as its own separate file; the two pieces are never bundled into a zip or archive.

What complementary tools work well alongside video splitter?

If you only need to keep one exact time range instead of two separate pieces, use Video Trimmer instead.
