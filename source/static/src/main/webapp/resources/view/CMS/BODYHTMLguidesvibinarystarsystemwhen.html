Khi Nao Dung Binary Star System 3D Explorer - Phien Tot Nhat va Gioi Han

Last reviewed: 2026-07-16

Mo Binary Star System 3D Explorer khi ban can xem hai cap da cong bo quay quanh barycenter chi trong trinh duyet - Sirius A/B (50.13 nam; Bond et al. 2017) hoac Alpha Centauri A/B (79.76 nam) - khong can lich thien van kinh thien van hay mo hinh ba sao.


Demo lop hoc nam phut

Demo lop hoc nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Binary Star System 3D Explorer, chon Sirius A/B, bam Play orbit, va nhap barycenter vang de hoc sinh thay sao nang hon nam gan hon tam khoi luong chung. Chuyen sang Alpha Centauri A/B va doc bien do khoang cach 11.2 AU den 35.6 AU tren bang.


Ti le khoi luong va do lech tam

Ti le khoi luong va do lech tam van doc duoc khi bang su kien mo: khoi luong Sirius 2.063 va 1.018 khoi luong Mat Troi, do lech tam khoang 0.59 va khoang 0.52, va mot chu ky quy dao doc duoc khoang 28 giay o 1x. Duong di la chuyen dong di thuong lech tam Kepler, khong phai tich hop luc n vat the.


Phien Binary Star System 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Phien Binary Star System 3D Explorer khong phu hop la lich thien van chuyen nghiep, Proxima Centauri nhu vat the thu ba, hoac khoang cach goc va epoch dung kinh thien van. Di qua dieu khien trong huong dan tung buoc Binary Star System 3D Explorer, hoac so sanh lua chon trong huong dan vs thay the Binary Star System 3D Explorer. Chi cho dinh luat elip, mo Kepler Orbits 3D Explorer.
