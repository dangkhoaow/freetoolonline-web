Cau tra loi trong 30 giay. Mo Sort Text Lines, dan danh sach cua ban voi moi dong mot muc vao o tren, roi bam Sort A-Z de sap xep tang dan hoac Sort Z-A de sap xep giam dan. Danh sach da sap xep xuat hien trong o ket qua ben duoi, san sang de sao chep.

Cach sap xep cac dong cua ban

  1. Mo cong cu Sort Text Lines.
  2. Go hoac dan van ban vao o nhap, giu moi dong mot muc - cong cu coi moi lan xuong dong la ranh gioi giua hai muc.
  3. Bam Sort A-Z de sap xep cac dong tang dan, hoac Sort Z-A de sap xep giam dan.
  4. Doc danh sach da sap xep trong o ket qua ben duoi va sao chep no ra. Bam nut con lai bat cu luc nao de dao nguoc thu tu.

Cach thu tu duoc quyet dinh

Viec sap xep dung phep so sanh chuoi theo ngon ngu cua trinh duyet, nen cac dong duoc sap xep theo tung chu cai. Co hai dieu nen biet truoc khi tin vao ket qua:

  • Chu hoa va chu thuong nam gan nhau. Phien ban chu hoa va chu thuong cua cung mot tu duoc sap xep canh nhau, nen "apple" va "Apple" nam ke nhau chu khong tach thanh hai khoi rieng.
  • So duoc so sanh nhu van ban, khong phai nhu gia tri. Vi moi dong duoc so sanh theo tung ky tu, "10" va "100" dung truoc "2" (cong cu doc ky tu "1" dau tien truoc "2"). Voi danh sach so don gian, hay dem cho chung cung do rong - "02", "10", "100" - de co thu tu so nhu ban mong doi.

Cach dung pho bien

Sap xep theo bang chu cai mot danh sach ten hoac dia chi email; sap xep mot bo the, tu khoa hoac lop CSS; sap xep cac dong cua mot tap cau hinh hoac danh sach tu truoc khi chay diff de hai ben khop nhau; hoac don gian la lam gon mot danh sach da dan de de nhin thay muc trung va cho thieu.

Sort Text Lines so voi bang tinh hoac trinh soan thao tren may

Mot bang tinh co the sap xep mot cot, nhung truoc tien ban phai dan danh sach vao mot vung o, chon no va mo hop sap xep; mot trinh soan thao ma nguon tren may co the sap xep cac dong da chon nhung thuong can mot tien ich bo sung hoac mot lenh. Sort Text Lines la mot o duy nhat voi hai nut - khong tai len, khong buoc nhap va khong cai dat - nen no nhanh hon khi danh sach chua nam san trong mot tai lieu. Voi cong viec nang co nhieu cot hoac sap xep theo so, bang tinh van la lua chon tot hon.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Lam sao cong cu biet mot muc ket thuc o dau va muc tiep theo bat dau o dau?

Moi dong la mot muc. Cong cu tach van ban cua ban tai cac lan xuong dong, sap xep danh sach thu duoc va noi lai bang cac lan xuong dong, nen hay dat dung mot muc tren moi dong.

Tai sao "10" lai dung truoc "2" trong danh sach da sap xep cua toi?

Cac dong duoc so sanh nhu van ban, theo tung ky tu, khong phai nhu so, nen "10" dung truoc "2" vi "1" dung truoc "2". De sap xep so don gian theo thu tu so, hay dem cho chung cung so chu so truoc (02, 10, 100).

Toi co the hoan tac mot lan sap xep hoac lay lai thu tu goc khong?

O ket qua giu ket qua da sap xep; van ban goc cua ban van nam trong o nhap cho den khi ban thay doi no. Sap xep khong ghi de len dau vao, nen ban co the bam Sort A-Z va Sort Z-A bao nhieu lan tuy thich de so sanh hai thu tu.

Van ban cua toi co duoc tai len hoac luu o dau khong?

Khong. Viec sap xep chay hoan toan trong trinh duyet cua ban; khong co gi ban go hoac dan duoc gui den may chu, va khong co gi duoc luu sau khi ban tai lai hoac roi trang.
