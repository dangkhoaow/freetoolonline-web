So nutzen Sie Sternspuren 3D Explorer

Open the Sternspuren 3D Explorer, watch the trails build up, then switch latitude presets to see which stars stay circumpolar.

  1. Open /space-3d/star-trails.html and wait for the pole marker and stars.
  2. Read the facts panel: sidereal day, rotation rate, circumpolar count.
  3. Press Equator, Mid-latitude, and Near pole to compare circumpolar circles.
  4. Reset to clear trails and watch them build up again from empty.
  5. Compare with Constellation Sphere 3D for static asterisms, or Sidereal vs Solar Day 3D for day-length comparison.
