How to explore Jupiter Trojan Asteroids 3D Explorer

Open the explorer and watch two asteroid swarms ride 60 degrees ahead of and behind Jupiter.

  1. Open /space-3d/trojan-asteroids.html and wait for the Sun, Jupiter, and swarms.
  2. See the blue swarm leading (L4, Greek camp) and the orange swarm trailing (L5, Trojan camp).
  3. Toggle the L4 and L5 markers to find the exact 60-degree points.
  4. Press Co-rotate to freeze Jupiter and watch the asteroids librate in tadpole loops.
  5. Read the facts panel: counts, the 5.2 AU orbit, and the stability limit.
