How to Explore DART Asteroid Deflection 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-24

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open DART Asteroid Deflection 3D Explorer, click "Fire DART" to replay the real 26 September 2022 impact, and read the before/after orbital period change in the facts panel.

  1. Open /space-3d/dart-asteroid-deflection.html and wait for Didymos and orbiting Dimorphos to render.
  2. Click "Fire DART" and watch the probe fly in and hit Dimorphos, followed by a fading ejecta burst.
  3. Read the facts panel: the state flips to "After impact" and the orbital period changes from 11h 55m to about 11h 22m.
  4. Compare the -33 minute change against NASA's 73-second minimum-success threshold, shown in the same panel.
  5. Click "Reset (before impact)" to return to the pre-impact state and replay the sequence, or drag to orbit and scroll/pinch to zoom for a closer look.
