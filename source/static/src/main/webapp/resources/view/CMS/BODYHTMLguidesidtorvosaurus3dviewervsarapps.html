When browser viewer fits

Pick browser viewer for drag-orbit with Size vs human at honest 10 m ratio and click facts - no install.


When AR or museum fits better

Phone AR wins for floor placement; this viewer never claims room AR. Museum wins for real fossil mounts.


What this comparison is not

Trade-off table only - not AR brand ranking, no invented AR, no mass outside published range, does not conflate Torvosaurus with Allosaurus or Megalosaurus. See how-to and size guides.
