Browser palette extract vs desktop and upload-based tools

Desktop apps and upload sites can cluster colors with heavier algorithms. This page extracts by sampling pixels in the canvas so the photo never leaves your device.

It does not recolor the image or export a styled file, and it does not replace a manual Color Picker or a WCAG contrast check.

Pick this extractor when you want shareable HEX swatches from a local photo without an account or upload.
