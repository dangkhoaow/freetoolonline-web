Demo lop hoc nam phut

Demo lop hoc nam phut bat dau khi ban mo Lagrange Points 3D Explorer, bam L2 roi JWST de hoc sinh thay tram phia sau Trai Dat, roi L4 va L5 cho tam giac deu 60 do voi Mat Troi va Trai Dat.


Khoang cach tram trong mot bang

Hieu biet khoang cach tram van doc duoc khi ban giu bang su kien hien thi va doc L1/L2 khoang 1.5 trieu km (~0.01 AU) tu Trai Dat trong khi Play orbit giu hinh hoc tuong doi theo nhip dong quay. SOHO gan L1 va JWST gan L2 la moc day, khong phai theo doi truc tiep.


Phien Lagrange Points 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Phien Lagrange Points 3D Explorer khong phu hop la tour hanh tinh day du, quy dao LEO ISS, hay demo nguoc chieu Sao Hoa. Dung Solar System 3D Explorer, ISS Orbit Tracker, hoac Retrograde Motion 3D Explorer. Trang can WebGL va la phac thao CR3BP, khong phai bo tich hop n vat the.
