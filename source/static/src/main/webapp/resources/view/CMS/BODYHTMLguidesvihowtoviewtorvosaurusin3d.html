Torvosaurus 3D Viewer shows a stocky Late Jurassic megalosaurid - panel length 10 m, panel mass 3 t. Drag, zoom, toggle a 1.8 m person, click a body region.


Open page and control camera

Open the viewer and wait for the facts panel. Orbit with pointer, zoom with wheel or pinch. Fullscreen keeps controls visible. Optional glTF may swap from CDN; procedural silhouette remains on failure.


Four controls under the canvas

Four controls under the canvas:

  • Fullscreen - fills display keeping buttons visible
  • Stop rotation / Auto-rotate - freezes or resumes auto-spin
  • Idle motion - adds subtle sway; tap again for Stand still
  • Size vs human - shows 1.8 m person at honest panel length ratio (10 m)

Read facts panel and click a body part

about 9-11 m (panel baseline 10 m); about 3.5 m at the hip (panel baseline); about 2-4.5 t (panel baseline 3 t); about 152-148 Mya Late Jurassic (Kimmeridgian-Tithonian); Morrison Formation USA and Lourinha Formation Portugal; carnivore; Galton and Jensen 1979 Morrison Formation USA; T. gurneyi from Lourinha Formation Portugal.
