Image Format Converter Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-12

Image Format Converter runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Image Format Converter. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Format options are listed in the dropdown above the file picker.


Step 3 - get the result

Inputs are processed locally and discarded when the page closes.

For the reverse-direction converter, see the related-tools section at the foot of the page.
