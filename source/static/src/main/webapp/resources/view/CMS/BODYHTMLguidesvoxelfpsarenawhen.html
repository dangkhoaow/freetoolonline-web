When to Play Voxel FPS Arena

Voxel FPS Arena loads about ~18 KB in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-13

Best for quick arena runs when you want a ~18 KB voxel FPS instead of a puzzle or stealth horror session.

Action focus

Two compact levels reward movement, aiming, and weapon swaps.

Mouse look

Desktop mouse and keyboard are the primary controls inside the iframe.

Low payload

About ~18 KB loads once behind the Play button.

Enemies and save limits

Enemies come in five types that use line-of-sight AI in Voxel FPS Arena, so stepping into view is what starts a fight; there is no save between visits and no multiplayer, and this build is an original voxel-FPS game, not a licensed Doom or Quake title, built separately from Retro FPS Online. For a closer look at how it compares to other arena shooters, see voxel fps arena vs alternatives.
