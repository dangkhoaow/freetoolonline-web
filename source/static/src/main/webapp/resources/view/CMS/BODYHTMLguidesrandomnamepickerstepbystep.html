Random Name Picker Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Random Name Picker runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Random Name Picker. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Use it for raffles, deciding who goes first, or picking a random winner from a list.


Step 3 - get the result

The spin result uses crypto.getRandomValues for fairness and runs locally - no upload and no account.

Edit the list and spin again any time; entries are not saved between visits.
