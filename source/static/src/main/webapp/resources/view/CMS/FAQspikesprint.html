Frequently Asked Questions

What is Spike Sprint?

A free browser pixel runner. Jump pits and dodge a spiked barrel that surges when you stall.

How do I play?

Press Play, then tap / click / Space to jump. Hold longer for a higher jump (about 320 ms max hold).

What ends a run?

Falling into a pit or touching the spiked barrel tip ends the run. Restart from the game-over screen.

Does it save a high score?

Yes, for this browser tab session only. Closing the tab clears it. Nothing is uploaded.

How big is the download?

About ~25 KB of HTML, CSS, and JS after you press Play.

Is this the same as Pixel Spike Run?

No. Spike Sprint is a barrel-chase runner with variable-height jumps. Pixel Spike Run is a different spike side-scroller on this site.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from shifulegend/spike-sprint (MIT). Procedural canvas art and audio; no CDN assets.
