Khi nao dung Moon Formation Giant Impact 3D Explorer - Thoi diem tot nhat va gioi han

Last reviewed: 2026-07-20

Mo Moon Formation Giant Impact 3D Explorer khi ban can day hoac on lai nguon goc Mat Trang - ma khong can chay bo giai co hoc N-body hay SPH.


Buoi hoc mau nam phut

Buoi hoc mau nam phut bat dau khi ban mo explorer o giai doan 0, nhan Theia va Trai Dat de xem thong tin, roi keo thanh truot qua ca nam giai doan de lop hoc thay dia vat chat sup thanh Mat Trang.


Tach khoa hoc da xac dinh khoi cau hoi con mo

Viec tach khoa hoc da xac dinh khoi cau hoi con mo se de hon khi ban so sanh so lieu thoi diem va goc va cham - da duoc dong thuan - voi so lieu thoi gian tich tu o giai doan 3, van con duoc tranh luan trong tai lieu nghien cuu.


Truong hop Moon Formation Giant Impact 3D Explorer khong phu hop

Trang nay khong bao gom co hoc quy dao N-body chi tiet, dia chat vung mare Mat Trang, hay cac pha Mat Trang hang ngay. Dung Moon Phases 3D Explorer cho chu ky anh sang hang thang, hoac Earth-Moon Barycenter 3D Explorer cho su lac quy dao. Theo huong dan tung buoc.
