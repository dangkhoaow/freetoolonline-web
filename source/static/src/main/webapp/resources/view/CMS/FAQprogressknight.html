Frequently Asked Questions

What is Progress Knight?

A free browser medieval life-sim incremental. Climb careers, learn skills, manage coins and aging, then prestige.

How do I play?

Press Play, then use the Jobs / Skills / Shop / Amulet tabs. Pick a job, learn skills, buy items, and watch age advance.

What is prestige?

When age catches up, open the Amulet tab and touch the eye to rebirth. You lose levels and assets but gain multipliers for the next life.

Can I pause or automate?

Yes. Pause sits in the sidebar. Auto-promote and auto-learn checkboxes appear as you progress.

Does it save progress?

Yes. Saves use the namespaced key ftol:progressknight:gameDataSave in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.

How big is the download?

About ~0.12 MB of HTML, CSS, and JS after you press Play.

Is this open source?

Yes. Adapted from ihtasham42/progress-knight (Unlicense / public domain). This build vendors W3.CSS locally and namespaces localStorage.
