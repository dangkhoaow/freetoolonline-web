Image To Webp Converter Step By Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Image to WebP Converter runs in three steps: open the tool, provide your input, then download the result. This page walks through what each step expects.


Step 1 - open the tool

Go to Image to WebP Converter. Nothing needs to be installed or signed in before you start.


Step 2 - provide your input

Choose a PNG, JPG, GIF, or BMP file from your device. The file is read locally; it is not uploaded anywhere.


Step 3 - get the result

Output sizing and quality are controlled by the option fields above the file picker; defaults match the most common downstream use.

If you need the reverse direction, use the WebP to PNG or WebP to JPG converter instead.
