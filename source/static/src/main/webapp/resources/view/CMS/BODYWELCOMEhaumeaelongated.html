Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-19

Haumea is a Kuiper Belt dwarf planet whose ~3.92-hour spin stretches it into a football-like triaxial body. A 2017 multi-chord stellar occultation (Nature) set axes of at least about 2,322 by 1,704 by 1,138 kilometers and revealed a narrow ring about 70 kilometers wide near 2,287 kilometers radius.

NASA places Haumea near 43 AU with an orbit of about 285 Earth years. This explorer shows a schematic elongated ellipsoid and ring toggle - not a DEM, not a dynamical ring model, and without moons Hi'iaka and Namaka.
