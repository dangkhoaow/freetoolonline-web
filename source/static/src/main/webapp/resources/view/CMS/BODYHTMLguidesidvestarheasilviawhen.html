When to use Vesta Rheasilvia Basin 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want to see how Rheasilvia spans most of Vesta's southern hemisphere and how tall its central peak is.

  • Comparing a ~500 km basin to a ~529 km mean-diameter body
  • Introducing Dawn's south-pole discoveries without a full DEM
  • Linking giant impacts on small worlds to Vestoids and HED meteorites in conversation

This is an educational visualization, not an impact hydrocode.
