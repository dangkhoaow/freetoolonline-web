When to Use Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer - Best Sessions and Limits

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Open the Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer when you need chirp and strain literacy - GW150914's 36+29 Msun binary, peak strain ~1e-21 - and skip it when you only need static curvature grids or gravity-assist flybys.


A five-minute classroom demo

A five-minute classroom demo starts when you open Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer, press Fast, and pause so students see the ripple rings tighten and the chirp frequency climb as the two black holes spiral into the 62 Msun merger.


Chirp literacy in Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer

Chirp literacy in Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer stays readable when you keep the facts panel open for the 35-150 Hz frequency sweep and ~1e-21 peak strain while toggling Slow / Fast.


Sessions Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer does not fit

Sessions Gravitational Waves 3D Explorer does not fit are numerical-relativity waveform solving, real Earth-scale strain geometry, or gravity-assist trajectories.
