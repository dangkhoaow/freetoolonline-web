Click Start to begin timing, Lap to record a split without stopping the clock, Stop to pause, and Reset to clear the display and the lap list.

How it works:

  1. Click Start; the display counts up in MM:SS.hh format.
  2. Click Lap at any point to add the current time to the list below, without stopping the clock.
  3. Click Stop to pause, Start again to resume, or Reset to clear the display and the lap list.
