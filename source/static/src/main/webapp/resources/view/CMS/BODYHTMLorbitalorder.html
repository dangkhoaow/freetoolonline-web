Orbital Order is a free browser Aufbau electron puzzle. Move the cursor to attract blue s-electrons and repel orange p-electrons, then seat them in empty orbitals. Fill six elements from hydrogen to oxygen. About ~32 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a radius-aim shooter instead? Try Orbital Radius Shooter. Prefer a space gravity mini-golf loop? Try Gravity Orbit Golf.

ORBITAL ORDER

Cursor physics: blue electrons follow you, orange electrons flee. Seat them in orbitals without double-hitting filled shells. Keys: M mute, I help, L log, Esc close. Saves use ftol:orbitalorder:*. Adapted from aftongauntlett/js13k-demo (MIT). About ~32 KB.

Works best with a mouse or trackpad. On small phones a desktop hint may appear.
