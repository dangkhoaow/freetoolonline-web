How to Play Particle Clicker - Step by Step

The Particle Clicker page loads a ~2 MB CERN Webfest physics clicker in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, click the detector for data, then research, hire workers, and buy upgrades.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the CDN iframe (~2 MB). The lab UI boots with the detector canvases.

Step 2 - click the detector

Click or tap the detector to generate events and collect data. Data is the main research currency.

Step 3 - research discoveries

Open Research and spend data to level discoveries. New levels raise reputation and unlock more of the physics timeline.

Step 4 - hire and upgrade

Use HR to hire workers for automatic data. Spend funding on upgrades that boost rates. Save from the menu when you want a checkpoint.
