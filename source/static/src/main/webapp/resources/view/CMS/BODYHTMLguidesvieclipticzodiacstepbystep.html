Ecliptic Zodiac 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Ecliptic Zodiac 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and watch the Sun walk the gold ecliptic. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - equator vs ecliptic

Press Equator vs ecliptic to compare the blue celestial equator with the gold path tilted about 23.44 deg.


Step 2 - Ophiuchus

Press Ophiuchus so the panel highlights the thirteenth constellation on the ecliptic belt.


Step 3 - precession literacy

Press Precession, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while the yearly path continues.
