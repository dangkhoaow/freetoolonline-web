Welcome to Star Fuel Battle - a free browser spaceship dogfight with no account or install. Press Play to load about 0.67 MB of canvas art and audio, then click Start game inside the frame. Fly with WASD, fire with Space, and keep fuel above zero by grabbing falling bottles (start at 15, bottles add 15, capped at 30). Hit enemies for five points and planets for ten after two hits; avoid friends because they cost ten score. Press P to pause and M to mute when you need a break. After a run you can enter a name for on-device ranking under ftol:starfuelbattle:gameData - nothing is uploaded. For more free browser games, open the games hub. Related picks: Andromeda Star Shooter and Nova Star Barrage.
