Khi nao viewer trinh duyet Deinonychus phu hop

Chon viewer trinh duyet khi ban muon xoay thu an thit mong liem co kich thuoc nguoi, Size vs human o ti le 3.4 m trung thuc, va bam de doc su that - khong cai gi. Khong gui chuyen tham len server - tot cho laptop lop hoc hoac kiem tra nhanh so lieu va pha vo than thoai kich thuoc Jurassic Park.


Khi nao ung dung AR hoac bao tang tot hon

Ung dung AR dien thoai thang khi can bong tren san phong; viewer nay khong bao gio tuyen bo dat trong phong. Bao tang thang khi di canh xuong Deinonychus lap. Anh tinh thang chi khi can mot khung chia se khong tuong tac.


So sanh Deinonychus nay khong phai la

Day la bang trade-off cho viewer da ship - khong xep hang thuong hieu AR, khong bia tinh nang AR trang khong co, khong bia mot can nang chinh xac ngoai khoang 60-100 kg, khong tuyen bo Deinonychus la Velociraptor theo ten, khong tuyen bo san bay dan da xac nhan, khong bia credit glTF, va khong bia diem.
