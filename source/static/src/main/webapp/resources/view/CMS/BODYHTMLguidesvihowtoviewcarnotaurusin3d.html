Doc panel facts Carnotaurus va bam bo phan co the

Panel facts Carnotaurus liet ke chieu dai khoang 7.5-8 m (uoc tinh khac tu cung bo xuong toi khoang 9 m; Size vs human dung khoang 7.8 m), khoi luong khoang 1.3-2.1 tan, cua so 69-66 Mya Ky Creta Muon (Maastrichtian) o Formacion La Colonia, tinh Chubut, Argentina, va kham pha - Jose Bonaparte dat ten Carnotaurus sastrei nam 1985 tu mot bo xuong duy nhat. Mot cap sung hinh non day nam tren mat - theropod duy nhat tren site nay co feats.horns. Chi truoc con teo hon ca Tyrannosaurus rex, voi ngon tay nho gan nhu khong chuc nang. Holotype giu dau an da that - kham ghep vay nho khong chong lap, khong bang chung long - nhung duoi va phan chan duoi khong bao gio duoc thu thap, nen nhung phan do suy ra tu ho hang abelisaurid gan. Bam ngan - khong keo - vao dau (gom vung sung), canh tay, duoi, hoac chan se hien fact hoa thach ngan; bam cho trong de tro lai to loai. Mau da la phuc dung nghe thuat, khong phai khung xuong hoa thach chinh xac. Mo hinh la theropod procedural co feats.horns va chua re-host glTF Carnotaurus mien phi.
