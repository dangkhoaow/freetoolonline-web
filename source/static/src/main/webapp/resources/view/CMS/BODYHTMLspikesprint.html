Spike Sprint is a free browser pixel runner. Tap or press Space to jump, hold longer for a higher hop, clear pits, and stay ahead of the spiked barrel. About ~25 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a pixel spike side-scroller with different hazards? Try Pixel Spike Run. Prefer one-button platform hops? Try One Tap Platformer.

SPIKE SPRINT

Tap / click / Space to jump. Hold for a higher jump. Clear pits and bushes; the spiked barrel surges when you stall. Session high score stays in this tab. Adapted from shifulegend/spike-sprint (MIT). About ~25 KB.

Works with keyboard, mouse, or touch. Sound toggle sits on the canvas HUD after Play.
