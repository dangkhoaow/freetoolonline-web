Como jogar Progress Knight - passo a passo

The Progress Knight page loads a ~0.12 MB medieval life-sim incremental in an iframe.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, pick a job, learn skills, buy gear, age through a life, then prestige on the Amulet tab.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the iframe (~0.12 MB). Age, day, lifespan, and coins appear in the sidebar.

Step 2 - take a job

Open Jobs and select work that matches your current skill level. Income and expenses update per day.

Step 3 - learn and shop

Open Skills to train. Open Shop to buy items that help income, happiness, or other stats.

Step 4 - prestige

When lifespan ends (or you choose), open Amulet and touch the eye to rebirth with multipliers. Progress autosaves to ftol:progressknight:gameDataSave.
