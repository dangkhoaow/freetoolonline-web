What this PDF to text converter does

This converter extracts the readable text out of a PDF so you can copy it, paste it into another document, or run it through a translator without retyping. Upload your PDF; it is processed on our server and the plain-text result is returned for you to copy or download, then cleared from temporary storage after a short retention window - no account needed. It works best on PDFs that already contain a text layer; scanned, image-only PDFs are run through OCR, where the accuracy depends on the scan resolution. Free.
