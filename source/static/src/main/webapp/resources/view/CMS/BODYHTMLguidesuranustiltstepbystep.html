How to explore Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Reviewed by FreetoolOnline editorial team

Open the Uranus Tilt 3D Explorer, read the ~97.8 deg / ~84 yr / ~25,559 km figures, and scrub the orbit year while watching which pole faces the Sun.

  1. Open /space-3d/uranus-tilt.html and wait for Uranus, the orange spin axis, and the orbital plane.
  2. Read the facts panel: axial tilt ~97.8 deg, orbit ~84 yr, radius ~25,559 km.
  3. Scrub orbit year from 0 toward 21 and 42 to see poles swing relative to the Sun marker.
  4. Press Play orbit, then Pause orbit, to auto-advance one Uranus year.
  5. Toggle Compare Earth 23.44 deg to place the ghost Earth obliquity for contrast.
