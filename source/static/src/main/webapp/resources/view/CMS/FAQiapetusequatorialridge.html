Frequently Asked Questions

How tall is the equatorial ridge?

Peaks rise up to about 20 kilometers above the surrounding plains along a ridge about 1,300 kilometers long.

Why does Iapetus look like a walnut?

The equatorial mountain chain bulges the silhouette at the equator, which Cassini images made unmistakable.

What causes the two-tone colors?

A dark leading hemisphere (Cassini Regio) contrasts with a bright icy trailing side. Thermal and dust-migration ideas are discussed in the literature; this page shows the contrast schematically.

How did the ridge form?

Origin is debated - internal tectonics versus accretion of an ancient ring are leading ideas. This explorer does not pick one.

When was the ridge discovered?

Cassini first clearly imaged it on 31 December 2004; Voyager had hinted at equatorial mountains earlier.

Is the ridge continuous all the way around?

It spans most of the equator in segments of peaks and parallel ridges, not a perfect unbroken wall.

Is this a physics simulation?

No. It is an educational visualization with exaggerated ridge relief, not a formation model.
