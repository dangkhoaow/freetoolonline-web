Speaker & Audio Channel Test Khi Nao Nen Dung

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Mo Speaker & Audio Channel Test khi ban can xac nhan kenh trai va phai - vi du sau khi cam tai nghe, doi loa Bluetooth, hoac kiem tra stereo truoc cuoc goi.


Khi nao phu hop

Dung de kiem tra nhanh L / R / ca hai trong trinh duyet. Bam Play Left, Play Right hoac Play Both; dung tai nghe neu can nghe pan ro. Chinh tan so, roi Stop khi xong.


Khi nao dung cong cu khac

Trang nay khong do am luong tuyet doi (dB) hay hieu chinh monitor studio. De kiem tra mic, dung cong cu microphone tren site. De quet tan so dai, ung dung desktop phu hop hon.


Bao mat

Moi thu chay trong trinh duyet - khong mic, khong upload, khong tai khoan. Am thanh dung StereoPannerNode (Web Audio API) de chi phat kenh da chon.
