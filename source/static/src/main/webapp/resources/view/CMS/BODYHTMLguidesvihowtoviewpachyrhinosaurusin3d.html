Doc bang su that Pachyrhinosaurus va nhap bo phan co the

Bang liet ke chieu dai khoang 6-8 m cho loai chuan P. canadensis (Size vs human dung 8 m; mot so nguon nhu Britannica trich khoang 6 m), khoi luong khoang 3-4 tan (spec hien 4000 kg; Britannica khoang 1.8 t duoc cong bo nhu chenh lech nguon), u xuong det day o mui va tren mat thay sung dai kieu Triceratops, vo co gai mep nho o mot so ca the, mieng chim an thuc vat, cua so Ky Creta muon khoang 73.5 den 71 trieu nam (Campanian-Maastrichtian), va lich su dat ten - Charles M. Sternberg dat ten chi nam 1950 tu holotype NMC 8867 thu thap 1945-1946 o Formacao Horseshoe Canyon, Alberta, Canada. Loai lien quan P. lakustai den tu xuong Pipestone Creek do giao vien Al Lakusta tim nam 1974; khai quat 2024 o do phat hien hop so lon goi "Big Sam" (khoang 138 cm, u mui 43 cm).
