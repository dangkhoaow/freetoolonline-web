How to Use Mars Terrain Explorer

Last reviewed: 2026-07-15

Load the page, wait for the real elevation data to download, then stretch the relief slider and click the globe for a real elevation reading.

  1. Open Mars Terrain Explorer and wait for the placeholder sphere to become the real Mars terrain.
  2. Drag to orbit the globe; keep the facts panel visible.
  3. Press Relief + a few times and watch Olympus Mons rise and Hellas Planitia sink.
  4. Press Real scale to see how flat Mars actually is at true proportion.
  5. Click anywhere on the globe to read the real elevation at that point.
