Khi Nao Choi Globe Siege

Globe Siege la game thu thanh tren mot hanh tinh 3D, chay trong tab trinh duyet, khong cai dat va khong tai khoan. No hop voi mot phien choi chien thuat 10 - 30 phut tren may ban, noi ban len ke hoach dat thap tren qua cau luc giac dang xoay thay vi phan xa that nhanh. Day la khi no la lua chon dung.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Khi ban muon chien thuat, khong phai phan xa

Thu thanh thuong cho su tinh toan: ban doc cac hanh lang do bo, chon noi xay va nang cap vai thap chu chot. Neu ban muon nghi ve vi tri va kinh te thay vi ngam ban nhanh, Globe Siege hop hon mot game ban sung arcade. Space tam dung bat cu khi nao ban can thoi gian de tinh.

Khi ban ngoi may ban co ban phim

Ban xoay qua cau bang cach keo va phong to bang cuon, nhung ban chon thap bang phim so 1 - 9, nen ban phim giup game muot hon nhieu. Tren dien thoai ban co the xoay hanh tinh bang cham, nhung phim tat thap khien laptop hoac may ban la noi tot nhat cho mot van day du.

Khi ban muon van ngan hoac leo dai

De nghi ngoi nhanh, choi mot man chien dich co muc tieu ro rang roi dung. De choi lau hon, che do vo tan cho ban dat do kho va quy mo bay dan roi dua toi so dot cao luu lai thanh ky luc. Tien do chien dich cua ban duy tri giua cac lan ghe tham, nen ban co the quay lai va choi tiep.

Khi rieng tu va offline quan trong

Moi thu chay tren client sau mot lan tai ~0.65 MB: khong dang nhap, khong tai len va khong goi may chu khi choi. Tien do chi nam trong trinh duyet o cac khoa ftol:globesiege:*, nen day la lua chon tot khi ban muon mot game tu chua khong gui du lieu di.

Khi no khong phai lua chon dung

Bo qua neu ban chi co dien thoai va khong co ban phim, neu trinh duyet thieu WebGL, hoac neu ban muon game arcade nhanh - mot game ban man hinh co dinh nhu Asteroid Blaster hop tam trang do hon. Neu muon ban do thu thanh 2D phang thay vi qua cau, thu Medieval Wall Defense.
