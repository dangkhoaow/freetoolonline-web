Khi Nao Choi Globe Siege

freetoolonline.com editorial team

Globe Siege la game thu thanh tren mot hanh tinh 3D, chay trong tab trinh duyet, khong cai dat va khong tai khoan.

No hop voi mot phien choi chien thuat 10 - 30 phut tren may ban, noi ban len ke hoach dat thap tren qua cau luc giac dang xoay thay vi phan xa that nhanh. Day la khi no la lua chon dung.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-17

Khi ban muon chien thuat, khong phai phan xa

Khi ban muon chien thuat, khong phai phan xa, Globe Siege phu hop: tower defense thuong ke hoach - ban doc hanh lang do bo, chon cho xay va nang cap vai thap chinh thay vi ngam nhanh.

Khi ban ngoi may ban co ban phim

Ban xoay qua cau bang cach keo va phong to bang cuon, nhung ban chon thap bang phim so 1 - 9, nen ban phim giup game muot hon nhieu. Tren dien thoai ban co the xoay hanh tinh bang cham, nhung phim tat thap khien laptop hoac may ban la noi tot nhat cho mot van day du.

Khi ban muon van ngan hoac leo dai

Khi ban muon van ngan hoac leo dai, Globe Siege dap ung ca hai: choi mot man chien dich voi muc tieu ro roi dung, hoac dung che do endless de chon do kho va quy mo swarm va day so song cao duoc luu.

Khi rieng tu va offline quan trong

Khi quyen rieng tu va offline quan trong, Globe Siege chay phia client sau tai mot lan ~0.65 MB: khong dang nhap, khong upload, khong goi server khi choi. Tien do chi nam trong trinh duyet duoi khoa localStorage ftol:globesiege:*.

Khi no khong phai lua chon dung

Globe Siege khong phai lua chon dung neu ban chi co dien thoai khong ban phim, neu trinh duyet thieu WebGL, hoac neu ban muon game arcade phan xa nhanh - fixed-shooter nhu Asteroid Blaster phu hop hon.
