Khi Nao Dung Redshift and Doppler Shift 3D Explorer - Phien Phu Hop Nhat va Han Che

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

Cong cu Redshift and Doppler Shift 3D Explorer phu hop cho phan minh hoa tren lop hoc ve bieu do song-Doppler, nhung phut giai tri ngan ve ly do vach quang pho dich chuyen, va de hieu dinh luat Hubble. Day la hinh hoc mang tinh giao duc - khong phai quy trinh quang pho hoc - nen mot so phien can cong cu khac.


Minh hoa nam phut tren lop

Mo Redshift and Doppler Shift 3D Explorer, nhan Tien gan roi Lui xa, va xem vach danh dau di chuyen tren thanh quang pho khi cac xung vong tron nen lai va gian ra.


So lieu trong mot bang

Giu bang thong tin hien thi khi doc z = v/c, buoc song nghi cua hydro-alpha o 656.28 nm, va vi du dinh luat Hubble o 100 Mpc.


Nhung phien khong phu hop

Nhung phien nay khong phu hop voi Redshift and Doppler Shift 3D Explorer: bat cu luc nao ban can cau truc thien ha, co hoc quy dao, hoac mot buoc di qua nac thang khoang cach. Hay dung Milky Way Map 3D Explorer, Kepler Orbits 3D Explorer hoac Parallax Distance 3D Explorer. No cung can WebGL trong trinh duyet.
