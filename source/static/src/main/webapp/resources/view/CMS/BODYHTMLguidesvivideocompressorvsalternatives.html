Khi nao chon Nen Video

Chon Nen Video khi ban muon nen nhanh va rieng tu, ma khong can cai phan mem hay giao file cho mot server ben thu ba.

Chon ung dung may tinh khi ban can kiem soat chi tiet toc do bit, do phan giai, hoac codec, hoac khi ban thuong nen cac file 4K lon va muon xu ly nhanh hon voi nhieu luong. Chon dich vu dua tren tai file len chi khi ban chap nhan file cua ban roi khoi thiet bi.

De xem cac buoc chi tiet, xem Nen Video tung buoc.

Frequently Asked Questions

Tai sao chon cong cu trong trinh duyet thay vi ung dung may tinh?

No khong can cai dat va giu file tren thiet bi cua ban suot thoi gian, dieu nay quan trong khi ban khong the hoac khong muon cai phan mem.

Chat luong nen co giong ung dung may tinh o cai dat tuong tu khong?

No tuong duong voi cung muc tieu chat luong kieu CRF, nhung mot ung dung may tinh thuong co tuy chinh chi tiet hon (toc do bit tuy chon, ma hoa hai lan quet) ma cong cu nay co tinh don gian hoa thanh ba muc.
