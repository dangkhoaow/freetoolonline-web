When to Play Black Hole Square

Black Hole Square fits short logic breaks where you want a tap-to-clear space puzzle without timers - about ~40 KB after Play.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

Move-budget puzzles

Each board is a self-contained clear-the-grid challenge. Skip ahead or reset with swipes and resume from ftol:blackholesquare:storage later.

Touch-first sessions

Tap and swipe on a phone during a commute. Canvas scaling keeps the 320x416 playfield readable.

When to pick another game

Want pipe rotation? Use Pipe Rotate Puzzle. Want sliding tiles? Use Cyber Slide Puzzle.
