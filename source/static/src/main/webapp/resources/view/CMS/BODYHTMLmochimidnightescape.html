MOCHI AND THE MIDNIGHT ESCAPE

Guide Mochi through nine pixel platformer levels. Collect stars, pull levers, ride ziplines, and reach each exit. Progress saves in this browser under ftol:mochimidnightescape. Adapted from Mochi and the Midnight Escape by Jasper Renow-Clarke (MIT, js13k 2025). About ~52 KB.

WASD, arrow keys, ZQSD, or a gamepad. Best with a keyboard; touch has no on-screen buttons.

Press Play to load the game.
Level progress uses namespaced localStorage (ftol:mochimidnightescape).
