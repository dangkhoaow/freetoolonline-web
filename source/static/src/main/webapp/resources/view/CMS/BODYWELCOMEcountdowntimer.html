Countdown Timer Online

Last reviewed: 2026-07-02

This countdown timer runs entirely in your browser and counts down from any duration you set in minutes and seconds. There is nothing to download, install, or sign up for - enter a time, click Start, and the display begins ticking down immediately.

Set the minutes and seconds fields to the duration you want, then use Start, Pause, and Reset to control the count down. The display updates every second in MM:SS format, and a "Time is up!" message appears the moment it reaches zero.

The countdown is driven by your browser's own clock, not a server, so there is no upload and no account. It does not play a sound or send a notification when it finishes - watch the tab for the on-screen message. The tab also has to stay open: closing it or navigating away stops the countdown, since there is no background process keeping it running.

Use it for things like a cooking timer, a workout interval, a presentation time limit, or a timed exam or quiz section - anywhere you need a simple, no-fuss countdown without installing an app.

Prefer to just watch the current time instead of counting down? The companion digital clock and analog clock both run the same live-display idea, one as a large numeric readout and the other as a classic sweeping clock face. Nothing you do on this page is uploaded or stored; there is no account and no tracking.
