Lagrange Points 3D Explorer Step by Step

Last reviewed: 2026-07-14

The Lagrange Points 3D Explorer starts as soon as the page loads: drag to orbit, scroll to zoom, and press Play orbit. Everything renders on your own device - no install, no account.


Step 1 - collinear points

Click L1 and L2 so the panel shows about 1.5 million km from Earth on the Sun-Earth line.


Step 2 - triangular points

Jump L4 and L5 to read the 60 deg leading and trailing equilateral geometry.


Step 3 - craft markers

Press SOHO and JWST, then fullscreen to keep controls visible while the co-rotating frame turns.
