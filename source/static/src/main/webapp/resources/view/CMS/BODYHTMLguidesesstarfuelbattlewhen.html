Cuando jugar Star Fuel Battle online

Star Fuel Battle fits a short fuel-timed dogfight - about ~0.67 MB after Play, no install.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

When you want WASD flight with a fuel clock

Fuel drains every second, so each bottle grab extends the run. Good for a focused keyboard session on desktop.

When you want local ranking

After a run you can save a name to on-device ranking (ftol:starfuelbattle:gameData). Nothing leaves the browser.

When to pick another game

Want a different star-shooter feel without the fuel bottle loop? Use Andromeda Star Shooter. Want chili arcade waves? Use Chili Blast Shooter.
