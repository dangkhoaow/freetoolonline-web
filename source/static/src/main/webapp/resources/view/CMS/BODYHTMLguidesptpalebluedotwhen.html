Quando usar Pale Blue Dot 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want a hands-on feel for just how far away, and how small, the real Pale Blue Dot photo shows Earth to be.

  • Showing the real 40.5 AU distance and the 0.12-pixel size of Earth in the photo
  • Explaining the real scattered-light-ray artifact that Earth happened to fall inside
  • Connecting the image to Carl Sagan's famous quote and 1994 book

This is an educational visualization, not a pixel-accurate reproduction of the real photograph.
