Cong Cu Chuyen Anh Sang WebP Tung Buoc

Last reviewed: 2026-07-11

Cong Cu Chuyen Anh Sang WebP chay qua ba buoc: mo cong cu, cung cap dau vao, roi tai ket qua. Trang nay giai thich tung buoc can gi.


Buoc 1 - mo cong cu

Truy cap Cong Cu Chuyen Anh Sang WebP. Khong can cai dat hay dang nhap truoc khi bat dau.


Buoc 2 - cung cap dau vao

Chon mot tep PNG, JPG, GIF hoac BMP tu thiet bi cua ban. Tep duoc doc tai cho; khong duoc tai len bat ky dau.


Buoc 3 - lay ket qua

Kich thuoc va chat luong dau ra duoc dieu khien boi cac tuy chon phia tren o chon tep; gia tri mac dinh phu hop voi cach dung pho bien nhat.

De chuyen theo huong nguoc lai, dung cong cu chuyen WebP sang PNG hoac WebP sang JPG.
