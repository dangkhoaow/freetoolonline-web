Doc bang su that va nhap phan than

Bang liet ke dai nguoi lon tip khoang 9-12 m (Size vs human dung 10 m), khoi luong trung binh khoang 5.6 tan (ca the lon bat thuong den khoang 15 tan hoac hon), cua so Cretaceo muon ~73-66 Ma o Tay Bac My, mo rong khong rang, va khong mao xuong tren dau - doi lap Parasaurolophus co mao da giao. Lambe dat ten E. regalis 1917 (Alberta); E. annectens mo ta 1892 boi Marsh la Claosaurus annectens (Wyoming). Mau uop noi tieng: AMNH 5060 "Trachodon mummy" (1908) va "Dakota" (1999).
