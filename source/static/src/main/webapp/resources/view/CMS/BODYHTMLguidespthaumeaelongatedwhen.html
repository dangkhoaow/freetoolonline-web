When to use Haumea Elongated Dwarf Planet 3D Explorer

Use this explorer when you want to show why Haumea looks elongated and how its narrow ring sits relative to the body.

  • Introducing Kuiper Belt dwarf planets beyond Pluto
  • Teaching fast spin and triaxial shape
  • Discussing the 2017 stellar-occultation ring discovery

This is an educational visualization, not a dynamical ring solver.
