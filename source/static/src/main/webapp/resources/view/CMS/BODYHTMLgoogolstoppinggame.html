Googol Stopping Game is a free browser decision puzzle based on the optimal-stopping (secretary) problem. Fifty tiles hide huge numbers; click tiles to reveal values, then press I'm done when you think you hold the biggest. About ~8 KB after Play, no install, no account.

Want a canvas table-tennis match instead? Try Classic Pong. Want a particle match puzzle? Try Quantum Shift.

GOOGOL STOPPING GAME

Pick numbered tiles that hide values from 0 up toward a googol. Your last pick is final - press I'm done when you believe you have the biggest. Or tap Computer to watch the 1/e sample strategy play. Adapted from Victor Ribeiro's googol (MIT). About ~8 KB.

Click or tap tiles on desktop or phone. Session-only - no save data in this build.
