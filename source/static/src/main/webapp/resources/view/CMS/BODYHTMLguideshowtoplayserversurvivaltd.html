How to Play Server Survival TD - Step by Step

The Server Survival TD page loads a ~13 MB 3D cloud-infrastructure tower defense in an iframe from the FreeToolOnline game CDN.

Last reviewed: 2026-07-21

Press Play, place services, connect traffic, and keep reputation above zero.

Step 1 - press Play

Press Play to inject the CDN iframe (~13 MB). The game boots inside the frame.

Step 2 - start a mode

Pick survival or campaign from the menu. Sound prefs and locale stay under ftol:serversurvivaltd keys.

Step 3 - place and connect services

Drop CDN, database, firewall, and related nodes. Wire them so requests can reach the right service.

Step 4 - manage load and budget

Watch RPS, upkeep, and reputation. Upgrade or add capacity before a surge knocks services down.
