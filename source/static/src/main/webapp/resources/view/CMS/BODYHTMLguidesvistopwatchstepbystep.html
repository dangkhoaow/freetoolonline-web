Cau tra loi trong 30 giay. Mo cong cu Online Stopwatch va nhan Start; man hinh dem len theo dinh dang MM:SS.hh. Nhan Lap bat ky luc nao de ghi lai mot vong ma khong dung dong ho, nhan Stop de tam dung, va nhan Reset de xoa man hinh va danh sach vong tro lai 00:00.00.

Start, Stop, va tiep tuc mot lan chay da tam dung

Start bat dau dem tu 00:00.00 (hoac tu vi tri man hinh dung lai sau khi Stop). So dem tang lien tuc, chinh xac den phan tram giay, khong can nhan gi them. Stop tam dung dung tai vi tri hien tai - man hinh giu nguyen gia tri do thay vi dat lai ve khong. Nhan Start lai se tiep tuc dem tu gia tri da tam dung, vay nen mot chuoi Stop-roi-Start se cong don tong thoi gian thay vi bat dau mot lan chay moi.

Ghi lai vong ma khong dung dong ho

Lap them thoi gian da troi qua hien tai vao danh sach ben duoi man hinh, theo thu tu ban nhan. Khac voi Stop, Lap khong dung gi ca - dong ho van tiep tuc dem trong khi danh sach vong tang len, dieu nay giup ich khi so sanh thoi gian tung doan trong mot lan chay lien tuc (vi du, moi km cua mot lan chay bo, hoac moi vong cua mot bai tap ngat quang).

Dong ho bam gio so voi dong ho dem nguoc va cac cong cu dong ho

Cong cu nay dem len tu khong, khong co diem ket thuc - no dung de do xem mot viec gi mat bao lau. Cong cu di kem dong ho dem nguoc thi nguoc lai, dem xuong tu mot khoang thoi gian ban dat, va dong ho so cung nhu dong ho kim hien thi gio hien tai thay vi thoi gian da troi qua. Dung dong ho bam gio khi ban quan tam den bao nhieu thoi gian da troi qua; dung dong ho dem nguoc khi ban quan tam den thoi gian con lai; dung cac cong cu dong ho khi ban quan tam den gio trong ngay.

Cac cach dung pho bien

Nguoi dung dung dong ho bam gio tren trinh duyet de do thoi gian mot bai tap, so sanh thoi gian tung doan trong mot lan chay hoac cuoc dua, thuc hien mot buoi nau an hoac hoc, tap duot mot bai thuyet trinh, hoac bat ky hoat dong nao khac ma ban muon co thoi gian troi qua chinh xac ma khong can cai dat ung dung hay tim dong ho bam gio vat ly.

Cong cu nay khong lam gi

No khong dong bo voi may chu thoi gian chuan - toan bo viec dem chay tren dong ho cuc bo cua chinh trinh duyet. No khong tiep tuc chay trong nen: dong tab hoac chuyen sang trang khac se dung viec dem hoan toan, vi khong co tien trinh nen hay service worker giu no hoat dong. No khong phat am thanh hay gui thong bao khi nhan bat ky nut nao, va khong luu thoi gian dang chay hay danh sach vong giua cac lan truy cap.

Quyen rieng tu

Dong ho bam gio chay hoan toan trong tab trinh duyet cua ban. Khong co gi ban do hay ghi lai o day duoc gui di dau, khong can tai khoan, va trang khong lien lac voi server nao trong khi dong ho dang chay.

Cong cu lien quan

  • Online Stopwatch - cong cu chinh.
  • Countdown Timer Online - dem xuong tu mot khoang thoi gian ban dat, thay vi dem len.
  • Digital Clock Online - man hinh HH:MM:SS lon, truc tiep cua gio hien tai, khong phai dong ho bam gio.
  • Analog Clock Online - cung gio truc tiep do tren mat dong ho co kim quay.
  • Trang cong cu tien ich - cac cong cu chuyen doi va tao noi dung hang ngay khac tren trang nay.

Cau hoi thuong gap

Nhan Lap co tam dung dong ho bam gio khong?

Khong. Lap chi ghi lai thoi gian da troi qua hien tai vao danh sach - dong ho van tiep tuc dem de ban co the ghi lai bao nhieu vong can thiet trong mot lan chay.

Neu toi nhan Stop roi nhan Start lai, co dat lai ve khong khong?

Khong. Stop tam dung tai gia tri hien tai; nhan Start lai se tiep tuc dem tu chinh gia tri do, vay nen tong thoi gian tiep tuc cong don thay vi bat dau lai.

Dong ho bam gio co tiep tuc chay khong neu toi chuyen tab hoac dong trinh duyet?

Chuyen tab khong lam dung dong ho - dong ho van chay khi tab nay con mo. Dong tab, hoac dong trinh duyet, se dung dong ho hoan toan, vi khong co tien trinh nen.

Man hinh chinh xac den muc nao?

Man hinh hien thi chinh xac den phan tram giay theo dinh dang MM:SS.hh, dieu khien boi dong ho cua chinh trinh duyet.
