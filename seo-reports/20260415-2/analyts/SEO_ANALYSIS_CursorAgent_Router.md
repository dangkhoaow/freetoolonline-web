### 2.2 Content

- **Intent mix (Semrush):** Predominantly **informational** queries (file conversion, “how to zip,” HEIC, MD5, etc.). Tool pages match that intent; **E-E-A-T** is supported mainly by **consistency, working tools, and transparent “About”** rather than bylined expertise.
- **Headings:** Playwright found **two `<h1>` elements on most tool pages** (56/63). That often comes from **template duplication** (e.g. visible title + secondary heading region). It dilutes semantic clarity for accessibility and SEO.
- **FAQ coverage vs schema:** **43/63** pages had **`FAQPage`** in JSON-LD after render. Pages with FAQ **HTML** that does not match `extractFaqItems` patterns (`<h3>` + `<p>` after an FAQ `<h2>`) will **not** get FAQ schema-gap between content and parser.
- **CTR vs impressions (GSC):** Large **impression growth** with **lower average CTR** usually means **more long-tail / lower-ranking queries** entering the portfolio-normal during expansion, but titles/snippets should be tuned on high-impression URLs.

### 2.3 Site structure

- **Navigation:** Fixed header, **W3.CSS**-style layout, **dark mode** toggle, donation CTAs-**predictable utility UX**.
- **Internal linking:** **Hub pages** (`*-tools.html`) and **`seo-clusters.mjs`** define **cluster → tool** relationships and **“Back to …”** hub links at build time. **“Related tools”** is injected via **`related-tools.js`** after load-**links are not in the initial HTML**, which can **delay or reduce** how consistently Google associates related URLs (depending on rendering and link discovery).
- **Aliases:** `ALIAS_ROUTES` in `site-data.mjs` consolidates duplicate tool URLs-good for **duplicate content control** if redirects are consistently used.

### 2.4 Content clustering strategy

**Declared clusters (code):** Eight groups in `seo-clusters.mjs`-**zip**, **image-editing**, **image-conversion**, **pdf**, **developer**, **video**, **device-test**, **utility**-each with a **hub route** and **member tool routes** (`resolveHubBacklink`).

**Sitemap alignment:** Export writes **hub** URLs to **`sitemap-hubs.xml`**, **tools** to **`sitemap-tools.xml`**, and **informational** routes (`INFO_ROUTES`) to **`sitemap-pages.xml`**-this **mirrors** the cluster model for crawlers.

**Traffic reality (GSC screenshot):** Organic clicks are **concentrated** in **ZIP** and **password-removal** tools, with **MD5**, **LCD/camera** tests, and **HEIC** as secondary drivers-so **commercial “clusters” in code** do not always match **demand clusters** in search; prioritize **internal linking and content depth** on hubs that sit **below** their strongest child URLs.

## 4. Key Issues (Root Causes)

1. **Heading hierarchy:** **Multiple `<h1>`** on most tool pages (Playwright: **56/63**)-likely **template/layout**, not editorial choice; weakens **semantic outline** and can confuse parsers.
2. **JS-dependent related links:** **Related tools** and tag links **populate after script execution**-**internal PageRank** and **discovery** may underperform versus **static HTML** links in the main document.
3. **4xx crawl noise (~6%):** Wastes crawl budget and may correlate with **outbound** or **legacy** URLs; needs **URL list** from GSC **Page indexing** / server logs.
4. **CTR compression on expanded queries:** **Impressions up**, **CTR down**-**snippet competitiveness** on new queries, not necessarily a quality demotion.
5. **Semrush vs GSC divergence:** **Do not** conclude “traffic collapsed” from Semrush alone while **GSC clicks grow**-reconcile with **URL inspection**, **brand vs non-brand**, and **keyword scope**.
6. **Monetization vs traffic (AdSense + GA4):** **Volume up**, **CPC/RPM down**; **key events down** in GA4-investigate **audience geography**, **ad layout**, and **event configuration**, not only SEO.
